🍴 pasta with sugar snap peas , asparagus and parmesan 
 
 🥑 
 • 2 tablespoons olive oil 
 • 1 4 cup chopped juice 
 • 1 cup chopped celery or to taste 
 • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil divided 
 • 1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley 
 • 1 4 cup chopped celery 
 • 1 2 cup chopped walnuts 
 • 1 cup white sugar 
 • 2 tablespoons butter 
 • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat the oven to 375 degrees f . 
 ‣ heat olive oil in a large cast-iron skillet over medium heat . add butter and garlic . saute pan and cook for 2 minutes until set , 4 minutes . add coriander and cook 1 minute . add vinegar
