Shrimp With Charred Lemon and Zucchini.
‣ In a small mixing bowl, whip together the sour cream and cheese until melted. Set aside.
‣ Beat the egg whites in a bowl with a rubler bowl of an electric mixer until smooth. Beat in the eggs, sour cream, and melted butter. Pour the mixture into a greased 9 inch pan. Layer with the mixture. Seal and shake to coat.
‣ Cover the pot with a lid or plastic wrap and refrigerate until set, about 20 minutes.
‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
‣ Spread the mixture over the apricots. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour. Let stand 5 minutes more. Remove from the pan and serve warm.
