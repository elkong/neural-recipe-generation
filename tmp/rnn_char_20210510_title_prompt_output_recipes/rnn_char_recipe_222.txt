Teriyaki Sauce:
‣ Place eggs in a saucepan over medium-high heat and bring to a boil. Add curry paste, corn and bread cubes; stir until melted. Remove from heat.
‣ Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook until tender but still firm to the bite, about 15 minutes. Drain well in a colander.
‣ Meanwhile, beat egg in a separate bowl; pour in eggs. Stir until dissolved. Pour over chicken and pour marinade over meat and use Leave Bread on top of cracker.
‣ Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
‣ Bake bass in preheated oven for 25 minutes, until golden brown on top.
