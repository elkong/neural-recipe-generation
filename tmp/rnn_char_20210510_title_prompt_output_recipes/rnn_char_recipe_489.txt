‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Brush the bread with the oil mixture. Place the bread in prepared dish, and flatten lightly with bread crumbs. Cut dough into forks and place about 2 inches apart on a baking sheet.
‣ Place bread in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Drizzle with beaten eggs. Spoon any pecans over the top. Pour half of the cheese sauce over the top.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until browned and crisp. Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before serving
