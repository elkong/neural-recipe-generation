Pecan Boniato Crusted Fish with Jalapeno Cream Sauce)
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Bake covered until cooked through and toasted; microwave on HIGH for 6 minutes.
‣ Remove skillet from heat and pour eventop of butter into pastry shell. Cover, and chill 30 minutes.
‣ Place tamales in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Add curry paste, and stir until thickened. Serve them with cracker crumbs.
