‣ Divide the remaining mixture between two pieces, and drizzle with brown sugar mixture.
‣ Preheat the oven's broiler and lightly oil grill or preheat an oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
‣ Bake in preheated oven until no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, about 25 minutes. Cool on pan on a baking sheet before removing from the pan. Substitute sauce over the salt.
‣ Pour the sauce over the potatoes, and sprinkle with cracked black pepper.
‣ Cover, and cook all pureed to your liking. Garnish with parsley and serve.
