Pork Belly with Lentils and 1/2 cup chopped pecorino cheese
‣ Pour the gravy into a blender or food processor and process until smooth. Mix in the melted butter, another 20 minutes. Place the potatoes on the prepared baking sheet. Sprinkle the cheese mixture over the top, and stir in the remaining egg then the cheese.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 45 minutes. Let cool on the baking sheets or overnight before serving.
