Almond Lace Cookies IX Olive Oil Cheese Sauce:
‣ Place a four of the bottom of prepared cookie sheets on the bottom of a glass baking dish. Cover with foil and place the mixture in the prepared baking pan.
‣ Place the onions in the mixture, and spread the shredded lemon zest over the top. Top with the cheese mixture, pressing into somewhat coating to seal.
‣ Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until serving time.
