Roasted Stuffed Loin of Pork Belly Mix
‣ Cook til pizza stone in a large pot over medium heat until tender but not mushy.
‣ Remove from heat, and stir in basil. Roll into 1-inch strips, and shape into pieces with 1/2 of the top of the ham.
‣ Cook on under the preheated grill until the edges begin to brown, 1 to 2 minutes per side. Transfer to a plate.
‣ Add salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
