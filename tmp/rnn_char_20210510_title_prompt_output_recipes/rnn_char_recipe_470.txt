PB Banana Oat Smoothie
‣ In a medium bowl, whisk together the remaining 1/2 cup sugar, cornstarch, soy sauce, and 1/4 cup oil. Season with salt and pepper.
‣ Cover and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 40 minutes. Remove from oven and sprinkle with pecans.
‣ In a small bowl combine mustard and pecans. Spread into a small bowl and sprinkle with egg whites. Chill until serving.
