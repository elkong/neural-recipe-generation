Rayna's Peanut Butter Jammies IIII
‣ Pour chili sauce into a shallow saucepan. Add cheese and milk, and stir to combine. Bring to a boil, and cook for 2 minutes.
‣ Pour the chili paste into the pan with the pepper and garlic cloves. Cover and bake refrigerator for at least 8 hours.
‣ Meanwhile, heat the grapeseed oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the chopped onions, celery, and celery. Stir in the cumin, coriander, garam masala and bay leaf. Stir in the strawberries and the vegetable mixture. Spread over the pasta and top with the lime zucchini. Sprinkle the remaining peanut butter. Cover and let sit for 8 to 9 hours.
‣ Serve with lemon slices sourdough baguette slices.
