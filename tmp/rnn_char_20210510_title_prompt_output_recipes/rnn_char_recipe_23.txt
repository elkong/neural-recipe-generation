Huevos Rancheros with Black Bean Puree
‣ Beat egg yolk and warm milk together in a bowl using a wooden spoon, stirring, until smooth.
‣ Pour dressing over casserole dish, and sprinkle with basil.
‣ In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine the peanut butter, sour cream, and half-and-half cream. Blend until smooth. Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally. Continue to simmer for 25 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the pineapple juice concentrate.
