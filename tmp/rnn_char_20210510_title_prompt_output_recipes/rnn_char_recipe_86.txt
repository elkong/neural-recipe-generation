Cranberry Punch Pizzazza Nice Soup III

‣ In a medium bowl, mix together flour, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon and allspice in a small bowl until well blended.
‣ Chop wheel in saucepan and ladle batter into the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, until topping is lightly browned. Check for doneness by cutting the rectangles whole.
