Gazpacho of Velvea® ORTO™ SAUCE: In blender or food processor, stir together the milk, olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, sugar, salt, pepper and nutmeg. Pour into a resealable plastic bag.
‣ Chill in the refrigerator at least 1 hour.
‣ Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
‣ Remove prepared pan or coat round, place on greased cookie sheet and bake until golden, about 8 minutes. Remove from oven, and sprinkle with chopped pecans.
‣ Stir cornstarch into cream to serve.
