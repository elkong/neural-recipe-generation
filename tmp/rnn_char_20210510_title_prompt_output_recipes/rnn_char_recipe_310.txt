Green Beer Sauce III
‣ Combine ranch dressing mixture, red onion, and remaining peanut butter into a large resealable plastic bag. Seal and shake depending on some speed for later.
‣ Pour dried cranberries into a saucepan, and stir in cornstarch and brown sugar. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce heat and simmer for 3 minutes or until heated through.
‣ Pour melted chopped dates into a serving bowl, and sprinkle with chopped parsley.
