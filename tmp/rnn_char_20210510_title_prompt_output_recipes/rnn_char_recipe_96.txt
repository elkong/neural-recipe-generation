Spiced Pound Cake with Orange Rum Sauce.
‣ Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
‣ Place potatoes in a medium baking dish.
‣ In a separate bowl, mix the shredded zucchini, crumbs, and the almonds. Pour into a 9x13-inch pan.
‣ Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven for 35 to 45 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown.
