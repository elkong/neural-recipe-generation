Noodle Kugel (Dairy) Shortbread Mango Chutney and ham hocks.
‣ Prepare the noodles on a baking sheet. Drizzle coconut on the cut side of a meat mallet with a kitchen towel. Cover with top layer of parchment paper.
‣ Bake in preheated oven until lightly browned, 10 to 15 minutes. Remove from oven and set aside to cool before serving.
