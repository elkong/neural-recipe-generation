Lobster & Potato Salad dressing
‣ In a food processor, combine eggs, 1/2 cup potato pearl onion, and cream cheese, vegetable broth, salt and pepper; mix well.
‣ In a mixing bowl, beat corn syrup, vegetable oil, onion soup mix with milk, and refrigerate overnight.
‣ To serve, remove coating liquid that from drying out with a sharp knife.
