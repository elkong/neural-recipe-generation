Grilled Eggplant Roulade with Balsamic Glazeta sauce.
‣ To make the filling: Cook and stir peppers until crisp-tender.
‣ Meanwhile, prepare a grill for high heat.
‣ In a small bowl, combine masa harina and feta cheese. Stir in butter, stirring constantly, for a few minutes. Gradually stir in cornflakes. Spread over pork.
‣ Cover, or large muffin pans in prepared baking pan; arrange 4 slices of provolone cheese on top of the mashed potatoes. Place on a baking sheet before serving.
