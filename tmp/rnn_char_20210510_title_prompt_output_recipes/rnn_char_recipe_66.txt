Beet and Red Onion Potato Latkes with Carrot Puree and Horseradish and Caraway Creme Fraiche:
‣ Mix the sour cream and cornstarch in a small bowl until well blended. Set aside.
‣ To make the Gruyere Cheese: Mix the ground beef and reserved oil in a bowl.
‣ Heat 1/4 inch of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Season the small bits with salt and pepper. Sprinkle the cheese over the top of the layers.
‣ Cook the patties until the cheese is melted and heated through, about 3 minutes per side. Serve the mushroom caps with the sauce.
