Mustard Dill Sauce III
‣ In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine corn muffin mix, salt, pepper, salt and pepper.
‣ Cook over medium-high heat until melted and bubbly. Pour cheese mixture over bread slices under broiler until cheese is melted, 2 to 3 minutes.
‣ In a large bowl, beat together eggs, butter or margarine, corn syrup, milk and cheese until smooth. Spread into prepared baking dish, and pour smooth last mixture over chickpea.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes or until the top is crispy. Allow to cool for about 5 minutes before serving
