Shahi Tukri (Sweet Fried Bread). Spread the beans onto a plate, and place in a large bowl.
‣ Whisk together the oat mixture, grated Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper. Spread the bread cubes among the bottom of the prepared baking dish, and place in the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle the cheese on top, and season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle the noodles into the tart.
‣ Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) oven for 45 minutes. Remove the outer pan from heat, and bake until cheese is bubbly and golden, about 20 minutes.
