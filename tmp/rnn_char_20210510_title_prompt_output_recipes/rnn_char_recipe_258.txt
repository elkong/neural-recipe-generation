Chocolate Chip Scones III

‣ In a large microwave-safe bowl, mix the flour, cardamom, baking soda, salt, and nutmeg. Add the onion, and mix well.
‣ Stir in the peaches to form a smooth paste. Transfer to the prepared dish. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
