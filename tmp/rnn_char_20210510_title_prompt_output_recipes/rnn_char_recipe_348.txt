Brie's Banana and Honey Dog Treats:
‣ Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
‣ Pour 1/2 of the green pepper over the first moistened masa mixture. Top with equal amount of butter.
‣ Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake in preheated oven until the cheese has melted and the cheese is bubbly and the pasta is cooked through, about 30 minutes. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with paprika. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.
