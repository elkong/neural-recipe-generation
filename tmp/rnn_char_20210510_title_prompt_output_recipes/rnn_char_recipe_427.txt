‣ Pour the chilled batter into ramekins, in a cloth under 1/2 inch wide strips, and line with plastic wrap and lightly grease an 8 inch square pan.
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease 12 muffin cups.
‣ Melt shortening in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in cheeses, and stir until melted and smooth. Add mixture to pan and continue cooking until bubbly. Stir in fresh basil, and add spinach mixture, stirring well.
‣ Return butter to saucepan or wok to cook until caramel is ship is frothy. Layer will clump the stuffing mixture and divide the mixture between each one. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown. Check it gets but with a spatula of white coconut take and serve warm.
