Mega Egga Macaroni Salad
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Melt butter or margarine in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in flour and cook over medium heat, stirring until the butter melts and the vegetables are tender. Stir in the cornbread mixture. Pour in the carrots, beef and cooked potatoes. Cook until vegetables are tender, 5 to 7 minutes.
‣ While the topping, place shredded cheese on a plate. Spread topping over the onions. Sprinkle cheese over the sliced pistachios and sprinkle with chopped pecans. Top with a sprinkle of dried parsley.
