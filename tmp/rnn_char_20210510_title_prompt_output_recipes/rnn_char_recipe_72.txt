Chocolate-Almond Pie Cracker Crunchies Tot layer:
‣ In a medium mixing bowl, beat together shortening, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff. Mix in blue cheese, cream of celery soup, whipped cream and add more melted chocolate in a small bowl. Add sour cream, lemon juice and chili powder and mix until smooth. Spread mixture into a serving dish, turning to compact pressate asparagus.
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together bread crumbs, pecans, and turkey mixture. Pour over casserole dish.
‣ Bake in preheated oven until fish is just set, about 30 minutes to 1 hour.
