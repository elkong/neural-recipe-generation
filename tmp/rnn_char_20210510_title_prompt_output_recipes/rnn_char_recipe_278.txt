Southern Style Pig Tails: Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (210 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Mix together the mayonnaise, green onions, and bread crumbs together in a bowl until well blended. Pour into prepared baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese mixture and sprinkle with paprika.
‣ Bake in preheated oven until the cheese melts, about 90 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes before removing from the mochi out of the pan.
