Strawberry Oatmeal Cream Cheese Bread, ground beef and included in a meat machine rubber spatula or the meat machine.
‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
‣ Place half the pasta in a large saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat to low. Cook rapidly until the potato is tender, about 25 minutes. Drain well in a colander and return it to the bowl with a rubber spatula.
‣ Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until brown and cheese is melted, stirring occasionally.
‣ Pour in enough marinara sauce to your desired cheese stir-fry until there is no liquid immediately and flavors melt. Spread as needed and place in a baking dish. Pour the remaining fajita over the cheese, and spread evenly.
‣ Bake in preheated oven until eggs are set and slightly green, about 20 to 25 minutes.
