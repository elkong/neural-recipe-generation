Smoky Chicken and Cider Mix with 1/2 cup beer (I like the bottom of a can fat to work with enough for a salad). Drain marinade. Season with salt and pepper. Mix in cheeses and mix well.
‣ Place a triangle on a baking sheet, and toast until lightly browned on top, about 5 minutes.
‣ Carefully spread each lightly with cornstarch mixture. Place foil onto the work surface and fill with some ladyfingers. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
‣ Chill the applesauce covered in refrigerator.
‣ Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
‣ Grill patties in the oven for 3 to 4 minutes per side for medium rare. Discard discard grease.
‣ Pour dried mushrooms and salt into a saucepan and heat through. Serve hot or cold.
