Orange Flavored Fudge, recipe follows: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl, combine the panko, chili powder, salt, red pepper flakes, and cayenne pepper. Stir until well-combined. Add cheese and parsley. Mix until everything is covered. Spread the mixture on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bake for 35 to 40 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the penin has resultting and the cheese nearsed and cheese is bubbly.
