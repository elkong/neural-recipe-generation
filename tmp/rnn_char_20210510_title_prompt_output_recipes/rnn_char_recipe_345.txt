Garlic Mayonnaise Sauce:
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Whisk eggs in a bowl until smooth. Stir in flour and cayenne pepper. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 1 hour.
‣ Preheat oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C). Lightly spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
‣ Spread 1/4 of the mixture evenly in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bake for 40 minutes in the preheated oven, until lightly browned on top.
