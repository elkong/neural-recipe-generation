Buttered-Braised Cabbage Rolls or chopped vegetables and cheese slices in a large bowl of salt. Mix in crackers and egg mixture; pour into a baking dish.
‣ Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 5 minutes.
‣ To Make the topping: In a medium bowl, whisk together the chopped pecans, pineapple, creme fraiche, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
‣ In a small saucepan, melt the chocolate and butter. Stir in the mashed ricotta cheese, then the flour and black pepper. Add in the macaroni, cook 15 to 30 minutes, until the mixture is hot and bubbly and it melts.
‣ Transfer to a medium bowl and add the shredded cheese. Toss to coat. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown. Remove from oven and pour over the casserole dish. Pour apple mixture over the top. Pour in hot water.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
