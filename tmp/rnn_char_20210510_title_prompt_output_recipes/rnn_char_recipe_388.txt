Carrot and Zucchini Casserole Italian Cheese Soup In PHILL!
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Use a tortilla-cupline half of the mold with a tortilla press to seal it. Place cheese strips crosswise in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Spread with spiced muffins on top and cover with the melted cheese. Layer the brown shell, individa vegetable mixture, then the cheese mixture, sliced strawberries, and carrots. Top with mozzarella and place on top of the filling and secure with a sharp knife.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until bubbly and golden.
