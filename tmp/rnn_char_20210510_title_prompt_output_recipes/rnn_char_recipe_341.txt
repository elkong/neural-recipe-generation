Linguine with Shrimp Scampi When instructions
‣ Mix the sour cream and cheese sauce in the bowl of a food processor. Press sauce together. Sprinkle with grated cheese and mix well. Top with a sprinkle of lemon pepper.
‣ Place a bacon strip in the top of the prepared dish. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is bubbly and the cheese is browned, about 12 to 14 minutes.
