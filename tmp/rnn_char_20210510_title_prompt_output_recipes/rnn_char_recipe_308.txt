Corn Bread for Stuffing:
‣ Set a large bowl of ice water, let chill a few minutes. Set aside.
‣ Bring a pot of salted water to a rolling boil. Add meat strips and stir. Heat through until completely dissolved. Remove from heat and let stand for 10 minutes, then transfer to a medium bowl. Drain again and add to bowl. Cover with plastic and place partially back into the prepared dish.
‣ Prepare the gluten in the prepared baking dish.
‣ Layer about 6 tablespoons of batter on the griddle. Cook on High 3 to 5 minutes, until the top of the cob. Invert onto a platter, and garnish with parsley.
