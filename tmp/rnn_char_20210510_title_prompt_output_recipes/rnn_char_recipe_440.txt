Chocolate Cherry Cake III

‣ Cook bacon in a large skillet over medium heat until lightly toasted. Drain grease. Mix in the remaining butter and milk. Stir brown and butter into the sauce. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish, and set the batter into the prepared casserole dish and bake in the preheated oven until the casserole is set in the center, about 30 minutes. Top with shredded cheese.
‣ Recommended Milk in a separate mold or small wedge of butter if desired.
