‣ Place the potatoes on a greased baking rack and dip into the pesto shell. Cover and bake in the preheated oven 20 minutes.

‣ Pour milk into a saucepan over medium heat; season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat and simmer until the sauce has thickened, about 20 minutes. Stir in the lemon juice. Spread the peach mixture evenly over the top of the potato slices.
