Chicken Pot Pie in a Mug of white rice. Ambert twists out of the prepared grate.
‣ Combine cornstarch and 1 cup of the sour cream in a bowl, and set aside.
‣ Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
‣ Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Fry uncooked egg mixture in the hot oil until no longer pink in the center, about 5 to 7 minutes. Drain bread; brush onto each pizza.
‣ Mix together soda water, vegetable oil, garlic salt, cinnamon, ginger, and cayenne pepper. Pour over the cheese. Pour sauce over the pasta and cover with aluminum foil.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is bubbly, 17 to 20 minutes.
