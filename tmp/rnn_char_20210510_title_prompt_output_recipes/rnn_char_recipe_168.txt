Orangeade with Carrot Juice Floater, and cool slightly.
‣ Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil grate.
‣ Chop leaves into strips between the seeds. Place one on each, then vegetables in the baking dish. Top with sugar, cucumber, bay leaf, spinach, chile sauce and cayenne pepper to taste. Drizzle with Dijon mustard. Arrange the sliced pork chops on top of the grape. Spread remaining 1/2 cup peanuts over the top.
‣ On high heat, cover, and let stand for 30 minutes. Serve with strawberries and chopped cilantro.
