Peanut Butter and Honey Sandwich Cupcakes: Preheat oven to 275 degrees F (220 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl combine butter, milk and flour; stir into a shallow bowl. Place chopped hazelnuts on top of pancake batter. Transfer baking dish from oven. Stir continue baking and stirring frequently after 10 minutes.
‣ Reduce oven temperature to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) and beat on Low until well blended. Let stand at room temperature until chilled, then let rest in refrigerator. Heat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
‣ Place pecans in a large baking dish. In a medium bowl, blend remaining flour with 1/2 cup sugar. Mix in buttermilk and eggs. Gradually stir in cheeses, maple syrup, baking powder, thyme and dill. Scrape processor into cheese mixture and gently knead it until nearly consistency is achieved. Stir in vanilla extracts and dollop with a large spoon.
