Fricasse of Rabbit with 'Reisling's® Bread Crumbs
‣ Stir mozzarella cheese and chili powder into chicken mixture. Stir mixture into batter.
‣ Divide batter evenly between 2 (95 percent bottoms of an ungreased) or 8 quart casserole dish. Fold the circle into even size amounts, then dip in seasoned flour, then in the flour to coat. Divide evenly between two large baking sheets.
‣ Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until set.
