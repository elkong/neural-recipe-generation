Grape-Nuts Ice Cream Sauce: Place cereal in a food processor and pulse to coat. Transfer to a large bowl.
‣ Pour the chocolate mixture into pastry shells. Let sit for 20 minutes.
‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
‣ Deflate the muffins out onto a large baking sheet and place the sheets in pan to a flattened baking dish. Cover and let rise until doubled in size.
‣ Preheat oven to 375 F. Pour 2 teaspoons butter over the mixture on a large baking sheet. Bake for 20 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool on wire racks.
‣ Remove prepared pastry shrimp, remove and discard remaining layers. (They will fill the strainer). Be careful not to over be lightly press mixture is too frying. Store leftovers in the refrigerator until served.
