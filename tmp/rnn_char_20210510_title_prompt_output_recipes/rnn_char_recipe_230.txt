‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
‣ Cut over grapes and shred in the mixture.
‣ Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Cook chopped onion, shiitake mushrooms, and drained catfish and onion in the skillet with a gas flame, or the prepared grill until the center (open the oven) thickly and tender, grill the meat.
