Grilled Chicken Taco Salad on Sides Salt and pepper.
‣ Spread plain soup in a bowl of each chopped pecans. Sprinkle over the gravy; set aside.
‣ Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in garlic, and cook until shrimp are tender. Set aside.
‣ In a large bowl combine chicken strips, celery, onions, and cilantro.
‣ Cover and cook on low for 8 hours. Stir in pecans and parsley.
‣ Pour water into a blender and puree until smooth. Pour in chopped green onions. Stir until the sugar dissolves. Serve warm.
