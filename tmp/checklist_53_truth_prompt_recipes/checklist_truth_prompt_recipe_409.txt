🍴 oven roasted potato ' fries ' 
 
 
 
 🥑 
 • 
 
 • 4 large russet potatoes , scrubbed , but not peeled 
 
 • 3 to 4 tablespoons olive oil 
 
 • pinch sea salt 
 
 • freshly ground black pepper 
 
 • dash smoked paprika 
 
 • dash steak spice seasoning 
 
 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 400 degrees f . set aside . 
 ‣ heat oil in a large skillet until lightly turn of a potato and saute over medium heat . cook until golden brown , 6 to 7 minutes
