Creamed Onions with Wine<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1/2 pound baby back pork chops• 1/4 cup butter, melted• 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice• salt and pepper to taste<INS>‣ Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the onion and pork chops; cook until browned on both sides, then turn and brown on the other side. Remove the chops from the skillet.‣ Stir the melted butter and lemon juice into the skillet, and season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring constantly, until the pork chops are no longer pink in the center, about 5 minutes.
