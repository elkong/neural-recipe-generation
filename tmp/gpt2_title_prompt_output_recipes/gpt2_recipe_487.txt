Fig and Goat Cheese Pizza<ING>• 2 cups shredded, cooked spinach• 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded sharp Cheddar cheese• 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese• 1 cup chopped fresh basil• 1 tablespoon dried oregano• salt and pepper to taste• 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen pizza crusts, thawed• 1 (9 inch) prepared pizza crust<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Place the spinach and spinach thawed in a medium bowl, and sprinkle the spinach with the shredded Cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese, basil, oregano, salt and pepper.‣ In a separate small bowl, mix the pizza crust with the spinach mixture. Pour into the prepared baking dish, and cover with the pizza crust.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until the crust is lightly browned.
