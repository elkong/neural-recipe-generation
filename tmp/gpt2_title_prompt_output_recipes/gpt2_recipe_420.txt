Very Berry Cheesecake<ING>• 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract• 2 cups white sugar• 3 eggs• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon baking powder• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda• 1 cup white sugar• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 cup buttermilk• 2 tablespoons lemon juice• 1 teaspoon lemon zest• 2 tablespoons lemon juice• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour• 1/4 cup butter, melted<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 8 inch round pans.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the cream cheese, 1/2 cup sugar, and vanilla until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the flour, baking powder, baking soda, white sugar, and cinnamon. Combine the buttermilk, lemon juice, and lemon zest in a large bowl, and stir into the creamed mixture. Stir in the flour mixture alternately with the buttermilk mixture, mixing just until incorporated. Pour batter into prepared pans.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.‣ To make the glaze: In a small bowl, combine 2 tablespoons lemon juice and 2 tablespoons lemon zest. Drizzle over cooled cake while still warm.
