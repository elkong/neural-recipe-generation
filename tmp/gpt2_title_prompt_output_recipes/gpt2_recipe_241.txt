Nanny's Goulash<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound ground beef• 1/2 pound ground pork• 1/2 pound red potatoes, diced• 1 pound ground turkey• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon dried thyme• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce• 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder• 1/2 teaspoon onion powder• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley• 1/2 cup red wine<INS>‣ Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute onion and ground beef until beef is browned. Stir in potatoes, turkey, salt, thyme, pepper, Worcestershire sauce, red pepper flakes, garlic powder, onion powder, nutmeg, parsley, wine, and beef mixture. Cover and simmer until heated through, about 5 minutes.
