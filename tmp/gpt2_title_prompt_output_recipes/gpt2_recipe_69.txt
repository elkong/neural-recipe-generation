Smothered Tilapia<ING>• 2 (6 ounce) fillets tilapia• 1 tablespoon vegetable oil• 1 tablespoon honey• 1 tablespoon lemon juice• salt and pepper to taste<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Brush fillets with oil and honey, and season with lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.‣ Arrange fillets in a single layer in a baking dish. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
