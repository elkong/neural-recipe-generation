Swedish Sticky Chocolate Cake (Kladdkaka)<ING>• 2 cups cake flour• 1 cup white sugar• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 3/4 cup butter• 3/4 cup packed brown sugar• 3/4 cup white sugar• 1 egg• 1/3 cup white sugar• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 3 cups confectioners' sugar<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 8 inch round pan.‣ Sift the flour, 1 cup white sugar, salt, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and butter together. Stir the brown sugar, 3/4 cup white sugar, egg, white sugar and vanilla into the flour mixture until just moistened. Beat in the flour mixture until just blended. Pour batter into prepared pan.‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 50 to 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Allow cake to cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
