Pressure Cooker Chicken with Duck Sauce<ING>• 2 cups chicken broth• 1 cup water• 1 (2 pound) whole duck, neck and giblets removed• 1/4 cup barbeque sauce<INS>‣ In a pressure cooker, combine the chicken broth, water, and duck. Cover, and cook on Low setting for 6 hours.‣ Remove duck from pressure cooker, and turn off heat. Cover, and allow to steam dry for about 10 minutes. Stir in barbeque sauce, and serve.
