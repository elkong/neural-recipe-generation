Simple and Amazing Peanut Butter-Chocolate Fudge<ING>• 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips• 1/2 cup peanut butter• 1/4 cup milk• 1 cup milk chocolate chips• 1/4 cup peanut butter• 1/4 cup butter• 1/4 cup packed brown sugar• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon<INS>‣ Melt the chocolate and peanut butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in the milk chocolate chips and peanut butter; cook and stir until the mixture is smooth. Remove from heat.‣ Stir in the brown sugar and vanilla extract. Stir in the salt and cinnamon. Spread the mixture into the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan. Chill for at least 4 hours before serving.
