Holly's Smoked Salmon Pasta Salad<ING>• 2 (6 ounce) salmon fillets• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/4 cup mayonnaise• 1/4 cup prepared Dijon-style mustard• 1 cup chopped celery• 1 cup mayonnaise• 1/2 cup prepared mustard• 2 tablespoons butter, melted<INS>‣ Cook salmon in a large pot of boiling salted water until easily flaked with a fork. Rinse under cold water and drain.‣ Place spinach in a large bowl, and toss with mayonnaise, Dijon-style mustard, celery, mayonnaise, and butter. Stir in salmon, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
