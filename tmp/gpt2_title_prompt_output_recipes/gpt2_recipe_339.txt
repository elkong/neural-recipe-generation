Brussels Sprouts with Bacon Dressing<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon onion powder• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 pounds Brussels sprouts, trimmed and cut into 1-inch cubes• 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar• 1/4 cup lemon juice• 2 tablespoons white sugar• 2 teaspoons salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 teaspoon dried oregano• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley<INS>‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir garlic and onion powder in the hot oil until the garlic has softened, about 1 minute. Stir Brussels sprouts into the skillet; cook and stir until the sprouts have wilted and started to brown, about 2 minutes.‣ Whisk balsamic vinegar, lemon juice, sugar, salt, black pepper, oregano, and parsley into the Brussels sprouts mixture; bring to a simmer, stirring constantly, until the mixture has thickened slightly, about 5 minutes. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the sprouts; cook and stir until the cheese has melted, about 3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.‣ Transfer the Brussels sprouts to a serving bowl and sprinkle with chopped fresh parsley; toss with the cheese sauce and serve immediately.
