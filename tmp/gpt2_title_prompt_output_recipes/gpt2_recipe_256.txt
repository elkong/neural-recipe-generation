Hawaiian Ham and Cheese Sliders<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/4 cup butter, melted• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/4 cup chopped green onion• 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream• 1/4 cup sour cream• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the spinach, butter, Cheddar cheese, green onion, cream and sour cream. Season with black pepper. Spread into a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
