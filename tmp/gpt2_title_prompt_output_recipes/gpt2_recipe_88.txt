Turkey Tetrazzini I<ING>• 2 cups uncooked pasta• 2 cups chopped, cooked turkey• 1 tablespoon butter• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/2 cup chopped onion• 1/4 cup chopped celery• 3 cups chicken broth• 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese• 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 cup chopped green onions<INS>‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ Meanwhile, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour and season with salt. Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture begins to bubble. Add onions, celery and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer over low heat for 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.‣ Stir in cheese, pepper and green onions. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for another 8 to 10 minutes.
