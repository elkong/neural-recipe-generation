Basil Chicken Ravioli Carbonara<ING>• 2 cups chicken broth• 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1 tablespoon garlic salt• 1/2 teaspoon dried basil• 2 cups fresh spinach, washed and dried• 3 tablespoons olive oil• 1/4 cup chopped onion• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme• 2 teaspoons dried oregano• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms• 2 (4 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves• 1/2 cup frozen green peas• 1/4 cup diced fresh parsley• 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese<INS>‣ In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the chicken broth, Parmesan cheese, garlic salt, basil, spinach, olive oil, onion, cumin, oregano, thyme, oregano, and black pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.‣ In a bowl, whisk together the mushrooms, chicken, peas, parsley, feta cheese, and remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Pour mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bake 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and lightly browned.
