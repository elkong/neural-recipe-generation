Jo Jo's Favorite Banana Muffins<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/4 cup white sugar• 1/2 cup shortening• 1 cup packed brown sugar• 1 egg• 1/2 cup buttermilk• 2 ripe bananas, mashed• 1 tablespoon butter• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease 12 muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.‣ Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Set aside.‣ In a medium bowl, cream shortening and brown sugar together. Add egg and buttermilk, then stir into creamed mixture. Mix in mashed bananas, butter and vanilla. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
