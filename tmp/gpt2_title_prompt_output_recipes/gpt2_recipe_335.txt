Tangy Sliced Pork Sandwiches<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon sesame oil• 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice• 2 cups sliced pork, cut into 1/2-inch strips• 1/4 cup chopped green onions• 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce• 1/2 cup brown sugar• 2 tablespoons butter• 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root• 2 teaspoons minced fresh cilantro• 1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon pepper• 1 (6 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms• 1 cup chopped peanuts• 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese• 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro<INS>‣ Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic, and saute for 1 minute, until fragrant, and remove from heat. Stir in sesame oil, lemon juice, and pork strips. Simmer for 5 minutes.‣ In a medium bowl, mix together tomato sauce, brown sugar, butter, ginger, cilantro, parsley, salt, and pepper.‣ Stir in mushrooms and peanuts. Simmer for 5 minutes, until soft. Remove from heat.‣ Mix in Monterey Jack cheese, and stir until melted.‣ Place pan in a large, deep skillet. Cook for 1 minute per side, or until bubbly. Serve immediately.
