Old-Fashioned Macaroni, Tomato, and Cheese Bake<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/4 cup butter, melted• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ In a large bowl, combine the spinach, butter, Parmesan cheese, 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, and 1 cup mozzarella cheese. Mix well, and spread mixture evenly over the cooked macaroni.‣ Bake for 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until heated through and cheese is melted and bubbly.
