Jackie's Killer Krab Dip<ING>• 2 cups mayonnaise• 2 cups sour cream• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/2 cup chopped green onion• 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley<INS>‣ In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, Jack cheese, Cheddar cheese, green onion, and parsley.
