Lyndsie's Dr. Pepper® Meatballs<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 pound lean ground beef• 1/2 onion, chopped• 1/2 green bell pepper, chopped• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1/2 cup water• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 teaspoon dried oregano• 1 teaspoon celery seed• 2 (16 ounce) packages frozen mixed vegetables, thawed• 1/4 cup water<INS>‣ Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir the ground beef, onion, bell pepper, and garlic until the beef is crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink, about 10 minutes. Drain excess grease. Stir in the water, salt, pepper, oregano, celery seed, mixed vegetables, and enough water to make a sticky paste. Simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes.‣ Mix the meatballs into the saucepan, and simmer until well coated with sauce, about 5 minutes.
