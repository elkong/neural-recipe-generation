Slow Cooker Buffalo Chicken Lettuce Wraps<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/4 cup butter, melted• 1/4 cup chopped onion• 1/4 cup chopped celery• 3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 2 eggs, beaten• 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese• 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese• 2 cups sour cream• 1/4 cup water• 1/4 teaspoon dried dill weed• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder• salt and ground black pepper to taste<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ Meanwhile, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion, celery and 1/4 cup shredded cheese and saute until soft. Stir in eggs, 3/4 cup shredded cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, sour cream, water, dill, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Mix well and pour mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
