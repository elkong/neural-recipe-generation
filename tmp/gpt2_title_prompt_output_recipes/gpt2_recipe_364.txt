Chocolate Covered Pretzels<ING>• 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1/4 cup water• 1 cup milk• 2 cups crushed pretzels<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch pan.‣ In a medium bowl, stir together the chocolate chips, sugar and water. Stir in the milk, one at a time, until well blended. Stir in the crushed pretzels. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.‣ Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
