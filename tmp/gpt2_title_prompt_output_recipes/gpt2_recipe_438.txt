Ginger Shrimp with Fried Rice<ING>• 2 cups uncooked white rice• 2 teaspoons lemon juice• 1 teaspoon sesame oil• 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest• 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce• 2 tablespoons sesame oil• 1/4 cup chopped green onions• 3 tablespoons sesame seeds• 1 tablespoon sesame seeds• 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes• 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root• 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro• 1/4 cup sesame oil• 2 tablespoons rice vinegar• 1 tablespoon soy sauce• 1 tablespoon sesame oil• 1/4 cup chopped green onions• 1 tablespoon sesame seeds• 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes<INS>‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add rice and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until tender but firm; drain.‣ Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine lemon juice, sesame oil, green onions, sesame seeds, sesame seeds, and red pepper flakes; bring to a boil, then reduce heat, and simmer for 5 minutes.‣ Meanwhile, in a large bowl, mix together rice, vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, rice, and red pepper flakes. Stir in ginger and cilantro. Cook, stirring frequently, for 15 minutes, or until rice is tender. Remove from heat, and stir in 1/4 cup sesame oil, and sesame seeds. Allow to cool, then mix in green onions and cilantro.
