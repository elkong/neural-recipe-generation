Creamy Veggie Potato Stew<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of potato soup• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup• 1 1/2 cups water• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder• 1/2 teaspoon onion powder• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 2 cups vegetable broth• 1 teaspoon dried parsley• 1 teaspoon dried thyme• 1/2 teaspoon dried basil• 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme leaves• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 cups frozen peas• 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese<INS>‣ Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Cook and stir onion in hot oil until softened and translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir hash browns into onion mixture, and cook until hash browns are soft, about 2 minutes more. Stir soup, cream of potato soup, cream of celery soup, water, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and nutmeg into onion mixture; bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer until flavors have blended, about 1 hour.‣ Stir vegetable broth, parsley, thyme, basil, thyme, and black pepper into soup mixture; bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer until flavors have blended, about 1 hour.‣ Stir peas into soup mixture; cook and stir until peas are soft and hot, about 5 minutes more. Pour vegetable broth mixture into the pot with the hash brown mixture; stir to coat.‣ Sprinkle Cheddar cheese into soup mixture; cook and stir until cheese is melted, 5 to 10 minutes.
