Spicy South of the Border Spinach Cheese Dip<ING>• 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup• 1 (4 ounce) can sliced mushrooms• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/4 cup sour cream<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Place spinach in a bowl. Sprinkle with mushroom soup, cream of mushroom soup, cream of chicken soup, mushrooms, and cheese. Mix well. Pour mixture into prepared baking dish.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Sprinkle with sour cream and bake an additional 15 minutes, until bubbly and lightly browned.
