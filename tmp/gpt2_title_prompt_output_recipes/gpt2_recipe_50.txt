Orange Snowman Cake<ING>• 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 cup butter• 3/4 cup packed brown sugar• 3/4 cup white sugar• 1 egg• 1/3 cup orange juice• 1/2 cup butter, softened• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 3 cups confectioners' sugar<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 8 inch round pan.‣ Sift the flour, 1/2 cup sugar, salt, baking soda, cinnamon and white sugar together. Set aside.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the 1 1/4 cups butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg and orange juice. Beat in the flour mixture alternately with the confectioner's sugar until just incorporated. Pour batter into prepared pan.‣ Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
