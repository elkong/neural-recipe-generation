Roasted Garlic Scalloped Potatoes<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme• 1 pound scallops, rinsed and drained• 1 cup chicken broth• 1/4 cup lemon juice• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley• 2 tablespoons butter• 2 teaspoons minced fresh rosemary<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in garlic, salt, and black pepper; cook and stir until the garlic softens, about 5 minutes.‣ Stir thyme into the hot oil, then add scallops and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 5 minutes more.‣ Stir chicken broth into scallops and cook until reduced by half, 5 to 8 minutes.‣ Remove scallops from skillet and place into a shallow baking dish. Pour chicken broth into the baking dish, then drizzle with lemon juice. Sprinkle with parsley and butter; toss to coat.‣ Bake in the preheated oven until the parsley has caramelized and the scallops are tender, about 30 minutes.
