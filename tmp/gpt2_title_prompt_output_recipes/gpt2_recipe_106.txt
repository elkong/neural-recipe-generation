Diet Soup III<ING>• 3 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil• 1 onion, chopped• 3 cups chopped celery• 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced• 1 cup chopped carrots• 1 pound ground beef• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon dried thyme• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce• 1 teaspoon dried basil• 1/2 teaspoon dried sage• 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder• 2 cups chicken broth• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar• 1 teaspoon white sugar• 1/4 cup water• 1/2 cup soy sauce• 1/4 cup brown sugar• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar• 1 tablespoon white sugar• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/4 teaspoon onion powder<INS>‣ Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in onion and celery and saute until tender.‣ Stir in mushrooms, carrots, beef, salt, thyme, black pepper, Worcestershire sauce, basil, sage, oregano, garlic powder, chicken broth, distilled white vinegar, white sugar, water, soy sauce, brown sugar, 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar, salt, black pepper and onion powder. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
