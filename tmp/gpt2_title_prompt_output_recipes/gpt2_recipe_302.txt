Blizzard Cake III<ING>• 1 1/4 cups white sugar• 1 cup butter• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 2 eggs• 1 1/4 cups milk• 1 cup sour cream• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 cup raisins• 1/4 cup chopped walnuts• 1 cup chopped pecans<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan. Sift together the flour, baking soda and salt; set aside.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar and white sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the milk, sour cream and vanilla. Beat in the raisins, nuts and pecans. Pour batter into prepared pan.‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 50 to 55 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
