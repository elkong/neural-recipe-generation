Old-Fashioned Vegetable Soup<ING>• 2 cups chicken broth• 1 cup half-and-half• 1 tablespoon vegetable oil• 1/2 onion, chopped• 2 cups carrots, diced• 1/2 cup diced celery• 1/4 cup chopped celery• 3 stalks celery, chopped• 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes• 1/2 teaspoon dried basil• 1 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 cup frozen peas• 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley• 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms• 1 cup chopped fresh spinach<INS>‣ In a large pot, bring the chicken broth, half-and-half, oil, onion, carrots, celery, celery, celery, tomatoes, basil, oregano, and black pepper to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.‣ Meanwhile, in a blender, combine the peas, parsley, mushrooms, spinach, and chicken broth. Blend until smooth.
