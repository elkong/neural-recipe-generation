Bread Machine Swedish Coffee Bread<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 cup butter• 2 cups white sugar• 2 eggs• 1 cup buttermilk• 2 tablespoons baking powder• 1 tablespoon allspice• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 cup raisins• 1 cup milk• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1/4 cup chopped walnuts (optional)<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour three 9x5 inch loaf pans.‣ In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, ginger, cloves, nutmeg, and spices. Cut in butter or margarine until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.‣ In a large bowl, combine sugar, eggs, buttermilk, baking powder, allspice, cinnamon and raisins. Mix thoroughly. Stir in milk and vanilla. Pour batter into prepared pans.‣ Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until done. Cool in pans for 5 minutes, then turn out onto wire rack to cool completely.
