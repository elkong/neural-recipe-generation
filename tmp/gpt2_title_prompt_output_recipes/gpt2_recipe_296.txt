Mexican Fiesta Pasta Salad<ING>• 2 (16 ounce) packages tri-colored rotini pasta• 1 pound lean ground beef• 1 (16 ounce) package cubed Cheddar cheese• 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained• 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained• 1 (8 ounce) can diced green chiles, drained• 1 (6 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers• 1/4 cup chopped onion• 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1 (2.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix• 1/2 cup water• 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)<INS>‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook rotini in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.‣ Place ground beef, Cheddar cheese, kidney beans, diced tomatoes, green chiles, green chile peppers, onion, bell pepper, celery, and taco seasoning mix in a large bowl; mix until well blended. Toss to coat.‣ Heat water and taco seasoning in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir beef mixture until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.‣ Mix green chili peppers, onion, green bell pepper, and celery into the skillet; cook and stir until the vegetables soften and begin to brown, about 5 minutes.‣ Stir vegetable mixture into ground beef mixture; heat through. Garnish with cilantro to serve.
