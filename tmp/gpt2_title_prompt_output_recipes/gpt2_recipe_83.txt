Venison Fajitas<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound venison stew meat• 1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1/2 cup water• 1/2 cup tomato sauce• 1 (1 ounce) package dry ranch dressing mix• 1 cup sour cream• 2 tablespoons butter• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 tablespoons chili powder• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro<INS>‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion; cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. Add venison, mushrooms, garlic, water, tomato sauce, ranch dressing mix, sour cream, butter, salt, pepper and chili powder to onion mixture; bring to a boil and cook until heated through, about 10 minutes.‣ Serve hot with Cheddar cheese and fresh cilantro.
