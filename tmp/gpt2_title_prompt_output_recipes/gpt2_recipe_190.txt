Irish Heritage Cabbage<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/4 cup butter, melted• 1 onion, diced• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon dried thyme• 1 (8 ounce) package baby carrots• 2 cups shredded cabbage• 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese<INS>‣ Cook elbow macaroni in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Rinse under cold water and drain.‣ In a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter or margarine and saute onion and salt until onion is soft and transparent.‣ Stir in spinach, butter, onion mixture, carrots, cabbage and cheese. Cook and stir until cheese is melted. Serve hot.
