Stir-Fry Chicken and Broccoli<ING>• 2 cups chicken broth• 1 cup dry sherry• 1/2 cup orange juice• 1/2 cup lemon juice• 2 tablespoons rice vinegar• 1 tablespoon vegetable oil• 1 tablespoon ground ginger• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 teaspoon dried oregano• 1 teaspoon celery seed• 1 tablespoon butter• 1 cup chopped fresh broccoli• 1 teaspoon sesame oil• 2 tablespoons honey• 1 cup sesame seeds<INS>‣ Heat the chicken broth and sherry in a small saucepan over medium heat until reduced by half. Stir in the orange juice, lemon juice, rice vinegar, vegetable oil, ginger, salt, pepper, oregano, celery seed, butter, broccoli, and sesame oil. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer gently until the broccoli is tender, about 20 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in honey and sesame seeds.
