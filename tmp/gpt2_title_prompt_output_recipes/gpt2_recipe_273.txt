Vegan Mexican Stew<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/2 cup water• 1 (1 pound) loaf processed cheese food, cubed• 1/2 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend<INS>‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ Meanwhile, combine spinach, water and cheese in a large bowl.‣ Place cubed processed cheese in the pot with the spinach mixture. Pour in the water, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes. Stir, and continue cooking for 20 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
