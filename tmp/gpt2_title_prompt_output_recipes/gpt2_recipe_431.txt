Grilled Romaine Pork Chops<ING>• 1 1/2 cups dry navy beans• 1 cup sherry• 1/4 cup vegetable oil• 1/4 cup water• 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice• 1/4 cup chopped onion• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin• 1/2 teaspoon paprika• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder• 1/4 teaspoon onion powder• 2 pounds pork chops• salt and ground black pepper to taste<INS>‣ Place navy beans in a large pot, cover, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes. Drain and reserve.‣ Preheat grill for high heat.‣ In a large bowl, combine sherry, vegetable oil, water, lemon juice, onion, cumin, paprika, black pepper, oregano, garlic powder, onion powder, pork chops, salt, and pepper. Gently stir to coat.‣ Lightly oil the grill grate. Cook pork chops for 5 to 7 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
