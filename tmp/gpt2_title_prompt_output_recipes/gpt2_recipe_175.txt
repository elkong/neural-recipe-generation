Soft Polenta with Spicy Tomato Sauce<ING>• 2 cups uncooked polenta• 2 teaspoons olive oil• 1 teaspoon minced garlic• 1/4 cup vegetable oil• 1/4 cup water• 3/4 cup uncooked long grain white rice• 1 cup chopped fresh mushrooms• 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce• 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Heat olive oil and garlic in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add water and cook for 5 minutes, or until just tender. Mix in rice, and cook for 1 minute. Stir in mushrooms and tomato sauce. Season with parsley. Pour into prepared baking dish.‣ Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 40 minutes.
