Chicken Afritada<ING>• 2 cups uncooked basmati rice• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/4 cup butter, melted• 1/2 cup chopped onion• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander• 1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper• 1/4 teaspoon ground coriander• 1/2 teaspoon ground cilantro• 1/4 cup fresh lime juice• 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro• 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar• 1 tablespoon white sugar• 1 teaspoon salt<INS>‣ In a saucepan, combine the rice, spinach, butter, onion, garlic, cumin, salt, pepper, coriander, cayenne pepper, coriander, cilantro, lime juice, and cilantro. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.‣ Meanwhile, whisk together the vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl. Pour over the rice mixture, and stir to coat.
