Strawberry Cobbler I<ING>• 2 cups white sugar• 1 cup packed brown sugar• 1/2 cup butter• 1/4 cup vegetable oil• 1/4 cup water• 3 eggs• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon baking powder• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/2 cup chopped fresh strawberries• 1/4 cup fresh pecans<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the white sugar, brown sugar and butter until smooth. Stir in the vegetable oil and water. Mix in eggs one at a time, then stir in the flour, baking powder, and salt. Stir in strawberries, pecans and fresh strawberries. Pour into the prepared pan.‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 45 minutes, or until a knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
