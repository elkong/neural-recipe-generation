Spinach and Chicken Enchiladas<ING>• 2 cups salsa• 1 1/2 cups water• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/2 cup sour cream• 1/4 teaspoon chili powder• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste• 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers (such as RO*TEL®), undrained• 1 (4 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained• 1/2 cup sour cream• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro• 1/4 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese<INS>‣ Mix the salsa, water, Cheddar cheese, sour cream, chili powder, oregano, black pepper, and tomato paste in a bowl until smooth; pour into a slow cooker; stir in diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, olives, and sour cream.‣ Cover and cook on Low until the enchiladas are tender, 8 to 10 hours. Stir in the reserved tomato juice, sour cream, cilantro, and Monterey Jack cheese during the last 5 minutes of cooking.
