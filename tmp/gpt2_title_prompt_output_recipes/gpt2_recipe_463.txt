Orzo Ham Salad<ING>• 2 cups uncooked orzo pasta• 1/2 cup mayonnaise• 1/2 cup creamy salad dressing (e.g. Miracle Whip)• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper• 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper• 1 cup chopped celery• 1/2 cup chopped onion• 2 teaspoons dried oregano• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 cup raisins<INS>‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add orzo and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ Combine mayonnaise, salad dressing, celery, green, and red bell pepper in a large bowl. Stir in orzo, celery, green, onion, oregano, salt, and black pepper; toss to coat.
