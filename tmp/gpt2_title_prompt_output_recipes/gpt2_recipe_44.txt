Sweeter Muffins<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 cup butter• 2 cups white sugar• 2 eggs• 1 cup buttermilk• 2 tablespoons melted butter• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1/4 cup raisins• 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease 12 muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla until light and fluffy. Stir in flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, ginger and cloves. Beat in the flour mixture just until incorporated. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.‣ To make the glaze: In a saucepan, combine the buttermilk, 2 tablespoons melted butter, vinegar, and vanilla. Bring to a boil, then stir in raisins and nuts. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes, until thickened.
