Healthier Good Old Fashioned Pancakes<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/2 cup milk• 1/4 cup butter, melted• 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour• 1 egg, lightly beaten• 1/4 cup plain yogurt• 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 2 quart baking dish.‣ In a large bowl, stir together 2 cups flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, ginger, and cloves.‣ In a separate bowl, whisk together 1/4 cup melted butter, 2 tablespoons flour, egg, yogurt, and Parmesan cheese. Pour flour mixture into creamed mixture until just combined. Spread batter evenly in prepared baking dish.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
