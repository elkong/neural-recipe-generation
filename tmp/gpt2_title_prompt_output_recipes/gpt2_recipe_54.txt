Beef Stew VI<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound boneless beef stew meat, cut into 1 inch cubes• 2 cups beef broth• 3 (14.5 ounce) cans peeled and diced tomatoes• 1 (15 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes• 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained• 1 cup chopped cooked ham• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme• 2 bay leaves• 1 teaspoon dried oregano• 1 teaspoon dried basil• 1 teaspoon dried thyme• 1 bay leaf• 1 teaspoon dried thyme• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese<INS>‣ Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat; cook and stir the onion until tender. Mix in the beef, broth, diced tomatoes, tomatoes, kidney beans, ham, pepper, thyme, bay leaves, oregano, basil, thyme, bay leaf, and thyme. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until the meat is very tender, about 1 1/2 hours.‣ Mix Parmesan cheese into the stew, cover, and cook until cheese is melted.
