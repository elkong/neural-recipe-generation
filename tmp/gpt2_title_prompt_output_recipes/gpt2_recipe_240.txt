Beef Stroganoff II<ING>• 2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup• 1 (10.75 ounce) can beef broth• 1 1/2 cups milk• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Swiss cheese• 1 pound cooked flank steak, cut into strips• 1 cup chopped fresh parsley<INS>‣ In a large pot over medium heat, combine the mushroom soup, celery soup, beef broth and milk. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 5 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed.‣ In a medium bowl, blend the cheese and beef. Stir in the mushrooms, then spread into a greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Sprinkle with parsley and cover with foil.‣ Bake in a preheated 400 degree F (200 degree C) oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
