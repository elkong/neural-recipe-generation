Chef John's Broiled Chicken<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes• 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup• 1/4 cup sour cream• 1/4 cup white wine• 1/4 cup sour cream• 1/4 cup chopped onion• salt and pepper to taste• 1 (4 pound) whole chicken<INS>‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes. Saute until fragrant, about 5 minutes. Add mushrooms and saute until tender, about 5 minutes.‣ Stir soup, sour cream, wine, sour cream, and onion into the soup mixture. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.‣ Add chicken and simmer for 5 more minutes.
