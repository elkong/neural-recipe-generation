Ginger Rhubarb Crisp<ING>• 2 cups white sugar• 1 cup packed light brown sugar• 1 cup all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 teaspoon ground cloves• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 cup chopped fresh ginger root• 1/2 cup raisins• 1/4 cup molasses• 1/4 cup vegetable oil• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.‣ In a large bowl, stir together white sugar, brown sugar, flour, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and ginger.‣ In a separate bowl, sift together raisins and molasses. Add oil, and whisk until blended.‣ Drop by teaspoonfuls onto prepared cookie sheets. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, until golden brown. Cool on baking sheet for 1 minute before removing to wire racks to cool completely.
