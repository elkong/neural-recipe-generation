Ricotta Breakfast Pancakes<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1 egg, beaten• 1 cup milk• 1/2 cup white sugar• 2 tablespoons butter, melted<INS>‣ Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 2-quart baking dish.‣ Whisk flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, egg, and milk together in a bowl; pour into the prepared baking dish. Stir butter into the flour mixture until evenly moist. Pour 1/2 cup of batter over the batter in the baking dish.‣ Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 25 to 30 minutes.
