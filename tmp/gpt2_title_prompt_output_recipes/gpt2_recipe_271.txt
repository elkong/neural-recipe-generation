Parmesan Lemon Chicken<ING>• 2 cups Italian-style salad dressing• 1/2 cup prepared lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage• 1 cup milk• 2 cups fresh sliced mushrooms• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley<INS>‣ Combine the Italian-style salad dressing, lemon-lime soda, milk, mushrooms, salt, and pepper in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes.‣ Remove the cover from the saucepan and continue to simmer until the sauce thickens slightly, about 5 minutes. Stir in the parsley before serving.
