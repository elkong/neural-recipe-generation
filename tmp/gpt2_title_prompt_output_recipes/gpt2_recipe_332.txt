Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ In a large bowl, mix the seasoned bread crumbs, seasoned salt, onion powder, garlic powder, pepper, and beef. In a separate bowl, mix the onion, celery, carrot, celery, carrot, and celery.‣ In a separate bowl, mix the tomato sauce, tomato sauce, tomato sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Pour the seasoned bread crumbs mixture into the beef mixture and mix well. Stir in the seasoned bread crumbs mixture and the chopped tomatoes. Spread evenly in the prepared baking dish.‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the meat is no longer pink.
