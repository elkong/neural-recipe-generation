Greek Orzo with Feta, Tomato, and Shredded Cheese<ING>• 1/2 cup plain yogurt• 1/4 cup olive oil• 1 tablespoon minced garlic• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary• 1/2 teaspoon dried basil• 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme• 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary leaves• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin• 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano• 2 (4 ounce) orzo pasta• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese<INS>‣ Mix yogurt, olive oil, garlic, garlic, oregano, rosemary, basil, thyme, rosemary, cumin, oregano, and 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese together in a bowl. Cover and refrigerate overnight.‣ Preheat an oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a medium baking dish.‣ Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat. Once the water is boiling, stir in the orzo, and return to a boil. Cook the pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the pasta has cooked through, but is still firm to the bite, about 11 minutes. Drain well in a colander set in the sink.‣ Stir 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese into the boiling pasta until melted. Pour over the orzo and serve.
