Easy Tomato Sauce<ING>• 2 (16 ounce) packages fresh tomatoes• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/4 cup butter, melted• salt and pepper to taste<INS>‣ In a large pot over medium heat, cook tomatoes and spinach until wilted. Season with salt and pepper to taste.‣ In a small bowl, mix together the melted butter and chopped tomatoes. Pour mixture into a 2 quart casserole dish, and sprinkle with cheese.‣ Bake uncovered at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes.
