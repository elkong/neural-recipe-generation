Wasabi Mayo Salmon Dip<ING>• 1 tablespoon wasabi• 1 teaspoon minced garlic• 1/2 teaspoon onion powder• 1/2 teaspoon dried basil• 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/2 cup mayonnaise• 1/2 cup sour cream• 2 tablespoons mayonnaise• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro<INS>‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the wasabi, garlic, onion powder, basil, oregano, thyme, and pepper. Stir in the mayonnaise, sour cream, mayonnaise, cilantro, and remaining wasabi. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
