Summer Zucchini Casserole<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/2 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese• 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese• 1 cup chopped fresh parsley• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ In a large bowl combine the spinach, Cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, parsley, Parmesan cheese, pepper and macaroni. Pour into a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.
