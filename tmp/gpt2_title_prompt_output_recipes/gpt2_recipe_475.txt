White Yeast Loaves<ING>• 2 cups warm water• 1 cup shortening• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour• 1 cup shortening• 1/4 cup cold water• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 egg, beaten• 1/3 cup white sugar• 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour• 1/2 cup water• 2 tablespoons cornstarch• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract<INS>‣ In a large bowl, mix together the warm water, shortening, 1/2 cup flour, cold water, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, allspice, 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves, allspice, and cinnamon.‣ In a small bowl, mix together the egg, sugar, 1 tablespoon flour, and 1/2 cup water. Stir in cornstarch until dissolved. Slowly stir in 1/2 cup water until dough is stiff, and can be pulled apart into balls.‣ Heat a large, heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Drop balls by large spoonfuls onto the skillet, and cook until golden brown on both sides. Serve hot.
