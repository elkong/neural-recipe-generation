Club Chicken Casserole<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/4 cup butter, melted• 1 pound ground chicken• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 1 cup chopped onions• 1/4 cup margarine• 2 cups heavy cream• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper• 1/4 teaspoon dried basil• 2 (4 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 1 cup shredded onion• 1/2 cup water• 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese• 1 tablespoon butter• 1 cup sour cream• 1 cup milk• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes; drain.‣ Melt 1/4 cup butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir chicken and 1 cup Cheddar cheese until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 10 minutes.‣ Stir chicken mixture into cooked pasta, cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.‣ Stir onions and 1/4 cup margarine into the skillet. Cook and stir until onions are tender and beginning to brown, about 5 minutes.‣ Stir cream, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon pepper, and cayenne pepper into chicken mixture.‣ Stir water, 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese, 1 tablespoon butter, sour cream, milk, and 1/2 teaspoon salt into the chicken mixture.‣ Pour sauce over cooked pasta.‣ Cover baking dish with aluminum foil.
