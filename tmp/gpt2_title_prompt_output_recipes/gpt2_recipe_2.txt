Cranberry Trail Bars<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1 cup packed brown sugar• 1 cup canned cranberries• 1 cup chopped pecans• 1 cup chopped walnuts• 1/2 cup butter, softened• 2 eggs• 1 cup sour cream• 2 teaspoons vanilla extract• 1/4 cup chopped pecans<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.‣ In a large bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar. Stir in the brown sugar and cranberries. Make a well in the center, and pour in the pecans, nuts, butter, eggs, sour cream, and vanilla extract. Mix until well blended. Pour into the prepared pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cool in pan for 5 minutes, then cut into bars.
