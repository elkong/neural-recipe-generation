Scalloped Potatoes with Ham and Bacon<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of potato soup• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup• 1 1/2 teaspoons salt• 1/2 teaspoon black pepper• 2 tablespoons butter• 2 teaspoons minced fresh rosemary• 2 tablespoons minced fresh thyme• 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir onion in hot oil until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir hash browns, condensed soup, cream of potato soup, salt, pepper, butter, and rosemary into onion mixture; cook until heated through, about 5 minutes. Pour cheese sauce over hash brown mixture; stir to coat.‣ Bake in preheated oven until hash brown mixture is bubbling and cheese sauce has melted, about 10 minutes.
