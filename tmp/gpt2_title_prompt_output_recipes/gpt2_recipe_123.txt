Chicken Stew with Pepper and Pineapple<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into thin strips• 2 cups red wine• 3 cloves garlic, crushed• 1 1/2 cups chicken broth• 1 cup chicken broth• 1 1/2 teaspoons salt• 1/2 teaspoon black pepper• 2 bay leaves• 2 teaspoons dried parsley• 1 teaspoon dried oregano• 2 teaspoons chopped fresh sage• 2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme• 1/4 cup butter, melted<INS>‣ Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the onion and saute for 5 to 10 minutes. Add the chicken and cook for 3 minutes. Add the wine, garlic, and chicken broth; bring to a boil and reduce heat to low. Simmer until the chicken is cooked through, and the juices run clear, about 20 minutes.‣ Add salt, pepper, bay leaves, parsley, oregano, sage, thyme, and butter. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to low. Simmer for 5 minutes, then add the cooked chicken and stir until the chicken is no longer pink and the juices run clear.
