Pumpkin Butter II<ING>• 2 cups white sugar• 1 cup canned pumpkin• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 cup butter• 1/4 cup chopped pecans<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x9 inch pan.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the sugar, pumpkin and flour. Stir in baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg and butter. Stir in pecans. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
