Okanagan Peach Soup<ING>• 2 cups peeled, seeded peaches• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour• 1/4 cup vegetable oil• 1/4 cup water• 3/4 cup sliced fresh peaches• 1/4 cup chopped onion• 3 tablespoons butter• 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root• 1 teaspoon ground cumin• 1 teaspoon ground coriander• 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric• 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper• 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/8 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/8 teaspoon salt• 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/8 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Place peaches into a large saucepan and fill with enough water to cover by 1 inch. Bring to a boil, and cook until peaches are softened and just starting to pop, about 15 minutes. Drain, cool slightly, and mash.‣ Heat vegetable oil and water together in a saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir onion in the hot oil until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in butter, ginger, cumin, coriander, turmeric, cayenne pepper, cinnamon, cloves, allspice, ginger, cloves, salt, black pepper, allspice, and cinnamon; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until spices have reduced, about 30 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in peaches, and serve.
