Jen's Creamy Garlic Mashed Potatoes<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed• 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened• 1 cup milk<INS>‣ Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in onion, and cook until onion is translucent. Mix in potatoes and Cheddar cheese. Cook and stir until cheese is melted. Stir in Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer, and reduce heat to low.‣ Gradually stir in cream cheese and milk until well blended. Continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until cheese is melted.
