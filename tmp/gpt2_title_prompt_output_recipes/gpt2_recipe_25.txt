Flash Baked Walleye Fillets<ING>• 2 cups walleye• 2 cups flaked coconut• 1 cup sifted all-purpose flour• 1 cup milk• 2 tablespoons butter, melted<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Sprinkle walleye and coconut in a single layer on a baking sheet. Bake in preheated oven until coconut is tender and coconut is golden, about 10 minutes.‣ Meanwhile, melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir flour until flour is a light brown color, about 1 minute. Stir milk and melted butter into flour; cook and stir until butter is completely melted, 2 to 3 minutes.‣ Arrange walleye fillets on the baking sheet and sprinkle with coconut mixture.‣ Bake fillets in the preheated oven until golden, 4 to 5 minutes.
