Mary Wynne's Crabapple Jelly<ING>• 2 cups white sugar• 1 cup light corn syrup• 1/2 cup orange juice• 1/2 cup lemon juice• 2 cups crushed ice<INS>‣ In a saucepan, combine sugar and corn syrup. Bring to a boil, stirring frequently. Remove from heat, and stir in orange juice, lemon juice and crushed ice. Cover, and chill in refrigerator for at least one hour.
