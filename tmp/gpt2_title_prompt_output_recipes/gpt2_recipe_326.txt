Roasted Sweet Potatoes and Vegetables With Thyme and Maple Syrup<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large sweet potato, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch cubes• 1/4 cup maple syrup• 1/4 cup honey• 2 tablespoons maple syrup• 1 tablespoon lemon juice• 1 teaspoon lemon zest• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 cloves garlic, minced• 1/2 cup chopped sweet potato• 1/4 cup chopped apples• 1/4 cup sliced almonds<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking dish.‣ Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir sweet potatoes in the hot oil until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir maple syrup, honey, and 1 tablespoon maple syrup into sweet potatoes; cook and stir until maple syrup is dissolved, about 1 minute. Season with lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and pepper.‣ Stir garlic and sweet potato into sweet potato mixture; cook and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute. Pour sweet potato mixture over sweet potato mixture and gently toss to coat.‣ Arrange apple slices over sweet potato mixture; sprinkle with almonds and drizzle with remaining maple syrup mixture.‣ Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, about 45 minutes.
