Bow Ties with Veggies<ING>• 2 cups quick cooking oats• 2 cups chopped kale• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1/2 cup chopped carrots• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1/2 cup chopped onion• 1 cup chopped celery• 3/4 cup brown sugar• 2/3 cup all-purpose flour• 2 eggs• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/4 cup milk• 1 cup vegetable oil• 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar• 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar• 1 tablespoon milk• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1/4 cup chopped pecans• 1 tablespoon white sugar• 1 teaspoon salt<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Place oats, kale, celery, carrots, celery, onion, celery, and brown sugar in a blender; pulse until well blended.‣ Beat eggs, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Whisk together the flour, eggs, and cinnamon in a separate bowl. Add the milk and oil, and stir until just combined. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish.‣ Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean, 45 to 50 minutes.‣ Whisk together the confectioners' sugar, apple cider vinegar, milk, vanilla extract, and pecans in a bowl until smooth. Stir in the pecans.‣ Drizzle the glaze over the cake and sprinkle with sugar and salt. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
