Upside Down Pizza<ING>• 2 (10 inch) flour tortillas• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/2 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese• 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder• 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper• 1 (9 inch) prepared pizza crust<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Place the frozen spinach into a bowl. In a separate bowl, mix the spinach with the Cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, Cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, cayenne pepper and pizza crust. Spread the spinach mixture into the prepared baking dish.‣ Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
