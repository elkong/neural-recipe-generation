Anne's Homemade Chorizo<ING>• 2 cups uncooked white rice• 2 1/2 cups water• 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1 inch pieces• 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), rinsed and drained• 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained• 1 (16 ounce) can white beans, rinsed and drained• 1 1/2 cups sour cream• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro• 2 teaspoons chopped fresh oregano• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro• 2 teaspoons chopped fresh cilantro• 1/4 cup chopped fresh lemon juice• 1 1/2 teaspoons salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese<INS>‣ In a saucepan, bring the rice and water to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 20 minutes.‣ In a large bowl, toss together the rice, chicken, chickpeas, kidney beans, white beans, sour cream, parsley, cilantro, cilantro, cilantro, oregano, oregano, cilantro, and 1 tablespoon cilantro.‣ In a separate bowl, mix together 1 tablespoon cilantro, 1 tablespoon oregano, 1 tablespoon oregano, and 1 tablespoon cilantro. Add the lemon juice, salt, and pepper; mix well.‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).‣ Remove the cover, and bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven. Remove from heat, and stir in Cheddar cheese. Serve immediately.
