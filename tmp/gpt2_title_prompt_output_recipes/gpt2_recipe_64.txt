Wonderful Waffles<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 cup butter• 2 cups white sugar• 2 eggs• 1 cup buttermilk• 2 tablespoons melted butter• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1/4 cup raisins• 1 tablespoon baking powder<INS>‣ Preheat waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions.‣ Combine flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, ginger, 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves, and 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg. Set aside.‣ Beat 2 cups sugar and 2 eggs in large bowl using an electric mixer until fluffy. Beat in buttermilk, 2 tablespoons melted butter, vinegar, vanilla, and raisins. Beat until smooth. Pour into prepared waffle iron.‣ Cook waffles according to manufacturer's directions until golden brown and crisp.
