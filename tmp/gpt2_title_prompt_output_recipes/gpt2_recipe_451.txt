White Chocolate Covered Pretzels<ING>• 2 cups white chocolate chips• 2 cups chopped walnuts• 1 cup all-purpose flour• 1 cup white sugar• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 cup confectioners' sugar for decoration<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Mix white chocolate, nuts, flour, sugar, baking soda and salt in a bowl until smooth. Cut in confectioners' sugar with a pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Press mixture into a 9x13 inch baking pan.‣ Bake in the preheated oven until pretzels are slightly browned, 10 to 12 minutes.
