Zucchini Brownies<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/2 cup butter, softened• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1 egg• 1 cup chopped zucchini• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 cup raisins• 1/4 cup chopped walnuts• 1/2 cup chopped walnuts• 1/2 cup chopped pistachio nuts<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch pan.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar, and egg until light and fluffy. Beat in the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Stir in the zucchini, vanilla and raisins. Combine the flour mixture with the remaining 1/4 cup of the zucchini mixture; stir into the zucchini mixture. Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan.‣ Bake for 15 to 20 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.‣ To make the topping: In a medium bowl, combine the chopped nuts, chopped nuts and pistachios. Blend together the walnuts and pistachios; sprinkle over the cooled crust. Refrigerate until serving.
