Chili I<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon onion powder• 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin• 2 cups red or green beans, rinsed and drained• 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes• 1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder• 1 teaspoon ground cumin• 1 teaspoon ground coriander• 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric• 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper• 1/2 cup white wine• 1/2 cup chopped red onion<INS>‣ Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Saute garlic and onion until soft, about 1 minute. Stir in the beans, tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, cayenne pepper, and white wine. Simmer 15 minutes. Stir in the onion and cook until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes more.
