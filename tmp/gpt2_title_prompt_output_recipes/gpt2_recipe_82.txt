Veracruz-Style Red Snapper<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes• 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms• 1 (12 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained• 1 cup chopped celery• 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained• 1 cup chopped celery• 1 cup chopped red bell pepper• 2 teaspoons dried oregano• 1 teaspoon ground cumin• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 pounds flank steak• 4 cups cooked, cubed red snapper<INS>‣ Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir the garlic, red pepper flakes, and mushrooms in the hot oil until the mushrooms have softened, about 10 minutes.‣ Mix the mandarin oranges, celery, pineapple, and celery in a bowl; set aside. In a bowl, whisk together the carrot, pineapple, and celery with their juice. Season with oregano, cumin, and pepper. Set aside.‣ Cook the flank steak in the skillet over medium heat until evenly browned, 5 to 7 minutes per side. Stir the snapper into the mixture; cover and cook until the steak is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 3 to 5 more minutes.
