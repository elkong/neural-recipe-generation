Katrina's Best-Ever Chocolate Brownies<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder• 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips• 1 cup chopped walnuts• 1/2 cup butter• 1/2 cup shortening• 1 cup white sugar• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 egg• 1/4 cup water• 1/4 cup vegetable oil• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips<INS>‣ Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.‣ Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cocoa in a large bowl. Set aside.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the unsweetened cocoa, 2 cups chocolate chips, 1 cup chopped nuts and 1/2 cup butter until smooth. Beat in the shortening, white sugar and vanilla. Beat in the egg and water. Beat until smooth. Stir in the sifted ingredients and blend just until blended.‣ Pour into the prepared pan. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes in the preheated oven, until the top springs back when lightly touched. Allow to cool completely before cutting into squares.
