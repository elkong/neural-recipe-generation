Kale Salad<ING>• 2 cups mayonnaise• 2 cups mayonnaise• 1 cup sesame seeds• 1/2 cup lemon juice• 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar• 1 teaspoon mustard powder• 1 teaspoon curry powder• 1 teaspoon dried dill weed• 1 teaspoon ground cumin• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 cup chopped celery• 1 cup chopped kale<INS>‣ In a medium bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sesame seeds, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, mustard powder, curry powder, dill, cumin, and salt. Chill until ready to serve.‣ To serve, place a leaf of kale on each serving plate. Top with mayonnaise mixture.
