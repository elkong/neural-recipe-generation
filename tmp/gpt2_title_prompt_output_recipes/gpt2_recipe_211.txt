Lentil Soup III<ING>• 2 cups chicken broth• 1 cup lentils• 1/2 cup chopped onion• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley• 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil• 1/4 cup chopped fresh thyme• 1/4 cup chopped fresh rosemary• 1 cup shredded carrot• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley• 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill• 1/4 cup chopped fresh lemon juice• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour• 1/4 cup butter, chilled<INS>‣ In a large stock pot combine the chicken broth, lentils, onion, parsley, basil, thyme, rosemary, carrot, parsley and dill. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 20 to 25 minutes, or until lentils are tender.‣ Melt the butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Gradually stir in the milk, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring constantly. Stir in the lemon juice, reduce heat to low and continue to simmer for another 10 minutes, until thickened. Serve hot.
