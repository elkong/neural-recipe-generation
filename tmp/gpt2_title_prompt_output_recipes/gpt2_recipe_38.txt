Stuffed Acorn Squash II<ING>• 2 acorn squash, halved and seeded• 1/2 cup butter• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour• 2 cups milk• 1 teaspoon dried parsley• 1 pinch ground nutmeg• 1 pinch ground cinnamon• 1 pinch ground allspice• salt and pepper to taste• 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add squash and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until tender but still firm. Drain, and transfer to a medium bowl.‣ In a medium bowl, combine the butter, flour, milk, parsley, nutmeg, cinnamon, allspice, salt and pepper. Mix well, and sprinkle over squash.‣ In a separate bowl, mix together the shredded mozzarella cheese and remaining 1/4 cup of cheese. Pour the mixture into a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until lightly browned.
