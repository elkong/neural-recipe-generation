Farmer's Ranch Potatoes<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/4 cup butter, melted• salt and ground black pepper to taste• 1 pound lean ground beef• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/4 cup sour cream<INS>‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ In a large skillet over medium heat, cook and stir beef and onion until beef is no longer pink; drain. Stir in spinach, butter, salt and pepper.‣ In a 9x13 inch baking dish layer ground beef, onion and cheese, followed by sour cream.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
