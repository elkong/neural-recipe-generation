Country Breakfast Casserole<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese• 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ Combine spinach, 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, and Parmesan cheese in a large bowl. Stir until cheese is melted and blended. Sprinkle with pepper and pour into a 2 quart casserole dish. Top with the remaining Cheddar cheese and Monterey Jack cheese.‣ Bake in preheated oven until cheese topping is golden brown, about 30 minutes.
