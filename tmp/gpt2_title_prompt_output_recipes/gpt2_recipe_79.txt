Cheesy Baked Grits<ING>• 2 cups Italian-style dry bread crumbs• 1/2 cup butter• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour• 2 cups milk• 1 teaspoon salt• 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese• 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ In a large bowl, mix the bread crumbs, butter, flour, milk, salt, and Cheddar cheese. Mix well and form into 1 inch balls. Roll the balls in the milk to coat. Dip in the cheese mixture and then press into the prepared baking dish to coat.‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until lightly browned.
