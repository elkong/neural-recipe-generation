Coconut Custard (Zucchini) Pie<ING>• 1 cup butter, softened• 1 cup white sugar• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar• 1 egg• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 cup all-purpose flour• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1 1/2 cups milk• 3 eggs, beaten• 1 cup white sugar• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 cup chopped pecans<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter and white sugar. Mix in the egg, vanilla and flour, and then salt. Stir in the milk, eggs and 1 cup sugar. Mix until well blended. Pour into a 9 inch pie plate.‣ Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until set. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
