Spanish Rice Chicken I<ING>• 2 cups uncooked white rice• 2 1/2 cups water• 2 cups uncooked instant rice• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 1 tablespoon butter• 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cubed<INS>‣ In a saucepan over medium heat, combine rice, water and instant rice. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken, and cook until lightly browned, about 5 minutes per side.‣ Transfer chicken to a 9x13 inch baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until cheese is melted and chicken is heated through.
