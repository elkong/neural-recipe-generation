Pressure Cooker Italian Chicken Soup<ING>• 2 cups chicken broth• 1 cup half-and-half• 1 tablespoon garlic powder• 1/2 teaspoon dried basil• 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley• 1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaves• 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary• 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin• 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/4 cup olive oil• 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 2 cups frozen broccoli florets• 1 cup chopped fresh spinach• 1/2 cup sliced green onions<INS>‣ In a pressure cooker, mix the chicken broth, half-and-half, garlic powder, basil, oregano, thyme, pepper, parsley, basil, rosemary, thyme, cumin, oregano, salt, olive oil, and grated Parmesan cheese.‣ Place the broccoli in the cooker and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours.‣ Remove broccoli from the pressure cooker, discard bay leaves, and cook on High for 3 to 4 hours.‣ Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir the broccoli until tender and crisp, about 5 minutes. Stir in the spinach, and cook until wilted, about 5 minutes. Season with green onions and cumin to taste.
