Turtles® Cheesecake<ING>• 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract• 2 cups crushed graham cracker crumbs• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened• 1/2 cup butter, softened• 2 eggs• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 cup frozen whipped topping, thawed• 2 (8 ounce) packages semisweet chocolate chips• 3/4 cup confectioners' sugar<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).‣ In a medium bowl, cream together cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Stir in vanilla and graham cracker crumbs. Stir in cream cheese mixture and butter until well blended. Spread into bottom and sides of 9 inch springform pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow to cool.‣ In a large bowl, beat together the whipped topping, chocolate chips and confectioners' sugar until smooth. Spread over cooled crust.
