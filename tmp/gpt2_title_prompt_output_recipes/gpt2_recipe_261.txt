Shredded Barbeque Chicken<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1/4 cup chopped onion• 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper• 1/2 cup chopped garlic• 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce• 1 teaspoon dried thyme• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves<INS>‣ Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute onion and bell pepper until tender. Stir in garlic, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, pepper, and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.‣ Place chicken in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover with remaining ingredients, and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
