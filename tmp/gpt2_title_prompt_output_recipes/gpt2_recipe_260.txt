Lasagna Helper<ING>• 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles• 1/2 cup butter• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour• 2 cups milk• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup• 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder• 1/8 teaspoon salt• 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper<INS>‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles, and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes; drain.‣ Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour until smooth. Stir in milk, condensed soup, shredded mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper; cook, stirring frequently, until smooth.‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a 9x13-inch baking dish with aluminum foil.‣ Arrange noodles in the prepared baking dish. Pour sauce over noodles; top with mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.‣ Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted, about 20 minutes.
