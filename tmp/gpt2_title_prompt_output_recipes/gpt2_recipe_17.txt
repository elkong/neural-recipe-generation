Raspberry Split Seconds Cake<ING>• 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 cup butter• 3/4 cup packed brown sugar• 3/4 cup white sugar• 1 egg• 1 cup milk• 1 cup raspberry preserves• 1 cup chopped pecans<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9 inch Bundt pan. Sift together the flour, white sugar, salt, baking soda and cinnamon. Set aside.‣ Cream together butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add egg and milk. Beat on low speed for 1 minute. Add flour mixture alternately with raspberry preserves and mix until just combined.‣ Pour batter into Bundt pan. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.‣ To make the glaze: In a large bowl, mix together preserves, pecans and nuts. Mix well and pour over warm cake.
