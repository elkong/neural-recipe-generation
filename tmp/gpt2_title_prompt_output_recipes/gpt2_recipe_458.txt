Homemade Chorizo<ING>• 2 cups uncooked regular long grain white rice• 1/2 cup water• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour• 2 cups chorizo sausage, sliced into 1/2 inch pieces• 1/2 cup chopped onion• 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce• 1/2 cup brown sugar• 1 tablespoon butter• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon chili powder• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin• 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper<INS>‣ Heat the water in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in the rice and stir until the rice is coated in flour. Place the chorizo into the saucepan and cook for 5 minutes.‣ Stir the flour into the rice mixture, stirring constantly. Cook and stir until the flour is smooth. Season with salt, chili powder, cumin, coriander, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until the chorizo is tender, and the sauce is thickened, about 30 minutes.
