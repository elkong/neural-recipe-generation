Homemade Barbecue Sauce<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon onion powder• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 cups red or green beans, rinsed and drained• 1 (15 ounce) can stewed tomatoes• 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce• 1/4 cup cider vinegar• 1/2 cup water• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper<INS>‣ Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the garlic and onion powder, and cook for a few minutes until fragrant. Mix in the ground black pepper and red beans, and stir to coat.‣ Pour in the stewed tomatoes, tomato sauce, vinegar, and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, and simmer until the sauce thickens, about 30 minutes.
