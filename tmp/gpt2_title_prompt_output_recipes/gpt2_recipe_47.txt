Peanut Butter Fudge Cake III<ING>• 1 1/4 cups white sugar• 1 cup butter• 1/2 cup peanut butter• 1 egg• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips• 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the sugar and butter. Beat in the egg, vanilla and peanut butter. Stir in the vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg; stir into the creamed mixture. Stir in the chocolate chips. Pour batter into prepared pan.‣ Bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
