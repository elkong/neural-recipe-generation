Gnocchi Bake<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1/4 cup grated Romano cheese• 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ In a medium bowl, combine the spinach, parmesan cheese, Romano cheese, 1 cup Parmesan cheese, and mozzarella cheese; mix well. Spoon mixture into prepared baking dish and sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese and remaining mozzarella cheese.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
