Frosted Corn Flake Cereal Clusters<ING>• 2 cups whole wheat flour• 2 cups whole wheat flour• 1 cup all-purpose flour• 1 cup shortening• 1/4 cup water<INS>‣ In a large bowl, mix together whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, and shortening. Stir in water. Gradually stir in flour mixture until dough comes together. Roll out dough to 1/4 inch thickness. Cut into desired shapes using cookie cutters. Place onto waxed paper. Store in airtight container.
