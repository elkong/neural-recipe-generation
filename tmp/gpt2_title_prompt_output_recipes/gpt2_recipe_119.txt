Barquillos (Wafer Rolls)<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 cup butter• 2 cups white sugar• 2 eggs• 1 cup buttermilk• 2 tablespoons vegetable oil• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1/4 cup chopped pecans<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.‣ Sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, cinnamon, and ground cloves. Set aside.‣ In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the buttermilk, oil, vinegar, and vanilla. Beat until smooth. Pour batter into prepared pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.‣ To make the pecans: in a small bowl combine the pecans, sugar, and vinegar. Mix well. Stir in the nuts, and drizzle over cooled cake.
