Savory Stuffed Chicken Breast with Plum Glaze<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper• 1 (6 ounce) can tomato sauce• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup• 1 cup chicken broth• 1/4 cup lemon juice• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro• 2 tablespoons butter• 2 teaspoons minced fresh rosemary• salt and pepper to taste• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley• 1/4 cup chopped fresh oregano• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil<INS>‣ Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the garlic and green pepper; cook and stir until the garlic is soft and beginning to turn golden, about 5 minutes. Mix in tomato sauce and cream of chicken soup. Bring to a simmer, and cook until thickened, about 10 minutes.‣ Pour the chicken broth into the skillet, and stir in lemon juice, cilantro, butter, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Simmer until the sauce has thickened, about 5 minutes more.‣ To serve, sprinkle the rosemary mixture on the chicken breasts, and top with parsley, oregano, and basil.
