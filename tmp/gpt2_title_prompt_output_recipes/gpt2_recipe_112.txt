Neapolitan-Style Pizza Dough with Garlic and Italian Seasonings<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano• 2 cups milk• 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, salt, pepper, oregano and milk. Mix in cheese, 1/4 cup Parmesan and 1/4 cup grated Parmesan. Press mixture into the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle the remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan on top of the dough, and sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan.‣ Bake 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.
