Cheesy Vegetable Chowder  <ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of potato soup• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese• 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese<INS>‣ Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Cook and stir onion until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in hash browns and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Mix in condensed cream of potato soup and cream of celery soup; continue cooking until cheese is melted and mixture is heated through.‣ Pour soup mixture into a 10-quart slow cooker. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over soup mixture and top with Monterey Jack cheese.‣ Cook on Low until cheese is melted and mixture is hot and bubbly, 6 to 8 hours.
