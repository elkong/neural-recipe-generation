Salsa Cheese Boule Dip<ING>• 1 (16 ounce) package tri-colored tortilla chips• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened• 1/2 cup mayonnaise• 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream• 1 cup chopped celery• 1 (8 ounce) jar salsa<INS>‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the cream cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream and celery.‣ Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly into the tortilla chips. Top with a dollop of sour cream. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
