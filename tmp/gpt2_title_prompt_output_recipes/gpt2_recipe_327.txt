Million Monster Cookies<ING>• 2 cups butter, softened• 2 cups white sugar• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar• 1 egg• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Cream together butter, brown sugar, white sugar, egg and vanilla. Stir in flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, allspice and cloves. Stir into creamed mixture.‣ Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 8 to 10 minutes.
