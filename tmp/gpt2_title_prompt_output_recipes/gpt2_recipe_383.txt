Angel Hair Pasta Salad<ING>• 1 (16 ounce) package angel hair pasta• 1/2 cup butter• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour• 2 cups white sugar• 2 teaspoons salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 cup pitted and chopped pecans• 1/4 cup chopped green onions• 1/4 cup chopped celery• 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper• 1/4 cup chopped celery<INS>‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ In a medium bowl, cream together the butter and flour. Mix in the sugar, salt, and pepper. Gradually mix in the chopped pecans, green onions, and celery. Toss with pasta until evenly coated. Sprinkle with the chopped pecans and green onion. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.‣ In a small bowl, combine the drained pasta, drained pasta sauce, and reserved pasta sauce; mix well. Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
