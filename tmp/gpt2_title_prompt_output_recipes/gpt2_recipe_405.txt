Meatball Cookies III<ING>• 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour• 1/2 cup brown sugar• 1/2 cup butter, softened• 1/2 cup shortening• 2 eggs• 1 1/2 cups milk• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.‣ In a medium bowl, cream together the shortening and white sugar. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the milk, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt. Mix in the flour mixture. Roll dough into walnut sized balls, and place 1 inch apart on the prepared cookie sheets.‣ Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
