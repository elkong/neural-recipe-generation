Nana's Apple Crisp<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 cup butter• 2 cups white sugar• 2 eggs• 1 cup buttermilk• 2 tablespoons melted butter• 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 2 quart baking dish.‣ Sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice and ginger. Set aside.‣ Cream together 2 cups sugar and 2 eggs. Add the flour mixture and mix until well blended. Add the buttermilk and stir well.‣ Pour into prepared baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for about 45 minutes. Remove from oven and sprinkle with melted butter and all purpose flour.‣ Bake for an additional 30 minutes or until topping is golden brown and an inserted wooden pick comes out clean.
