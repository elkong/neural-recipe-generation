Haldi Ka Doodh (Hot Turmeric Milk)<ING>• 2 cups uncooked basmati rice• 1 cup plain yogurt• 1 cup chopped green onions• 1/2 cup plain yogurt• 1/4 cup honey• 2 tablespoons molasses• 1 tablespoon red curry powder• 1 teaspoon ground cumin• 1 teaspoon ground coriander• 1 teaspoon ground turmeric• 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper• 2 teaspoons garam masala• 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1 tablespoon water• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon<INS>‣ Place rice, plain yogurt, green onions, and yogurt in a saucepan over medium heat, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.‣ In a small bowl, stir together honey, molasses, curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, cayenne pepper, garam masala, cayenne pepper, and salt. Bring to a boil, and reduce heat to low. Simmer until thickened, about 30 minutes.‣ Stir water, cinnamon, and salt together in a small bowl. Stir into rice mixture until evenly blended.
