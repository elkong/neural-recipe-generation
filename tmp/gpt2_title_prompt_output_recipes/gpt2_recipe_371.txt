Bronco Burger<ING>• 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs• 2 teaspoons seasoned salt• 1 teaspoon onion powder• 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder• 1/4 teaspoon onion powder• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme• 1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, cut into 1/2 inch slices• 1 pound beef top sirloin, cut into 1/2 inch slices• 2 tablespoons vegetable oil• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar• 1 teaspoon white sugar• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 cup mayonnaise• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese<INS>‣ Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ In a medium bowl, stir together the seasoned bread crumbs, seasoned salt, onion powder, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, thyme, and 1/4 teaspoon of the black pepper.‣ Spread bread crumbs on one side of each slice of beef. Layer both sides of beef slices in a single layer on a baking sheet. Top with slices of French bread. Drizzle vegetable oil over both sides of the sandwiches.‣ Bake for 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until French bread is golden brown and French bread is hot. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving.‣ While the sandwiches are baking, prepare the mustard. In a small bowl, stir together the mayonnaise, Cheddar cheese, and 1/2 the black pepper. Spread over each sandwich.
