Ellen's Buffalo Meatloaf<ING>• 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs• 2 teaspoons seasoned salt• 1 teaspoon garlic powder• 1/4 teaspoon onion powder• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 pound ground beef• 1 cup chopped onion• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/4 cup sour cream• 1/4 cup mayonnaise• 2 eggs• 1/4 cup milk• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the seasoned bread crumbs, seasoned salt, garlic powder, onion powder, pepper and ground beef. Press ground beef into the bottom and up the sides of a 9x5 inch loaf pan. In a separate bowl, mix together the onion, celery, 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese, sour cream, mayonnaise and eggs.‣ In a separate bowl, mix together the milk, 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese and the remaining 1/2 cup cheese. Pour the mixture over the beef in the loaf pan. Sprinkle remaining 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese over the top and spread evenly.‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes, until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
