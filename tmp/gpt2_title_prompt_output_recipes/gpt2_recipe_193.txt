Grilled Pork Tenderloin<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon onion powder• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 (1 1/2 pound) pork tenderloin• 1/4 cup mayonnaise• 2 tablespoons white vinegar• 2 teaspoons soy sauce• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley<INS>‣ Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat.‣ Place olive oil, garlic, onion powder, 1 teaspoon ground black pepper, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; toss to coat.‣ Lightly oil the grill grate. Cook the tenderloin on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 7 to 10 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 145 degrees F (63 degrees C). Remove from the grill, discarding any excess marinade, and place on a cutting board.‣ Whisk mayonnaise, vinegar, soy sauce, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper in a bowl; stir to combine.‣ Grill pork tenderloin until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 5 to 8 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 145 degrees F (63 degrees C). Stir parsley into the seasoned pork.
