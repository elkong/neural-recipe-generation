Zucchini Alfredo<ING>• 2 cups grated zucchini• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour• 1/4 cup vegetable oil• 1/4 cup water• 3 cloves garlic, minced• 1 1/2 teaspoons paprika• 1 1/2 teaspoons ground black pepper• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano• 2 teaspoons dried basil• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).‣ In a large bowl, combine zucchini, flour, oil, water, garlic, paprika, black pepper, cayenne pepper, oregano, basil, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Transfer to a 2 quart baking dish.‣ Bake 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and golden brown. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
