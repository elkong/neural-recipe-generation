Baked Kielbasa and Potatoes in Sauce<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound potatoes, peeled and cubed• 1 pound kielbasa sausage, sliced• 3 cloves garlic, minced• 1 1/2 cups chicken broth• 1 cup chicken broth• 1 1/2 teaspoons salt• 1/2 teaspoon black pepper• 2 tablespoons butter• 2 teaspoons minced fresh rosemary• 2 teaspoons minced fresh thyme• 1 teaspoon lemon juice<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion; cook and stir until onion is soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in potatoes and kielbasa; cook and stir until potatoes are lightly browned, about 5 more minutes.‣ Pour chicken broth into skillet; bring to a boil. Season with salt, pepper, and butter; simmer until potatoes are tender, about 20 minutes.‣ Melt 2 tablespoons butter in skillet. Stir in rosemary, thyme, and lemon juice; cook, stirring frequently, until lemon juice is melted, about 5 minutes.‣ Pour sauce over potatoes and kielbasa and stir to coat. Sprinkle with salt and pepper; cover and simmer until potatoes are heated through, about 5 minutes.
