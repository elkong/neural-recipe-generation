Pork Chops with Creamy Scalloped Potatoes<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound boneless pork loin chops• salt and pepper to taste• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup• 1/2 cup water• 1/2 cup butter• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/4 cup sour cream<INS>‣ Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute onion until tender. Stir in pork chops and cook until lightly browned. Season with salt and pepper.‣ Stir soup, water, butter and cheese into skillet and cook until butter is melted. Mix in sour cream.‣ Serve pork chops with cream sauce on the side.
