Pina Colada Pie<ING>• 1 (9 inch) pie shell• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour• 1/4 cup white sugar• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1 cup white sugar• 1/4 cup light corn syrup• 1/2 cup butter, softened• 1 egg• 1/4 cup water<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ In a large bowl, mix flour, white sugar, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves. Cut in sugar slowly until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in corn syrup, butter, egg, and water. Mix until smooth. Pour into the pie shell.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until set and golden brown.
