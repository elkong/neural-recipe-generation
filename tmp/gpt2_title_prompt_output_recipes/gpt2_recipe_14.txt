Italian Chicken Marinade<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes• 1 (16 ounce) package angel hair pasta• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup• 1 cup milk• 1 (8 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers• 1/4 cup butter• 1/2 cup chopped onion• salt and pepper to taste<INS>‣ Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic and pepper. Saute until tender. Stir in pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes. Add soup and milk. Simmer, covered, for about 10 minutes.‣ Stir in tomatoes, and cook for 5 minutes. Stir in butter and onions. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
