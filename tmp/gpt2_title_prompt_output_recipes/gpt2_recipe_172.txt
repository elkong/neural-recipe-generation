Simple Salmon with Balsamic Sauce  <ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon sesame oil• 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice• 2 cups fresh or frozen crabmeat• 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar• 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar• 1/4 cup lemon juice• 2 tablespoons white sugar• 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root• 2 tablespoons minced garlic• 1 teaspoon sesame oil• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar• 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar• 1/4 cup lemon juice• 1/4 teaspoon ginger root<INS>‣ In a small bowl, whisk together 2 tablespoons olive oil, garlic, and sesame oil. Stir in crabmeat, and cook until it starts to brown, but is not done, about 5 minutes. Set aside.‣ In a large bowl, toss together 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, sugar, and ginger. Set aside.‣ In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, 1/4 teaspoon ginger, and garlic. Stir in sesame oil, and simmer until tender, about 5 minutes. Add 2 tablespoons olive oil, and cook until hot, about 1 minute.‣ Add crabmeat to sauce, and cook for 3 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat, and garnish with remaining 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar, and serve.
