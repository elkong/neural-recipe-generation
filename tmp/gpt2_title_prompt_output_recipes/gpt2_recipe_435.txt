Blueberry Oat Muffins<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/4 cup rolled oats• 1/2 cup brown sugar• 1/4 cup white sugar• 1 egg• 1/2 cup buttermilk• 1/4 cup buttermilk• 2 tablespoons butter, melted<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease 12 muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.‣ Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Set aside.‣ In a large bowl, cream together brown sugar, white sugar, egg, and buttermilk. Beat in the dry ingredients until just blended. Stir in the melted butter until just blended. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.‣ Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
