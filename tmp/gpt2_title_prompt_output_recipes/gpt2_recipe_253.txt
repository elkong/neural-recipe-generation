Donna's Famous Cheesecake<ING>• 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract• 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips• 2 cups graham cracker crumbs<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ In a medium bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Mix in sugar and vanilla. Add chocolate chips and graham cracker crumbs. Stir until well combined. Pour mixture into a 9x13 inch pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes.
