Banana Yogurt Muffins<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/4 cup white sugar• 1/2 cup shortening• 1 cup yogurt• 1 egg• 1 cup chopped walnuts• 1 cup mashed ripe bananas<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners. Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Set aside.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the shortening and yogurt. Beat in the egg and nuts. Stir in the flour mixture. Stir in the banana mixture. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
