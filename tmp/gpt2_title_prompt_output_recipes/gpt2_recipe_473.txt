Baked Ham<ING>• 2 cups cooked, cubed ham• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour• 1/4 cup vegetable oil• 1/4 cup water• 3/4 cup brown sugar• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the ham, flour, vegetable oil, water, brown sugar, and cinnamon. Cut in the ham until all pieces are evenly coated. Place a layer of the mixture on a baking sheet, and bake for 5 minutes in the preheated oven, or until lightly browned.
