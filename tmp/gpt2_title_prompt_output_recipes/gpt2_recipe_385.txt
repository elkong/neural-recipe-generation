Aush (Afghani Chili)<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound ground beef• 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed• 2 cups water• 1 teaspoon ground coriander• 1 teaspoon ground cumin• 1 teaspoon ground turmeric• 1 teaspoon garam masala• 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper• 2 teaspoons ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1 tablespoon water• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon<INS>‣ Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Saute onion until tender, and remove from heat.‣ Place ground beef into the pot, and cook until evenly browned. Drain, and mix in garbanzo beans, 2 cups of water, coriander, cumin, turmeric, garam masala, cayenne pepper, ground ginger, cayenne pepper, salt, and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until meat is tender, about 1 hour.‣ Stir cinnamon into the chili, and continue simmering until thickened, about 1 hour more.
