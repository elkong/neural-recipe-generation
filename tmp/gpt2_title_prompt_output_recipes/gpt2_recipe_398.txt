Coconut Cake I<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1/2 cup butter• 1 egg• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1/4 cup sour cream• 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)• 1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix• 2 cups flaked coconut• 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar for dusting<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.‣ Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and sugar. Set aside.‣ Beat butter and egg together in a large bowl until fluffy. Beat in vanilla and sour cream. Add flour mixture alternately with the sour cream mixture, mixing just until incorporated. Stir in coconut and confectioners' sugar. Pour batter into prepared pans.‣ Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cake comes out clean, 40 to 50 minutes. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
