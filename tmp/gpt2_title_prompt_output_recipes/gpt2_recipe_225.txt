Spicy Rub for Seared Tuna Steaks<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes• 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms• 1 (2 pound) tuna steaks, skin on• 1 teaspoon lemon juice<INS>‣ Heat olive oil in a medium skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic until fragrant. Add red pepper flakes, mushrooms, and tuna steaks. Continue to cook and stir until heated through.‣ To serve, spoon about 1/2 cup of the sauce onto a serving plate. Top with lemon juice to serve.
