Zucchini Saute<ING>• 2 cups uncooked white rice• 2 1/2 cups water• 3 cups shredded zucchini• 1 cup shredded carrots• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1/2 cup chopped onion• 1 cup chopped celery• 3 cups zucchini, halved lengthwise and sliced• 3 tablespoons butter• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Bring the rice and water to a boil in a saucepan over high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender, and the liquid has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.‣ Meanwhile, cook the zucchini and carrots in a skillet over medium heat until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in the celery, onion, celery, and onion, and cook another 5 minutes.‣ Melt the butter in a small skillet over medium heat, and stir in the salt and black pepper. Cook and stir until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.‣ Pour the sauce over the rice mixture, and toss to coat.
