Spaghetti Soup III<ING>• 3 tablespoons olive oil• 2 onions, chopped• 1 pound ground beef• 1/2 pound ground pork• 1/2 pound mushrooms, chopped• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 cup chopped celery• 3 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup• 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes• 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste• 1/4 cup water• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder• 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes• 1 teaspoon dried basil• 1 teaspoon dried thyme• 1 teaspoon dried parsley• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/4 teaspoon dried basil leaves• 1/4 teaspoon dried sage• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/4 teaspoon ground thyme• 1 teaspoon dried parsley• 1/4 teaspoon oregano• 1/4 teaspoon dried basil leaves• 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder• 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes<INS>‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir onion in hot oil until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add ground beef, pork, mushrooms, salt, pepper, celery, cream of mushroom soup, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, water, oregano, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, basil, thyme, parsley, oregano, basil, sage, black pepper, thyme, parsley, oregano, basil, and garlic powder to the pan. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until flavors blend, about 15 minutes.‣ Stir basil, thyme, parsley, and oregano into the soup, and continue simmering until flavors have blended, about 15 minutes more.
