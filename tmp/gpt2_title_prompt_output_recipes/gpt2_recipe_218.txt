Mexican Strawberry Water (Agua de Fresa)<ING>• 2 cups water• 1 1/4 cups sugar• 1/2 cup orange juice• 1/2 cup lemon juice• 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled<INS>‣ In a saucepan, combine water, sugar, orange juice, lemon juice and strawberries. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until strawberries are tender, about 20 minutes.
