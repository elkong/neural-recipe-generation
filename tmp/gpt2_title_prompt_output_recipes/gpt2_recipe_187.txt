Zucchini-Cheese Appetizer Squares<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/2 cup butter, softened• 1 cup packed brown sugar• 1/4 cup chopped pecans• 2 eggs, beaten• 1/4 cup milk• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar for dusting<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.‣ Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice and allspice in a bowl. Set aside.‣ Cream together butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in milk and vanilla. Mix flour mixture into butter mixture alternately with the flour mixture.‣ Spoon batter into prepared baking pan. Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow to cool.‣ To make the glaze: In a medium bowl, combine 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar and 1/4 cup milk. Beat until smooth. Spread over cooled crust.
