Salad Oleander Dressing<ING>• 1 1/2 cups mayonnaise• 1/4 cup chopped green onions• 1/4 cup mayonnaise• 1 tablespoon lemon juice• 2 tablespoons prepared Dijon-style mustard• 1 teaspoon lemon zest• 1/4 teaspoon lemon juice<INS>‣ In a medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise, green onions, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and mustard. Fold in lemon zest and lemon juice. Chill for 1 hour before serving.
