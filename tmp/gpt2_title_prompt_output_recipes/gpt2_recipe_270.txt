Meringue I<ING>• 2 cups white sugar• 1 cup packed brown sugar• 1/2 cup butter• 1/4 cup vegetable oil• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 3 eggs• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon baking powder• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda• 1 cup white sugar• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract<INS>‣ In a large bowl, cream together the brown sugar, white sugar, and butter until light and fluffy. Beat in the oil, eggs, and salt. Combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and white sugar; stir into the creamed mixture. Stir in the vanilla. Cover, and chill in the refrigerator at least 1 hour.‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x8 inch pan.‣ Mix together the flour mixture, white sugar, and vanilla until just combined. Pour into the prepared pan.‣ Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
