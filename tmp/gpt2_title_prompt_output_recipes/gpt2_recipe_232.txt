Lemonade Pie V<ING>• 2 cups white sugar• 1 cup packed light brown sugar• 1 cup all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1 cup lemon juice• 1/4 cup water• 2 eggs• 1/2 cup vegetable oil• 1 teaspoon lemon juice<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ In a medium bowl, mix white sugar, brown sugar, flour, salt, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves. Mix in lemon juice, water, eggs, oil and lemon juice. Stir until well blended. Pour into an ungreased 9 inch pie pan.‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes.
