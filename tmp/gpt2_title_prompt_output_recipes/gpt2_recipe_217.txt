Buffalo Chex® Mix<ING>• 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs• 2 teaspoons seasoned salt• 1 teaspoon onion powder• 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder• 1/4 teaspoon onion powder• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened• 1 cup milk• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).‣ Mix together bread crumbs, seasoned salt, onion powder, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, thyme, cream cheese, and milk in a bowl. Mix until well blended. Spread mixture into an 8x8 inch baking dish.‣ Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 20 minutes.‣ Spread Cheddar cheese over bread crumb mixture. Return to oven until cheese is melted, about 5 more minutes. Remove from oven and sprinkle remaining 1/4 cup cheese over Cheddar cheese. Return to oven and continue baking until cheese is bubbly and lightly browned, about 5 more minutes.
