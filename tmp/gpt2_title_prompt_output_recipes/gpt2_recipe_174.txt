Grilled Salmon With Orange Glaze<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1/4 cup chopped fresh orange zest• 1 tablespoon orange juice• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 tablespoon ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 2 teaspoons orange juice• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 4 (6 ounce) salmon fillets• 1/2 cup orange glaze• 1/4 cup butter<INS>‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir garlic and orange zest until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add orange juice, pepper, and ground black pepper; cook and stir until the liquid is reduced by half, about 5 minutes. Season salmon with salt and pepper.‣ Pour the glaze over the salmon, then drizzle with the remaining orange glaze. Cook and stir until glaze is reduced by half, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand 5 minutes before serving.
