Roesti Cake III<ING>• 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/4 cup butter, softened• 1 cup white sugar• 1 egg• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 cup milk• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/2 cup chopped walnuts• 3/4 cup confectioners' sugar for dusting<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9 inch Bundt pan.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg, then stir in the vanilla and milk. Beat in the cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, allspice, and walnuts. Combine the flour, baking soda, salt and butter; stir into the creamed mixture alternately with the milk. Finally, fold in the confectioners' sugar. Pour batter into prepared pan.‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
