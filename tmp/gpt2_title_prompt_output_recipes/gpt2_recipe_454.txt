Grilled Soy-Sesame Asparagus<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon sesame oil• 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice• 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar• 1 teaspoon rice vinegar• 1 teaspoon cornstarch• 1/2 cup chicken broth• 1/4 cup fish sauce• 2 tablespoons white sugar• 2 teaspoons Asian (toasted) sesame seeds• 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root• 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro<INS>‣ Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, and stir in the garlic. Cook and stir until the garlic has softened and turned golden brown, about 5 minutes. Mix in the sesame oil, lemon juice, and red wine vinegar, and bring to a simmer over medium-high heat. Mix in rice vinegar, cornstarch, chicken broth, fish sauce, sugar, sesame seeds, and ginger until smooth. Reduce heat to low, and simmer until the fish is tender, about 15 minutes.‣ Mix in the cilantro, and serve.
