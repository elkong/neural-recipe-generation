Black Bean, Corn, and Tomato Salad with Feta Cheese<ING>• 2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained• 1/4 cup mayonnaise• 1/4 cup red wine vinegar• 1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill• 1 tablespoon olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 tablespoon minced fresh oregano• 1/2 teaspoon white sugar• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1/4 cup sliced black olives, for garnish• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro<INS>‣ Place black beans into a large bowl and cover with cold water; let sit for 5 minutes. Drain and rinse.‣ Stir mayonnaise, vinegar, dill, olive oil, garlic, oregano, sugar, salt, pepper, and olives in a bowl. Pour dressing over beans and toss to coat.‣ Toss to coat and serve.
