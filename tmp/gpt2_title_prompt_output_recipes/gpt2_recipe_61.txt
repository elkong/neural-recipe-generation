Apple Cinnamon White Cake<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 3/4 cup shortening• 1 cup white sugar• 1 egg• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 cup applesauce• 1 cup brown sugar• 2 tablespoons butter• 1 cup chopped pecans<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan. Sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and cloves. Set aside.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the shortening and white sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg, then stir in the vanilla. Combine the flour mixture and the applesauce; stir into the creamed mixture.‣ Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake for 60 to 70 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.‣ To make the topping: In a medium saucepan, combine brown sugar, butter, and pecans. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently until thickened.
