Salmon Chowder<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound fresh salmon, cut into bite-size pieces• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 3/4 cup chopped carrots• 3/4 cup chopped celery• 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper• 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 cups milk• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour• 1/4 cup butter, melted<INS>‣ Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the onion and salmon, and cook until the salmon is easily flaked with a fork. Season with 1/4 cup of flour and 1/2 cup of celery, and continue cooking until tender. Mix in the carrot, celery, and green bell pepper. Season with parsley, black pepper, and milk. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer for about 15 minutes.‣ Meanwhile, in a large saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Stir in the flour and 1/4 cup of celery. Cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens slightly. Remove from heat, and stir in the melted butter and milk. Stir in the salmon mixture. Simmer for 10 minutes, then serve.
