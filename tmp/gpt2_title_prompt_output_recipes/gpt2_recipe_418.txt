All-in-One Casserole<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/4 cup butter, melted• 1 pound ground beef• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup• 1 (10 ounce) can milk• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter. Stir in the ground beef, and cook until beef is crumbly. Drain off any excess grease, and stir in spinach. Mix in 1/4 cup of the mushroom soup, cream of mushroom soup, cream of celery soup and milk; heat through, but do not boil. Stir in 1 cup of shredded Cheddar cheese.‣ Bake uncovered for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
