Old Fashioned Oatmeal Cookies<ING>• 2 cups quick cooking oats• 1 cup chopped walnuts• 1 cup raisins• 1/2 cup margarine• 2 cups rolled oats• 1 cup water• 1 cup packed brown sugar• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda• 1 cup rolled oats• 1 cup buttermilk• 2 eggs<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.‣ In a large bowl, stir together the quick cooking oats, walnuts, raisins, margarine, rolled oats, water, brown sugar, cinnamon and baking soda. Mix in the rolled oats until well blended. Stir in the buttermilk and eggs. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto the prepared cookie sheets.‣ Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, until lightly browned. Cool on baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
