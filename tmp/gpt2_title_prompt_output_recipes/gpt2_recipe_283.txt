Slow Cooker Sweet and Tangy Chicken<ING>• 2 cups chicken broth• 1 cup water• 1 (2 pound) boneless chicken breast, cut into bite-size pieces• 1/2 cup soy sauce• 2 tablespoons honey• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 teaspoon garlic powder• 1 teaspoon ground cumin• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 cup brown sugar• 2 tablespoons butter• 2 teaspoons cornstarch• 1 teaspoon sesame oil• 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar• 1/4 cup sesame oil<INS>‣ In a slow cooker, combine chicken broth, water, chicken, soy sauce, honey, ground black pepper, garlic powder, cumin, salt, brown sugar, butter, cornstarch, sesame oil, vinegar and sesame oil.‣ Cover, and cook on Low setting for 4 to 6 hours.
