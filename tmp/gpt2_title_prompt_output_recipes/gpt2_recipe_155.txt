Broiled Lemon and Garlic Tiger Prawns<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon lemon juice• 1/4 cup vegetable oil• 1/4 cup water• 1 (6 ounce) can lemon, drained and juiced• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper<INS>‣ Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the garlic in the hot oil until fragrant, about 5 minutes. Stir in the lemon juice, vegetable oil, water, and lemon juice. Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until the water has been absorbed, about 10 minutes. Stir in the cilantro, salt, and pepper.
