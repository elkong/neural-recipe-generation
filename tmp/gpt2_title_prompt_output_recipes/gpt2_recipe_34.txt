Peanut Butter and Fudge Swirl Pie<ING>• 1 cup butter, softened• 1 cup white sugar• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened• 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust• 1/2 cup peanut butter• 1/4 cup margarine• 2 eggs• 1/4 cup sour cream<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9 inch pie pan.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar, brown sugar, and cream cheese until smooth. Beat in the sour cream and eggs one at a time, then stir in the peanut butter and margarine. Pour filling into the prepared crust.‣ Bake for 35 to 40 minutes in the preheated oven, until filling is set. Allow pie to cool before cutting into squares.
