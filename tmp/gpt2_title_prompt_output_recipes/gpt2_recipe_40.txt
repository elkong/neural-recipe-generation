Cabbage-Carrot Casserole<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded sharp Cheddar cheese• 1 cup shredded carrots• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup• 1/4 cup butter• 1/2 cup chopped onion• 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.‣ Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add broccoli, and cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Drain, and set aside. In a bowl, mix the broccoli, Cheddar cheese, carrots, celery, soup, and butter until thoroughly combined. Spread the mixture into the prepared baking dish, and top with onion, green bell pepper, and 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese.‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
