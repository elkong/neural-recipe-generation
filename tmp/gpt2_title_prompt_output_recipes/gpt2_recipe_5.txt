Spicy Potatoes and Scrambled Eggs<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large yellow onion, sliced• 1 large green bell pepper, seeded and sliced• 1 (8 ounce) package baby bella (plum) tomatoes• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers• 1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained• 1/4 cup sliced fresh mushrooms• 1/4 cup sliced black olives, drained• 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper• 1 cup sliced white mushrooms• 1 cup chopped fresh parsley• 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese<INS>‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in onion, green pepper, baby bella, tomatoes, salt, and ground black pepper. Cook and stir until vegetables are soft and transparent, about 5 minutes.‣ Stir diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, black olives, mushrooms, and black olives into vegetables; cook and stir until vegetables are softened, about 3 more minutes.‣ Stir parsley into vegetables; cook and stir until parsley is soft, about 3 more minutes.‣ Transfer vegetables to a large bowl.‣ Top vegetables with feta cheese.
