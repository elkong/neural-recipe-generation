Garlic Ginger Chops<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root• 1/2 cup soy sauce• 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil• 1/2 teaspoon curry powder• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves• 1/4 cup red wine• 1/4 cup fresh lime juice• 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger root<INS>‣ Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).‣ Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in the garlic, ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil, curry powder, and pepper; cook and stir until the garlic has softened, and the flavors have blended, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat, and set aside.‣ Place the chicken breasts into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Pour the red wine, lime juice, and ginger into the skillet, and stir to distribute the flavors. Cover, and simmer for 20 minutes, or until chicken is no longer pink in the center.
