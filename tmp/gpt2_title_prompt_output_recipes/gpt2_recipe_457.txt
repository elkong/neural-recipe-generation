Ray's Chicken and Dumplings<ING>• 1 1/2 cups chicken stock• 1/2 cup frozen chopped broccoli, thawed• 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent roll dough• 1 tablespoon butter, softened• 1 cup chopped celery• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup• 1/4 cup milk• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon dried thyme• 1 pinch dried basil• 1 pinch dried oregano• 1 dash hot pepper sauce• 1 tablespoon butter, melted<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).‣ Mix together chicken stock, broccoli, and crescent roll dough. In a small bowl, combine celery, cream of chicken soup, milk, salt, thyme, basil, oregano, hot pepper sauce, butter, and chicken. Pour into a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and browned.
