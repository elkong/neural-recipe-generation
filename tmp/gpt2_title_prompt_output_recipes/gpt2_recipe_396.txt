Texas German Potato Salad<ING>• 2 cups mashed potatoes• 1 cup chopped celery• 1/2 cup chopped onion• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 3 tablespoons cider vinegar• 1/4 cup white sugar• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin• salt and pepper to taste<INS>‣ In a large bowl, combine the mashed potatoes, celery, onion, celery and vinegar.
