Green Bean Bundles II<ING>• 2 cups frozen green beans• 2 cups chopped kale• 1/2 cup frozen chopped spinach• 1 (16 ounce) package corn muffin mix<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.‣ Mix green beans, kale, spinach, and corn muffin mix in a large bowl. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.‣ Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, about 20 minutes.
