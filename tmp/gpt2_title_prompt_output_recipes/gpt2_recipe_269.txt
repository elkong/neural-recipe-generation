Spinach and Feta Pita Bake<ING>• 2 (16 ounce) packages fettuccine pasta• 1 cup feta cheese• 1/2 cup olive oil• 2 cups fresh spinach, washed and dried• salt and pepper to taste<INS>‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil and saute feta until golden brown. Mix spinach and salt and pepper into spinach mixture. Sprinkle with feta cheese and serve.
