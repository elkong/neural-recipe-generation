Jalapeno Garlic Tilapia Pasta<ING>• 2 (16 ounce) tilapia fillets• 1 tablespoon olive oil• 1 tablespoon minced fresh garlic• 1 tablespoon lime juice• salt and pepper to taste<INS>‣ Place the tilapia fillets in a medium skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning once, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain the fillets on paper towels and set aside.‣ In a large bowl, toss the olive oil, garlic, lime juice, salt and pepper. Whisk in the tilapia until coated.
