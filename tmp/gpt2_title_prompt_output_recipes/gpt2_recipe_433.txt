Andy's Spicy Green Chile Pork<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon onion powder• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1 (2 pound) boneless pork loin roast• 1 cup chopped celery• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup• 1/4 cup water• 1/2 cup sour cream• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro• 2 teaspoons salt• 1/2 cup uncooked white rice• 1/2 cup tomato sauce<INS>‣ Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic, onion powder, and pepper; cook and stir until garlic is fragrant, about 2 minutes. Stir in pork and celery. Reduce heat, cover skillet, and simmer until pork is tender, about 20 minutes.‣ Pour in cream of mushroom soup, cream of celery soup, water, sour cream, parsley, cilantro, and cilantro. Bring to a simmer, and cook for about 5 minutes. Stir in salt, rice, tomato sauce, and 1/4 cup water. Simmer for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat, and garnish with parsley and cilantro.
