Creamy Spinach Soup<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound fresh spinach, chopped• 1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced• 1 clove garlic, minced• 3 tablespoons soy sauce• 3 tablespoons white sugar• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 teaspoon dried oregano• 1 teaspoon ground cumin• 2 bay leaves• 2 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley• 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger• 2 teaspoons chopped fresh sage<INS>‣ Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Cook the onion in the hot oil until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the spinach and mushrooms; cook and stir until the spinach wilts, about 5 more minutes. Stir in garlic, soy sauce, sugar, black pepper, oregano, cumin, and bay leaves; cook and stir until the sauce thickens, about 2 minutes. Add the parsley, ginger, and sage; cook and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
