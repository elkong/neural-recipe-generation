Mahi Mahi Rockefeller<ING>• 2 cups uncooked white rice• 2 1/2 cups water• 3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese• 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese• 1/4 cup chopped onion• 3 tablespoons soy sauce• 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper• 1 teaspoon ground cumin• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander• 1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper• 1/4 teaspoon paprika• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves<INS>‣ In a saucepan, bring rice and water to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.‣ In a medium saucepan, cook Cheddar cheese and Swiss cheese until melted and smooth. Stir in onion, soy sauce, green pepper, cumin, salt, black pepper, coriander, cayenne pepper and paprika. Simmer until cheese is melted.‣ In a small bowl, combine olive oil, chicken and remaining 1/2 cup cheese mixture.‣ Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook chicken in oil until no longer pink in center and juices run clear. Remove chicken to a platter and keep warm.‣ Toss the rice, chicken, 2 tablespoons cheese, onions and remaining 1/2 cup cheese mixture together in a large bowl until well coated.
