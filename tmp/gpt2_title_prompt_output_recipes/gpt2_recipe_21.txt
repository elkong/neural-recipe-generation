Banana Cookies III<ING>• 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 cup butter, softened• 1/2 cup brown sugar• 2 eggs• 1 cup mashed bananas• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ In a large bowl, sift together flour, sugar, salt, baking soda and cinnamon. In a separate bowl, cream butter and brown sugar together until smooth. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in mashed bananas.‣ Combine flour mixture with butter mixture; stir just until moistened. Pour into the greased and floured 12x8 inch baking pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until set. Remove from oven; sprinkle with vanilla and continue baking for 5 minutes.
