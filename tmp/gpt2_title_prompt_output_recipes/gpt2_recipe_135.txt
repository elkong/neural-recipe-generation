Aunt Jewel's Chicken Dressing Casserole<ING>• 2 cups mayonnaise• 2 cups sour cream• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/2 cup diced onion• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup• 1/2 cup milk• 1/4 teaspoon celery seed• 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme• salt and pepper to taste• 1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained• 1 (8 ounce) package uncooked rotini pasta<INS>‣ In a large bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, Cheddar cheese, onion, cream of chicken soup, milk, celery seed, thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix together and pour into a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 45 minutes, or until bubbly and lightly browned.
