Savory Swiss Chard with Portobellos<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1/4 cup chopped portobello mushrooms• 1 (8 ounce) package elbow macaroni• 2 cups portobello mushroom stems• 1 cup chopped celery• 1 (8 ounce) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed• 1/2 cup olive oil• salt and pepper to taste• 1 (16 ounce) package angel hair pasta• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley<INS>‣ Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add portobello mushrooms, macaroni, and the remaining 1/2 cup of mushroom stems and cook until heated through, about 5 minutes. Stir in celery, and cook until the celery softens, about 3 minutes.‣ Stir in beans, olive oil, salt, and pepper, and continue cooking until heated through, about 5 minutes.‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook angel hair pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain.‣ Toss pasta with the pasta mixture and serve immediately. Garnish with Parmesan cheese and parsley to serve.
