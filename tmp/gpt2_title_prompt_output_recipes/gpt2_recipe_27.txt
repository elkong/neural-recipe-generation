BBQ Sauce for the Slow Cooker<ING>• 1 cup barbecue sauce• 1/2 cup ketchup• 1/4 cup brown sugar• 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce• 1/4 cup vinegar• 1/4 cup water<INS>‣ In a medium bowl, stir together the barbecue sauce, ketchup, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, vinegar and water. Mix well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
