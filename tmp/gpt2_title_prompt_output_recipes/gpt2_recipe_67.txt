Potato Pancakes I<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/2 cup vegetable oil• 1/4 cup brown sugar• 2 eggs• 1/2 cup milk• 1 cup shredded cabbage• 1/2 cup chopped onion• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1/2 cup chopped carrots• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1/2 cup chopped green onions<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, ginger, and cloves. Set aside.‣ In a medium bowl, mix together vegetable oil, brown sugar, eggs, milk, cabbage, onion, celery, carrots, and celery. Pour this mixture into the flour mixture and stir until just combined.‣ Bake for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the pancake comes out clean. Allow to cool.‣ To make the Filling: In a small bowl, stir together the onion, celery, and carrots.‣ In a large bowl, stir together the green and green onions. Pour over the cooked pancakes.
