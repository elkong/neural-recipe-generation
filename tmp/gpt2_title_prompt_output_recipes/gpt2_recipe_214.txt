Bread Pudding II<ING>• 2 cups white sugar• 1 cup butter• 1 egg• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1 cup milk• 1/4 cup chopped walnuts<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Cream sugar and butter together until light and fluffy.‣ Beat egg, vanilla and flour together.‣ Sift together the baking powder and salt.‣ Add milk, nuts and butter.‣ Roll dough into 1 inch balls and place in greased 9x5 inch loaf pan.‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
