Mock Lobster Dip<ING>• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened• 1 cup mayonnaise• 1/2 cup lemon juice• 2 cups sour cream• 1 (1 pound) lobster tail, peeled and deveined• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the cream cheese, mayonnaise and lemon juice. Blend in the sour cream, lobster tail and Cheddar cheese. Spread the mixture onto a baking sheet.‣ Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and lightly browned.
