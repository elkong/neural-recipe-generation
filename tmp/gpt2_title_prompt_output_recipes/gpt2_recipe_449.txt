Chicken Catch a Cola Cake<ING>• 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/4 cup vegetable oil• 1/4 cup water• 3/4 cup cornstarch• 1 tablespoon lemon juice• 1 teaspoon lemon zest• 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon zest• 1 cup chicken broth• 2 eggs• 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce• 1/4 cup water• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar• 1 teaspoon white sugar• 1/4 cup margarine• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1/4 cup brown sugar• 1/4 cup butter, softened• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 egg, beaten• 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch pan.‣ In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, vegetable oil, and water. Mix in cornstarch, lemon juice, lemon zest, and 1 cup chicken broth. Stir in eggs, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, 1/4 cup water, baking soda, and vinegar. Blend until smooth.‣ Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Remove from oven, and cool 10 minutes.‣ To make the glaze: In a small saucepan, combine 1/4 cup brown sugar, 1/4 cup butter, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and egg. Bring to a boil, and stir in confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth. Pour glaze over cooled cake.
