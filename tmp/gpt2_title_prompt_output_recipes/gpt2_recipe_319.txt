Microwave Bread and Butter Pickles<ING>• 2 cups white sugar• 1 cup packed brown sugar• 1/2 cup vegetable oil• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1 egg• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar• 1 cup chopped pickling cucumbers• 1/4 cup chopped pecans<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.‣ In a large mixing bowl, combine sugar, brown sugar, vegetable oil, salt, nutmeg, egg, vinegar and pickling cucumbers. Mix well. Stir in pecans. Pour mixture into prepared loaf pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour, or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
