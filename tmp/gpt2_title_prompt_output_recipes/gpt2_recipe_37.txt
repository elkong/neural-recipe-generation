Christmas Stollen Cake<ING>• 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 cup butter• 3/4 cup packed brown sugar• 3/4 cup white sugar• 1 egg• 1/3 cup water• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1/2 cup chopped walnuts• 1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix• 2 cups confectioners' sugar• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.‣ In a large bowl, combine flour, 1/2 cup sugar, salt and baking soda. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Mix in brown sugar and 3/4 cup sugar until well combined. Beat egg and water into flour mixture alternately with the vanilla. Stir in the walnuts and cake mix. Pour batter into prepared pan.‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 60 to 75 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.‣ To make the glaze: In a medium bowl, combine 1 cup confectioners' sugar, 1 teaspoon vanilla and 1/2 cup chopped nuts. Whip until smooth, then sprinkle over cooled cake.
