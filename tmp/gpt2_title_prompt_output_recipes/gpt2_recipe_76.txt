Pumpkin Chip Muffins<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/4 cup white sugar• 1/2 cup shortening• 1 cup canned pumpkin• 1 egg• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 cup buttermilk• 2/3 cup brown sugar• 2 teaspoons vanilla extract• 2 cups confectioners' sugar<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 12 muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.‣ Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, white sugar, shortening, pumpkin, egg, cinnamon, nutmeg and buttermilk together in a bowl.‣ Stir brown sugar, vanilla extract, and confectioners' sugar together in a separate bowl; stir wet ingredients into wet ingredients. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full.‣ Bake in the preheated oven until muffins are lightly browned and a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, 18 to 22 minutes.
