Tomato Soup III<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound fresh mushrooms, chopped• 1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced• 1 clove garlic, minced• 3 tablespoons soy sauce• 3 tablespoons sherry• 1 teaspoon sesame oil• 1 teaspoon sesame oil• 2 teaspoons white sugar• 2 teaspoons red wine vinegar• 2 teaspoons cornstarch• 1 teaspoon sesame oil• 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar• 1/4 teaspoon sesame seeds<INS>‣ Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and mushrooms; cook and stir until the onion is tender, about 5 minutes.‣ Stir in garlic, soy sauce, sherry, sesame oil, sesame oil, sugar, and red wine vinegar. Cook and stir until the sugar has dissolved, about 1 minute. Remove from heat and stir in cornstarch and sesame oil.‣ Whisk cornstarch mixture with the reserved onion mixture and vinegar into the pot. Reduce heat to low and simmer until thickened, about 30 minutes.‣ Stir in distilled white vinegar and sesame seeds and simmer until the sauce is thick, about 10 minutes more.
