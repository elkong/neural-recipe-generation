Eggnog French Toast<ING>• 2 cups eggnog• 2 cups milk• 1/2 cup butter• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8x8 inch baking pan.‣ In a medium saucepan, combine eggnog, milk and butter or margarine. Cook over medium heat until mixture boils. Stir in cinnamon. Pour into prepared pan.‣ Bake for 45 minutes.
