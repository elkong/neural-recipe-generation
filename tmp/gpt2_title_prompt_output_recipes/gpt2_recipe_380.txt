Hummus I<ING>• 2 cups uncooked white rice• 2 1/2 cups water• 3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese• 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese• 1/4 cup honey• 2 tablespoons molasses• 1 tablespoon olive oil• 1 teaspoon lemon juice• 1 teaspoon ground cumin• salt and pepper to taste<INS>‣ In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the rice and water. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes, or until water is absorbed.‣ In a large bowl, mix together the rice, Cheddar cheese, Swiss cheese, honey, molasses, olive oil, lemon juice, cumin, salt and pepper. Refrigerate until chilled, then serve.
