Steak on a Stick<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon onion powder• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 pounds red or green bell pepper, chopped• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup• 1 1/2 cups milk• 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour• 2 tablespoons butter• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper<INS>‣ Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the garlic, onion powder, and ground black pepper, and cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the red bell pepper, condensed chicken soup, and milk. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until the mixture is thickened, about 5 minutes.‣ Melt the butter in the skillet, and season with salt and oregano. Stir in the onion mixture, and cook until the onion has softened, about 5 minutes. Mix in the flour, and cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens, about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.
