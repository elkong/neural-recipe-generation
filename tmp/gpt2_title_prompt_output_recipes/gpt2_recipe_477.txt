The Arnold Palmer® Cake<ING>• 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/4 cup butter, softened• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1 egg• 1 cup milk• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch tube pan. Sift together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg, then stir in the milk, vanilla and chocolate chips. Beat for 3 minutes on medium speed. Pour batter into prepared pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 55 to 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
