Chicken Alfredo Pizza<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 2 cloves garlic, minced• 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1 cup fresh basil, chopped• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese• 1 cup fresh basil, chopped<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a pizza pan.‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ Meanwhile, cook spinach in a large pot of boiling salted water until wilted and tender, about 10 minutes; drain and transfer to a large bowl.‣ Stir spinach, Parmesan cheese, Cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese, 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, garlic, oregano, and pepper together in a bowl; pour mixture into a 2 quart casserole dish.‣ Bake in preheated oven until bubbly and lightly browned, about 20 minutes. Remove from oven; sprinkle with 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese and 1 cup fresh basil.‣ Return to oven and bake until sauce is reduced and sauce is browned, about 20 minutes more.
