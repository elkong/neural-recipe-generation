Amazing Peanut Butter Cup Biscotti<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 cup peanut butter• 1/4 cup water<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ In a large bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, allspice, ginger, cloves, and nutmeg. Cut in peanut butter using a pastry cutter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add water, and mix until just combined. Press into a 9 inch square baking pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove to wire rack to cool.
