Mexican Grilled Corn<ING>• 2 (15 ounce) cans whole kernel corn, drained• 1 cup salsa• 1/2 cup creamy salad dressing (e.g. Miracle Whip)• 1/2 cup chopped cilantro• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 1/2 teaspoon paprika<INS>‣ In a medium bowl, mix the corn, salsa, salad dressing, cilantro, cheese, and paprika. Refrigerate overnight.
