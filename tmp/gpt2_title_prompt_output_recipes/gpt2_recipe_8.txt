Twenty Minute Chicken Curry<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into cubes• 1 cup chopped onion• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1/2 cup chopped fresh ginger root• 1/2 cup chopped fresh chile peppers• 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes• 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon curry powder• 1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/2 cup plain yogurt• 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro• 1 teaspoon sesame seeds• 1 teaspoon curry powder• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves• 1 pinch ground nutmeg• 1 pinch ground allspice• 1/2 cup white sugar• 1/4 cup water• 1 cup shredded coconut<INS>‣ Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook chicken until no longer pink in center, about 10 minutes. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside.‣ Stir onion, celery, ginger, chile peppers, red pepper flakes, cumin, salt, curry powder, cayenne, cinnamon, turmeric, ginger, allspice, yogurt, cilantro, sesame seeds, curry powder, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, sugar, water, coconut, and coconut together in a bowl. Serve.
