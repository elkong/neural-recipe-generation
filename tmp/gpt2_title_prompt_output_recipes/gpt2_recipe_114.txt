Macaroni and Tuna Fish Salad<ING>• 2 cups elbow macaroni• 2 (10 ounce) cans tuna, drained• 1/4 cup mayonnaise• 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar• 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard• 1 tablespoon prepared horseradish• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/4 teaspoon salt• 1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper<INS>‣ Cook elbow macaroni in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Rinse under cold water and drain.‣ Combine tuna, mayonnaise, vinegar, Dijon mustard, horseradish, black pepper, salt, and cayenne pepper in a large bowl. Set aside.‣ Pour dressing over elbow macaroni, and toss to coat. Chill and serve.
