Lemon Butter Seasoned Vegetables<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon lemon juice• 1/4 cup vegetable broth• 1/4 cup water• 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), rinsed and drained• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro• 1 tablespoon white sugar• 1 teaspoon dried oregano• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley• 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint• 1/4 cup chopped red onion<INS>‣ Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute garlic and lemon juice for 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in vegetable broth and water. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 30 minutes.‣ Stir chickpeas, cilantro, sugar, oregano, and pepper into the skillet. Continue cooking until heated through. Remove from heat and stir in parsley, mint, and onion.
