Pudding in a Mug<ING>• 2 cups white sugar• 1 cup packed light brown sugar• 1 cup all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 3/4 cup milk• 1 egg, beaten• 1 cup chopped pecans• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 cup raisins• 1/4 cup chopped pecans<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 10 inch Bundt pan.‣ In a medium bowl, combine white sugar, brown sugar, flour, baking soda and salt. Cut in milk until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. In a medium bowl, combine eggs, 1 cup chopped pecans, vanilla, and raisins. Pour mixture into prepared pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before removing from pan. Sprinkle top with chopped nuts and sprinkle with additional nuts if desired.
