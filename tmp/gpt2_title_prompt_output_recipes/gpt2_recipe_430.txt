Chewiest Brownies<ING>• 1 cup butter, softened• 1 cup white sugar• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar• 1 egg• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract• 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon baking soda• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.‣ Mix together butter, white sugar, brown sugar, and egg until well blended. Beat in vanilla. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt. Blend in chocolate chips. Pour batter into prepared pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 25 minutes. Cool in pan on wire rack for 5 minutes.
