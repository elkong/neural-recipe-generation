Pasta e Fagioli a la Chez Ivano<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 large onion, chopped• 1 pound fresh mushrooms, chopped• 1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced• 1 clove garlic, minced• 3 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano• 1/2 cup chicken broth• 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes• 1 cup uncooked white rice• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley• salt and pepper to taste• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil<INS>‣ Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, and cook the onion until translucent, about 5 minutes. Mix in the mushrooms, mushrooms, garlic, oregano, and chicken broth. Bring to a simmer, and cook for 3 minutes. Mix in the rice, parsley, salt, and pepper. Reduce heat to low, and continue cooking until the rice is tender, about 20 minutes.
