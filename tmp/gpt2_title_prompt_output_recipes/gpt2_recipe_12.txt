Cranberry Sour Cream Kuchen<ING>• 2 cups white sugar• 1 cup packed brown sugar• 1/2 cup orange juice• 1/2 cup lemon juice• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1 teaspoon ground ginger• 1 teaspoon ground cloves• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1 cup chopped cranberries• 1 cup chopped pecans• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro<INS>‣ In a large bowl, mix sugar, brown sugar, orange juice, lemon juice, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, cloves, and cinnamon. Cut in the cranberries, pecans, mint, and cilantro until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator until serving.
