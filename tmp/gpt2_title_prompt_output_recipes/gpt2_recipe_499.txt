Lime-Marinated Grilled Salmon<ING>• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1 clove garlic, minced• 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes• 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms• 1 (2 pound) salmon fillet<INS>‣ Preheat grill for medium heat.‣ Heat oil in a medium skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic until fragrant. Add red pepper flakes and mushrooms; cook and stir until mushrooms are softened.‣ Lightly oil grill grate. Grill salmon for 10 minutes on each side, or until easily flaked with a fork.
