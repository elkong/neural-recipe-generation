Steak Dry Rub<ING>• 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs• 2 teaspoons seasoned salt• 1 teaspoon onion powder• 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder• 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme• 1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon paprika• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper• 1/4 teaspoon paprika• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1/4 cup sliced fresh mushrooms<INS>‣ Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a shallow baking dish.‣ Mix bread crumbs, seasoned salt, onion powder, garlic powder, thyme, rosemary, allspice, paprika, cayenne pepper, paprika, and black pepper together in a bowl until evenly combined; press into the bottom of the prepared baking dish.‣ Bake in the preheated oven until crumb mixture has risen, 10 to 15 minutes.
