Sourdough Tomato Bread<ING>• 2 cups warm water• 1 cup sourdough starter• 1 cup sifted all-purpose flour• 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese• 1 teaspoon dried basil leaves• 1 tablespoon ground dried thyme• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper• 1 1/2 cups bread flour• 1 cup sourdough starter<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.‣ In a large bowl, stir together the warm water, sourdough starter, 1 cup of the flour and the Cheddar cheese. In a separate bowl, stir together the basil, thyme, black pepper and bread flour. Stir into the sourdough mixture and toss to combine. Pour into prepared loaf pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes, or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
