Fried Pie Pastry<ING>• 2 cups all-purpose flour• 2 teaspoons baking powder• 1 teaspoon salt• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice• 1/2 cup chopped walnuts<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9 inch pie pan.‣ In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, ginger, and cloves. Stir in walnuts. Pour batter into the prepared pan.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until a knife inserted into the center of the pie comes out clean. Allow to cool for 5 minutes before cutting into squares.
