Funky Enchilada Casserole<ING>• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese• 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese• 1 cup chopped green onions• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese• 1 cup sour cream• 1/2 cup sour cream• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.‣ In a large bowl, combine macaroni, spinach, Cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, green onions, Cheddar cheese, sour cream and sour cream. Mix together and pour mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, or until bubbly and lightly browned. Sprinkle with cilantro just before serving.
