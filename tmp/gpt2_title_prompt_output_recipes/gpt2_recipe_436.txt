Seasoned Roasted Root Vegetables<ING>• 2 cups uncooked brown rice• 2 1/2 cups water• 2 cups shredded carrot• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables, thawed• 1 (15 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained• 1/2 cup honey• 1/2 cup red wine vinegar• 1/4 cup red wine powder• 1/2 teaspoon salt• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper• 2 tablespoons olive oil• 1/4 cup chopped onion• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley<INS>‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the brown rice, water, carrot, mixed vegetables, artichoke hearts, honey, vinegar, red wine, red wine powder, salt, pepper, olive oil, onion, and parsley. Spread the mixture into the prepared baking dish.‣ Bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until the vegetables are tender.
