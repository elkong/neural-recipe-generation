Chicken Oreganato<ING>• 2 cups uncooked white rice• 2 1/2 cups water• 3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup• 1 1/2 cups shredded carrots• 1 cup chopped celery• 1 cup chopped onion• 1/2 cup chopped celery• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley• 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour• 1/4 cup butter, melted<INS>‣ In a large pot, combine rice, water and cheese. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.‣ In a separate pot, combine soup, cream of chicken soup, cream of celery soup, carrots, celery and onion. Simmer over medium heat until carrots are tender and celery is tender. Add rice mixture, broth and parsley. Simmer until vegetables are tender.‣ In a small bowl, combine flour and butter. Mix well and form into a ball. Roll into 1 inch balls. Place in large bowl and cover with plastic wrap and let stand for 15 minutes.‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).‣ In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine melted butter and rice mixture. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.‣ Remove from heat, ladle into hot greased 9x13 inch baking dish and bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
