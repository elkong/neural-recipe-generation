Mix in 1 1/2 cups water, and 1 cup of the reserved cooking liquid, thyme sprigs, and white sugar; simmer over medium heat until mixture just start to set in volume, about 10 minutes; mix in 1 1/4 cups chopped bittersweet chocolate chips; set aside 1/2 cup of strong blender.
