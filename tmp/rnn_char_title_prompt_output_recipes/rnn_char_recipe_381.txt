‣ To Make Cupcakes: Sprinkle the candy light green topping, remaining 1 1/2 cups butter, dill pickle, chives, sugar, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract into crushed cranberries, and stir until smooth. Line in color, then spoon 1/4 cup butterscoored into 12 pieces and top with halved citrus zest. Top with remaining chocolate chips, 1/3 cup bittersweet chocolate chips and 1/2 cup chopped walnuts.
