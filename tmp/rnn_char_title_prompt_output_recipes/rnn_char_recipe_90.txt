Old Fashioned Cream Scones
‣ Arrange romaine lettuce, cucumber, chopped onion, 1 tablespoon cider vinegar, 1 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper in the bowl of a food processor or blender. Pulse until smooth and creamy.
‣ Transfer mixed vegetables and tofu with the green onion and fennel seeds. Drizzle 3 tablespoons water plesty over the approximately 1/2 cup for top. Serve with toast at room temperature.
