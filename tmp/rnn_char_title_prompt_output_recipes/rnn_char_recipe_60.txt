‣ Bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 20 minutes.
‣ Line bottom of a 9x13-inch glass baking dish with foil. Let cool and strain brown sugar mixture. Add 1/4 cup toasted coconut, 1 cup rum, and 1 ounce cream to equal amounts of warm sugar. Beat until stiff peaks are soft. (The dip will be firm enough. Makes the rest of the liquid, again in the top with the flattened.)
‣ Melt 1 tablespoon butter. Pour 1/3 of the berry-mixture over the top, and sprinkle with the crushed caramel. Top with raisins and bananas. Pour remaining 1 cup of sugar on top of each cinnamon and stir until well blended. Freeze 7 hours or overnight before serving.
