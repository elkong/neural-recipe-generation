Shabu-Shabu Pan Rusti Grill C).
‣ Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) with liderables should be slightly runny a bit, 12 to 15 minutes. Set aside.
‣ In a Dutch oven, brown beef with 1 tablespoon orange, orange juice, and 2 more tablespoons minced ginger; frequently season with salt and pepper. Reduce heat to low; cook, stirring with the saucepan, stirring occasionally, until thickened and evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Spread onto waxed paper to cool. May be stored in a covered container for at least 3 hours to overnight.
