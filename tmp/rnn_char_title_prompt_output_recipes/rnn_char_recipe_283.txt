Southern Green Beans II Pie Crust Salad with Parmesan-style Cream Cheese Cocoa Cream Cheese.
‣ Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook garlic in the hot oil until they begin to brown, about 2 minutes; remove from heat.
‣ In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs and cheese until well blended. Stir in 1/2 cup bread crumbs, pecans, and 1/4 cup cider vinegar; pour over the batter.
‣ Bake in preheated oven until golden and crunchy, 5 to 10 minutes.
