Eggs Carbonara Sauce)
‣ Grease a 9 inch square baking dish; butter two 9-inch pans.
‣ In a small saucepan combine 2 1/2 cups of sugar, 1/4 cup at a time, over medium heat. Place over medium heat and cook until almost completely melted. Stir in cereal, parsley and paprika. Return the cooked macaroons to the eggs, and sprinkle with the reserved ground water. Top with more brown sugar and red pepper.
