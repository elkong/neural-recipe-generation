🍴 Salmon and Vegetables en Papillote

 🥑 
 • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese 
 • 1 4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ in a medium saucepan , saute ground beef until the meat is crumbly , 5 to 7 minutes ; drain ; pour sauce over spaghetti and fill with parmesan cheese . 
 ‣ heat a large skillet over medium-high heat . cook and stir mushrooms in hot butter until just tender . remove from heat and stir in parmesan cheese . heat cream cheese and sauce . pour sauce over ingredients in the skillet . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven until edges are easily with a fork , about 35 minutes . remove from oven and top with parsley sauce . let cool slightly , then slice in half .
