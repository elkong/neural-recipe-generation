🍴 Easter Brunch Casserole

 🥑 
 • 1 4 cup plain maple syrup 
 • 1 tablespoon cornstarch 
 • 2 tablespoons instant tapioca 
 • 1 cup crushed pineapple 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ in a saucepan over medium heat , cook the pears and cranberry juice until very soft . stir in the corn syrup , orange zest , and salt . cook for 2 minutes , pour over the water . cover , and continue to cook for 30 minutes . 
 ‣ return the jars to a boil . cook the stirring for about 2 minutes , stirring occasionally . remove from heat and stir in the vanilla . remove from the heat and stir in the marshmallows . 
 ‣ pour the remaining sugar mixture over the layer of marshmallow mixture and stir . sprinkle over the cooled potato . chill for several hours before serving .
