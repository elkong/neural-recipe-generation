🍴 White Peach Margarita

 🥑 
 • 2 cups water 
 • 2 tablespoons sugar 
 • 2 teaspoons salt 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ in a small saucepan , stir together vinegar and sugar . bring to a boil , and cook for 2 minutes . 
 ‣ remove from heat , and place in a metal bowl . place sugar in a saucepan over medium-low heat , and bring to a boil . stir in salt and boil . cook , stirring constantly , until thickened . 
 ‣ 16 minutes with cooking spray . cook until softened , at least 8 minutes . remove from heat . stir in the vanilla extract , and whiskey . cook and stir for 1 minute at 375 degrees f ( 190 degrees c ) . pour into sterilized jars .
