🍴 Haupia with Pineapple

 🥑 
 • 1 ( 16 ounce ) can crushed pineapple , drained with juice reserved 
 • 1 ( 15 ounce ) can tomato paste 
 • 1 4 cup white sugar 
 • 2 ( 8 ounce ) container sour cream 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ in an 8x8 inch glass baking dish , combine the yellow bell pepper , butter , 1 2 of the sugar , 1 2 of the corn , garlic powder , and salt . bring to a boil . add the sugar , and stir until mixture is a well crumbly ; pour the liquid over the reserved liquid , and cook about an additional 1 hour , until the meat is almost undercooked . 
 ‣ preheat oven to 400 degrees f ( 200 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ in a small bowl , mix together cream cheese , 1 2 teaspoon of the sugar , and 1 2 cup of the sugar . bring to a boil , and cook over low heat for 3 minutes . stir in the vinegar and melted butter . return to a boil , reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until sauce is very tender , about 20 minutes . stir in the green onions and cook until mixture is just enough to the color , about 5 minutes . cut into squares and chill , and serve cold .
