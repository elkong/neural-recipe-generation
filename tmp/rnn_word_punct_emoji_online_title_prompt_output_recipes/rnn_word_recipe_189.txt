🍴 Laurie's Tequila Marinade

 🥑 
 • 1 3 cup white sugar 
 • 1 2 cup water 
 • 2 tablespoons water 
 • 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest 
 • 1 ( 10 . 75 ounce ) can condensed cream of celery soup 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . lightly grease a medium baking dish . 
 ‣ in a saucepan , combine sugar , water , ginger , and melted butter . stir until very smooth . 
 ‣ in a blender , combine blender , cream , brown sugar , pecans , egg yolks , parmesan cheese and vanilla yogurt . fill pastry blender with a pastry blender or food processor . add the sugar , more water , sea salt and pepper . mix until well combined . spread the mixture evenly over the top of the prepared cake shell . 
 ‣ bake the crust in the preheated oven until warmed through and bubbly , about 30 minutes . set aside to cool completely . 
 ‣ cream the cream cheese , 1 2 cup sugar , 3 tablespoons at a time , 2 minutes , then add 1 tablespoon of sour cream , 1 cup sherry , and 1 2 teaspoon vanilla extract . 
 ‣ beat cream cheese with butter , cheddar cheese , and lemon juice to the lemon juice spread with the cream cheese until fluffy . pour in the cream cheese mixture . stir the cream and chocolate chips into the mixture . stir in the sour cream and cream of tartar ; pour over cooled filling . serve the cake with the remaining frosting . 
 ‣ bake covered in a preheated 350 , 175 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) for topping on the preheated oven .
