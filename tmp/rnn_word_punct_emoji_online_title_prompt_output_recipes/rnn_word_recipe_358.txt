🍴 Honeydew Smoothie

 🥑 
 • 1 1 2 cups white sugar 
 • 1 4 cup light brown sugar 
 • 1 2 cup white sugar 
 • 1 ( 6 ounce ) can crushed pineapple ( optional ) 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan . 
 ‣ mix sugar , yogurt , sugar , sour cream , sugar , salt , vanilla , heavy cream , sugar , and bourbon in a saucepan ; bring to a boil , and cook until tender , about 5 minutes . remove from heat . pour hot cream into a 9 inch or baking dish . 
 ‣ in a saucepan with 1 4 of lid sugar , mix glaze , sugar , and cream of tartar . bring to a boil . reduce heat to low and simmer mixture until tender , about 1 hour . remove from heat . stir in coconut and baking mix . 
 ‣ pour into prepared baking dish . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven until the edges are set , about 6 minutes .
