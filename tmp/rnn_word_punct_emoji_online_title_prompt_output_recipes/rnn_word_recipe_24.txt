🍴 Oven-Baked Chicken Linguine

 🥑 
 • 2 cups self-rising flour with baking powder 
 • 1 2 cup chopped walnuts 
 • 1 cup cooking sherry 
 • 1 pinch cayenne pepper 
 • 1 2 cup milk 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 375 degrees f ( 190 degrees c ) . spray a 9 inch springform dish with cooking spray . 
 ‣ in a medium bowl , cream together the butter and light brown sugar . stir in another 1 2 cup of the hot milk and the chicken . pour in the milk , and mix well and combine . in a separate bowl mix together the flour , oats , flour , baking soda , salt and paprika . stir constantly until it is fully lumpy . pour into flour . 
 ‣ knead dough in the flour mixture for about 5 minutes . pour the remaining batter into the prepared baking dish . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven for 3 minutes , until top is golden brown .
