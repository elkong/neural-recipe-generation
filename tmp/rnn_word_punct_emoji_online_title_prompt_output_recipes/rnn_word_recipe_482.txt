 ‣ place the sesame seeds in a food processor . pulse in some of the olive oil and vinegar until smooth . mix in the cream cheese until well blended . use a blender to blend in the sour cream . pour in the vegetable broth and slowly stir until the mixture forms a smooth ball and blend . if the dough seems too thick , slowly pour in the remaining olive oil . gently remove from the heat and chill the mixture until very soft . 
 ‣ to serve , pour some of the wine and the cream into the hot skillet . stir in gelatin and cook until golden . pour into a serving dish and serve cold , or sauce .
