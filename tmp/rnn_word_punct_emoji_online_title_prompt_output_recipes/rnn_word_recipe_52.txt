 ‣ heat a large skillet over medium-high heat . cook and stir until browned . add the onions and cook and stir until the onions is translucent , about 10 minutes . drain and discard the grease from the skillet with the fresh saute until the butter melts ; let it is cool . 
 ‣ in a medium bowl , mix the beef , cottage cheese , onion , cream cheese , and garlic . season with kosher salt and pepper and stir in the remaining parsley sauce . pour into the pan , and stir in the crumbled and mozzarella . bring to a simmer , and cook until the cheese has softened , 2 to 3 minutes . remove from heat and turn onto a joe plate . toss with the remaining sauce , and serve .
