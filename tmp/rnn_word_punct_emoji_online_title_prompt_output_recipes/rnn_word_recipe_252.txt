 ‣ melt the white sugar in a saucepan over medium heat , stirring constantly until fragrant . reduce heat to low and simmer 20 minutes . 
 ‣ in a medium saucepan , measure 2 tablespoons of milk and cornstarch in a heatproof bowl . stir in the vanilla extract and orange zest . stir in the orange juice and pineapple . 
 ‣ pour the glass and add enough water to just cover the jars with the hot beer . chill while the refrigerator for 1 hour . 
 ‣ heat the remaining oil in a saucepan over medium heat . stir in the ground white sugar . pour the hot water into the vegetable oil and stir until the mixture is smooth and creamy . reduce heat to low , cover , and simmer for 20 minutes . remove from heat , and let cool for 1 hour before serving .
