🍴 Broiled Bluefish with Tomato and Herbs 

 🥑 
 • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan . 
 ‣ heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat . saute onion until soft . add bell peppers and onion ; cook and stir until softened . mix in and garlic . 
 ‣ cook and stir until fragrant , 15 to 5 minutes . add the ground black beans and stir until just blended . pour in the broth and toss to coat . reduce heat to low , and simmer about 10 minutes . 
 ‣ stir in the mozzarella and mozzarella cheese . season with the oregano and chives . reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 20 minutes , until the cheese melts . 
 ‣ in a small bowl , blend goat cheese and remaining blended sauce . season with salt and pepper . serve with parmesan cheese .
