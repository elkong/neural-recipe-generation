 ‣ melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat , brown the beef in the butter until evenly browned . add the egg and water to the cornbread ingredients until it is well mixed . stir in the chocolate chips and sugar . bring to a boil , reduce heat to low , and simmer for 1 hour . 
 ‣ puree the marshmallow mixture in a blender . puree until smooth . remove from heat and stir in the cheddar and vanilla ; continue beating until the mixture begins to thicken . 
 ‣ stir in the red food coloring . if the mixture is too thick , pour mixture into the jars jars . drink remove side up in a bowl . season with salt and pepper . stir in lemon zest . mix in chocolate . pour filling over the 9x13-inch pie shells .
