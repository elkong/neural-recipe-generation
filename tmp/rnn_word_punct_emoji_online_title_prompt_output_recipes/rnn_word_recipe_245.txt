 ‣ preheat oven to 325 degrees f ( 165 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ transfer the liquid from the refrigerator , and spoon the half of the liquid into the sterilized jars . replace the rim of the dutch of 1 inch glass baking dish . add the apples and 1 4 cup of the reserved apples . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 60 minutes . turn the oven occasionally over low . frost the meringue with whipped topping .
