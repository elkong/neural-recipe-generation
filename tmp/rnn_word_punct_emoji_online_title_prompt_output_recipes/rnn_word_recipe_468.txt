🍴 Microwave English Muffin Bread

 🥑 
 • 2 cups all-purpose flour 
 • 1 tablespoon baking powder 
 • 3 tablespoons salt 
 • 3 cups cold water 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 300 degrees f ( 150 degrees c ) . lightly oil the grate . 
 ‣ in a large bowl , cream together the butter and sugar . add the dry ingredients and mix in the oats , eggs , and add the eggs . stir in the tequila and vanilla . 
 ‣ divide mixture evenly into each of thirds in getting greased and bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven . remove from oven and bake for 30 minutes . 50-60 patties and remove from oven .
