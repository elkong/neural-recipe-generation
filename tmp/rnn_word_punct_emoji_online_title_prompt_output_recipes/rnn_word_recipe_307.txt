🍴 Coconut-Cranberry Bars with Pecans

 🥑 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat . cook and stir onion and sweetened milk in the hot oil until translucent , about 5 minutes . add sweet potatoes and cook for 3-5 blend ; cool completely . press over less layer , and cool . 
 ‣ beat eggs with vanilla . add eggs and mix until smooth . add water . add marshmallows , and stir lightly . add sugar and mix . 
 ‣ pour into skillet . stir until heated through . remove from heat and let cool completely . 
 ‣ pour water into pie shell ; chill until firm .
