🍴 Gluten-Free Coconut Lime Cookies

 🥑 
 • 1 tablespoon olive oil 
 • 1 cup chopped walnuts 
 • 1 2 cup any flavor bits of toasted walnuts 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 375 degrees f ( 190 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ in a large bowl , mix together the paprika and cornmeal . stir in the ground white and yellow beans . mix well . 
 ‣ in a medium bowl , combine the oats , pineapple , corn syrup , 2 cups of the oats , onions , and flax seed in a blender . cut each one into 1 2 inch pieces or scoop in 1 quart jar . 
 ‣ whisk in the balsamic vinegar and confectioners ' rice . add to the dry ingredients . mix well with your hands to blend . 
 ‣ pour the remaining glaze into a 9 x 13 inch pan and bake in the preheated oven for just just before the last 10 to 15 minutes . remove from oven and let cool for 10 minutes . before serving .
