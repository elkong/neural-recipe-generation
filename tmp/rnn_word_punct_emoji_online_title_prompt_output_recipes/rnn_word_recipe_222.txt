 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ in a large bowl , mix together the flour , baking powder , and ground beef . add eggs and stir . add the dry ingredients and pour in oil , stirring until well blended . stir in the nuts , then simmer , uncovered , for 5 minutes . mix in the brown sugar and bread crumbs . simmer for 30 minutes . stir in the cheddar cheese . 
 ‣ in a medium mixing bowl , combine flour and sugar . stir in the cheddar cheese and pour for a 9x13 inch baking dish . spread over the pork mixture . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven until the soup is tender and the pork is easily pierced with a fork , 15 to 20 minutes .
