🍴 Pappardelle with Asparagus and Lemon

 🥑 
 • 1 ( 8 ounce ) can crushed tomatoes , drained and chopped 
 • 1 cup chopped celery 
 • 1 4 cup white sugar 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ bring a pot of water to a boil . add the sugar and cook for about 2 minutes , stirring occasionally . add the water and cook until tender . add water , and bring to a boil . reduce heat to medium-low , cover and simmer for 20 minutes . 
 ‣ stir in the pectin and water . heat for 2 minutes , stirring constantly . reduce heat to low , cover , and simmer for an additional 20 minutes . remove saucepan from heat , and allow to cool . 
 ‣ melt the remaining 1 tablespoon of syrup , 1 teaspoon of sugar , 1 teaspoon of the coconut extract , and red food coloring in a blender , to blend until smooth . store in an airtight container at room temperature .
