🍴 Kale Mint Cookies

 🥑 
 • 3 cups water , divided 
 • 1 ( 32 ounce ) bottle sauerkraut , drained and rinsed 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ heat oil in a skillet over medium heat . saute onions , celery , and onion until tender . stir in the leek ; cook and stir until vegetables are tender , about 1 minute . 
 ‣ stir in the tomatoes , tomatoes , onion , onion and tomato . continue to stir all 8 to 10 minutes , then stirring in more water if needed . 
 ‣ stir in the bacon mixture , half-and-half and cornstarch . add rice . raise heat to high and bring to a boil . reduce heat , cover and simmer for 20 minutes . season with salt and pepper . serve hot .
