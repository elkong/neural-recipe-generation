🍴 Kielbasa Pasta Casserole

 🥑 
 • salt and pepper to taste 
 • 1 4 teaspoon dried basil

 🥣 
 ‣ in a heatproof blender , boil the olive oil until it shimmers or 10 . add sugar , and 2 teaspoons of cayenne pepper . stir in the ground beef and eggs . season with salt and pepper . set aside . 
 ‣ in a large skillet over medium-low heat , cook the ground beef until the meatballs are soft . drain and discard any remaining fat . stir in the onion , celery , tomatoes with oregano . reduce heat to low and simmer until mixture is just tender . remove from the heat and place 1 cup of the skillet in the pot with a lid . stir into the ground beef , and continue to cook until crisp . mix the turkey with the tomato soup and salt , and pepper into the beef , and serve .
