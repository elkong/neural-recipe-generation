🍴 Bloody Shrimp

 🥑 
 • 3 tablespoons white sugar 
 • 1 ( 10 . 75 ounce ) can whole kernel corn , drained 
 • 1 pound lean ground beef 
 • 3 ounces dry bread crumbs 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 375 degrees f ( 190 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ spread the ground beef mixture evenly over the top of the beef . spread the ground beef evenly in a layer of beef mixture . spread the beef mixture over the top of the mixture . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven until beans are tender and golden brown , 10 to 15 minutes .
