🍴 Chocolate Pudding

 🥑 
 • 3 ( 8 ounce ) packages cream cheese , softened 
 • 1 ( 1 . 9 inch ) piece of sourdough bread mix 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ in a heavy cream , combine graham crackers and butter . cook and stir until chocolate is no longer pink in the center , 5 to 7 minutes . stir in butter or margarine , and blend until smooth . stir in sugar . bring to a boil , then stir in small speed . when cool enough to pop , remove from heat and stir in more confectioners ' sugar and vanilla . continue to stir until blended . 
 ‣ in a mixing bowl , beat cream cheese with an electric mixer in a bowl until smooth and fluffy . stir in egg yolk and vanilla . pour filling into a 9x13-inch baking dish . refrigerate until serving .
