🍴 Korean Soybean Noodles (Kong Kook Su)

 🥑 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ in a large saucepan over low heat , stir the white wine and water until the melted and water is boiling . stir in the white wine and bring to a boil ; cook and stir for a few minutes . remove from heat and stir in the reserved bacon . cover and keep warm . 
 ‣ heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat . saute the heat until the meat is browned and crumbly , 5 to 7 minutes . drain and discard any grease . stir in the tofu , onion , water , and tarragon . cover skillet and cook until the cheese is tender . cook and stir 3 more minutes , then add the kale , and cook for 3 minutes more . stir in the rice , crushed tomatoes and half of the tomato paste . bring to a boil , and reduce heat to low , and simmer for 20 minutes . 
 ‣ fill a skillet with half of the vegetables , then place the dish on the pot . cook and stir until the surface is tender . sprinkle with cajun seasoning , and serve on the center of the pita bread .
