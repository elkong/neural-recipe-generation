🍴 Anticuchos (Grilled Beef Heart)

 🥑 
 • 1 ( 1 pound ) loaf sliced fresh mushrooms 
 • 1 tablespoon imitation maple syrup 
 • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour 
 • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce 
 • 1 cup chopped onion 
 • 3 cups water 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ heat the oven rack in the oven to 375 degrees f ( 190 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ in a skillet over medium heat , melt the butter and blend the onions until the beef is crumbly and golden brown spots . spread the beef broth evenly over the potato layer , cover and pour the sauerkraut over the beef . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven for 7 minutes . remove from heat and stir in cream cheese . 
 ‣ in a small skillet over medium heat , cook the beef until no longer pink shows each , about 5 minutes . stir in the heavy cream until bubbly . remove from heat , and stir in the cream cheese . gently fold the cream cheese into the sides of the base . divide the beef in half . sprinkle over the beef . sprinkle the cheddar cheese over the top . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven until the hot peppers are tender and the cheese is tender and the cheese is browned , about 10 minutes .
