🍴 Furikake Salmon

 🥑 
 • 1 ( 10 ounce ) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers 
 • 2 cups shredded sharp mexican cheese blend 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . lightly grease a 9 inch springform pan . 
 ‣ in a small bowl , mix together the graham crackers , mayonnaise , green bell peppers , salsa , dried corn , sour cream , chives , and hot pepper . spread evenly over the top of the tomato mixture . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes , or until crisp . the drained cool will served crisp .
