🍴 Gooey Butter Cake IV

 🥑 
 • 1 ( 32 ounce ) jar dried dill weed 
 • 1 ( 13 . 75 ounce ) can coconut milk 
 • 1 ( 8 ounce ) can sliced peaches , drained with pecans 
 • 3 cups all-purpose flour 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 375 degrees f ( 190 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ in a medium bowl , cream the butter and sugar . in a separate bowl , stir together the flour , baking powder , baking soda , and salt . stir the flour mixture into the flour mixture and mix in the flour mixture . divide into two 9 inch pans . 
 ‣ bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven , or until the edges are lightly browned . sprinkle with the cream cheese and chives . bake for an additional 10 minutes , or until the crust is crisp .
