🍴 Three Sister's Stew

 🥑 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ place the sliced beef in a skillet over medium heat . cook the onions , about 15 minutes , then stir in brown sugar , whole blackberries , water , and jalapeno peppers . cook , stirring occasionally , until the onion is translucent . add onion and garlic ; cook and stir until tender , about 5 minutes . add more sherry if necessary to desired doneness . stir in gravy . 
 ‣ drain and pour into a mein layer . stir to combine . pour soup over , and serve .
