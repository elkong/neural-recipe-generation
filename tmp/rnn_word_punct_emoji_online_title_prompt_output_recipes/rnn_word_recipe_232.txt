🍴 Arroz con Leche

 🥑 
 • 3 cups water 
 • 1 teaspoon salt 
 • 1 2 teaspoon ground black pepper 
 • 1 ( 8 ounce ) package cream cheese ( optional ) 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 375 degrees f ( 190 degrees c ) . grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan . 
 ‣ in a medium bowl , microwave the eggs , stirring until very smooth . add the eggs and mix well . add sugar , water , cheese , and worcestershire sauce ; mix well . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned and the meat is bubbling , 1 to 2 minutes . let cool completely before storing in the same bowl , turn over the bowl , and refrigerate for at least 3 hours .
