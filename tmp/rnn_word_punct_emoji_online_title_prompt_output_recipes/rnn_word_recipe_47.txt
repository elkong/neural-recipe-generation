🍴 Greek Chicken (Maybe)

 🥑 
 • 1 tablespoon olive oil 
 • salt and pepper to taste 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ heat olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat , and cook and stir onion and garlic until onion is translucent and translucent , about 5 minutes . 
 ‣ stir chicken broth , soy sauce , balsamic vinegar , garlic powder , and tarragon into the skillet . bring to a boil , reduce heat to medium-low , low and simmer until thick and bubbly , at least 30 minutes . season with salt and black pepper . 
 ‣ cook the pasta sauce over low heat for 10 minutes or until the pasta is warmed . when hot , cooked pasta is done , remove the bone and the vegetables from skillet . skim off and discard excess fat .
