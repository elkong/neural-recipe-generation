🍴 Mashed Sweet Potatoes

 🥑 
 • 1 2 cup tomato paste 
 • 1 cup canned tomato , chopped ( optional ) 
 • 1 2 cup heavy cream 
 • 1 tablespoon cornstarch 
 • salt and ground black pepper to taste 
 • 1 ( 8 ounce ) can italian green beans , drained 
 • 3 cups sour cream 
 • 1 2 cup grated parmesan cheese 
 • 1 2 teaspoon ground black pepper 
 • 1 ( 10 . 75 ounce ) can condensed cream of mushroom soup and cream cheese soup 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet over medium heat . saute the onion until soft and translucent . stir in the tomatoes and cook until the onion has softened and translucent . stir in the mushrooms and saute 5 minutes , stirring until the onion has softened . add the jalapeno and italian seasoning . bring to a boil , and simmer until the grits are tender , about 20 minutes . stir in the milk ; cook and stir until the broccoli is bright green and hot . return to a boil , and stir in the tomatoes with the lemon zest . cook and stir for 10 minutes . 
 ‣ drain the stock and place into a saucepan over medium heat . stir in the tomato sauce and salt . simmer for 30 minutes , stirring occasionally . stir in the spinach and cauliflower ; cook for 2 minutes . stir in the cream and the bacon until vegetables are tender . remove from heat and stir in the cream cheese , cheddar cheese , and grated poppy seeds . pour soup into bowls and stir in the spinach mixture . serve .
