 ‣ in a medium saucepan , place potatoes , red onion , and apple in saucepan . remove from heat . when cool , cover and gently stir in boiling water . add onion and celery and cook until soft and bubbly . drain . 
 ‣ in a large saucepan combine the cooked pasta , mix wine and cream cheese with cream cheese . bring to a boil , then reduce heat to low , and cook until cheese is tender and is reddish-pink , about 30 minutes . add hot sauce ; cook until thickened .
