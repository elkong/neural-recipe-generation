🍴 Mozzarella and Herb-Stuffed Chicken Breast Sous-Vide Style

 🥑 
 ‣ preheat the oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ cut the chicken breast halves , and dip up into the shapes drop by leaving a meat in the skillet . fry each side on each side until meat is no longer pink inside of the inside , about 10 minutes . remove the mushrooms on paper towels . 
 ‣ cool the second slice of bacon in a 9x13-inch baking dish and pour the remaining sauce over the chicken . 
 ‣ bake in preheated oven , until the cheese has melted and bubbling into the skillet . stir in the lemon zest , and season with kosher salt and pepper .
