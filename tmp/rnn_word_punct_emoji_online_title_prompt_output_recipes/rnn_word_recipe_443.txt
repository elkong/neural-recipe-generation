🍴 Saffron Alioli 

 🥑 
 • 2 ( 8 ounce ) container cream cheese , softened and divided 
 • 1 2 cup white sugar 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ in a large bowl , stir together the ground beef , sugar , sour cream , 3 tablespoons cream of tartar , 1 3 of the sugar , 1 2 teaspoon of ground nutmeg , and salt . in a medium bowl , beat egg whites until sugar is dissolved . stir in the eggs , adding more water if necessary . with a fork , add the eggs , one at a time , beating well . press the bottom of a lightly floured springform pan . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven until the edges are golden and soft , about 10 minutes .
