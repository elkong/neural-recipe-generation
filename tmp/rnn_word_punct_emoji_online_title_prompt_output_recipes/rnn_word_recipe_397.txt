🍴 Chicken Salad with Grapes and Walnuts 

 🥑 
 • 1 2 cup olive oil 
 • 1 2 cup chopped fresh basil 
 • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ whisk together the vinegar , olive oil , balsamic vinegar , lime juice , garlic powder , and oregano in a bowl until smooth . add the cobbler and chicken breast halves . 
 ‣ place the chicken in a large bowl ; pour the chicken stock into the skillet . cook until onion is translucent . drain on paper towels . 
 ‣ in a mixing bowl , mix the chicken , chicken stock , mayonnaise and lemon zest . pour the olive oil into the skillet and stir over chicken . bring to a boil and cook for 2 minutes . remove from heat and stir in the black cream and parmesan cheese .
