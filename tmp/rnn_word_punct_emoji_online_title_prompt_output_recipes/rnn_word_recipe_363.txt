 ‣ mix spinach , miso , tomatoes , orange zest , and seasoned salt in a bowl until well combined . 
 ‣ mix olive oil , garlic powder , baking powder , oregano , and ground black pepper in a bowl ; place hash browns in a separate strainer . 
 ‣ combine crushed bread crumbs , 1 2 teaspoon ground black pepper , and cayenne pepper in a large bowl ; season with salt and pepper . 
 ‣ toss tomato sauce mixture in adobo sauce ; cook and stir until fragrant and beef is coated in a large , about 10 minutes ; stir in currants with paprika . stuff to coat pastry ; transfer mixture to a prepared baking dish .
