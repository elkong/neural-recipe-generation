 ‣ heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat . saute garlic until starting thin ; stir in lemon zest . reduce heat to medium-low and cook until the grits are tender , about 5 minutes . stir in eggs and half of the parmesan cheese . stir in the sour cream . cook and stir until the sauce thickens , about 5 minutes . add the cream and stir until well blended . 
 ‣ pour the remaining sauce onto the skillet and cook until tender . pour in the tomato sauce and bring to a boil . reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until the sauce thickens , about 7 minutes . add the diced tomatoes , and stir to combine . 
 ‣ pour into soup and serve .
