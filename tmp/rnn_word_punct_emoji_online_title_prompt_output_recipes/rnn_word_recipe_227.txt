🍴 Mud Pie

 🥑 
 • 1 cup white sugar 
 • 2 eggs 
 • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 
 • 1 cup sliced walnuts 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ in a medium bowl mix together 2 tablespoons olive oil , red sugar , vanilla extract , sugar , and baking powder . mix in eggs and vanilla . stir in the mixture at 375 degrees f ( 190 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ beat cream cheese with electric mixer in a small bowl until fluffy . gradually pour in the remaining egg mixture mixture . blend until gradually beginning to form . place in a large bowl and pour into a baking dish . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean , 30 to 35 minutes . cool completely .
