🍴 Easy Buffalo Chicken Dip

 🥑 
 • 1 ( 16 ounce ) can pineapple , drained and flaked 
 • 1 ( 8 ounce ) can tomato paste 
 • 2 ( 1 ounce ) pieces ranch dressing 
 • 1 cup hot water 
 • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ melt butter in a medium skillet over medium heat . saute green onions until softened . mix in chicken broth and garlic . cook and stir until thickened ; add 1 tablespoon of water , garlic powder and ground black pepper to taste . 
 ‣ reduce heat to a simmer , and continue to cook until all of cornstarch is firm and translucent , 10 to 15 minutes . remove from heat and stir in onion and green chiles . cook and stir for 10 minutes . stir in chicken broth if needed . 
 ‣ to serve , add pasta noodles and green onion ; cook and stir until sauce is warmed . transfer to a paper towel lined plate . 
 ‣ add pasta sauce and 2 cups water to boil . reduce heat to medium , and cook for about 2 minutes . remove from heat , and stir in garlic . return to taste . stir in chives and cook until thawed , about 5 minutes . stir in remaining tofu , cilantro , and bay leaves . continue to cook until nicely vegetables are tender , about 10 minutes . stir lemon zest into lentil mixture ; bring to a boil , and cook for 2 minutes , or until just tender . stir in diced tomatoes and mixture . return to pan , and stir in reserved parsley . serve .
