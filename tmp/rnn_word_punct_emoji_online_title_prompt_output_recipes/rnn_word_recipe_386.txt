🍴 Easy Chicken Fajitas from RO*TEL

 🥑 
 • 4 ( 6 ounce ) skinless , boneless chicken breast halves , pounded to 1 hour . 
 ‣ preheat oven to 400 degrees f ( 200 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ in a small bowl , mix together chicken , chicken , chicken broth , chicken broth , and salt . in a shallow bowl , mix together chicken broth , cornstarch and water . pour half of chicken stock mixture over the chicken mixture . 
 ‣ bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes .
