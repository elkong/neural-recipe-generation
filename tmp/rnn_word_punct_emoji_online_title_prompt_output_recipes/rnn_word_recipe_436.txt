🍴 Mesclun Salad

 🥑 
 • 8 cups bread flour 
 • 1 cup finely chopped walnuts 
 • 1 cup shredded provolone cheese 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan . 
 ‣ mix flour , baking powder , baking powder , salt , and sugar together in a bowl . stir flour and baking powder into flour until smooth ; add more flour if necessary . spoon batter into prepared loaf pan . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour . 
 ‣ remove from heat and stir in the milk . cover , and let stand 5 minutes for full hour . 
 ‣ whisk together the flour , baking powder , and baking powder in a bowl . top with the remaining flour mixture . return to oxtail mixture to the prepared baking dish , and bake another 35 to 40 minutes in the preheated oven . let cool for 10 minutes before slicing .
