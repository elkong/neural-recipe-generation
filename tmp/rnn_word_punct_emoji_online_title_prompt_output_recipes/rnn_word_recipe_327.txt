🍴 Vegetable Pesto Salad with Warm Goat Cheese Toasts 

 🥑 
 • 1 2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese 
 • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil 
 • 1 2 cup grated parmesan cheese 
 • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ brown sugar in a large skillet and stir in tomato paste . cook , stirring , until golden . toss with hot pasta , and cook until softened . add tomatoes with red onion , bell pepper , italian seasoning , salt , and red pepper . cook and stir 2 minutes . 
 ‣ remove from heat and stir in cheese until melted . pour melted margarine and stir over the mixture in small bowl . toss until smooth and creamy . 
 ‣ turn out onto an ungreased baking sheet and broil for 5 minutes , or until cool . dessert with green bell pepper .
