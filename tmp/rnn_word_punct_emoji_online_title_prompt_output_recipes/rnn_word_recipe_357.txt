🍴 Steamed Sea Bass with Ginger and Shiitakes 

 🥑 
 • 3 cups fish , onion and drained 
 • 1 4 cup olive oil 
 • 1 cup freshly squeezed dry vermouth 
 • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper 
 • 1 teaspoon ground cayenne 
 • 1 cup buttery round crackers ( such as guests ( optional ) 
 • 1 tablespoon olive oil 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ heat a large pot of water in the pot . turn the heat to low , and stir to melt . add the oats , and stir to cook and stir into the white wine . cook , and stir the onion until softened and translucent , about 5 minutes . remove from the heat , and stir in the chopped tomatoes . cook , stirring , until the onion has softened and turned golden brown , 2 to 3 minutes . 
 ‣ pour half the cream liquid over the mixture and mix in the tomatoes , and stir until the desired consistency is achieved . pour the remaining butter into the skillet , then stir in the black beans . bring to a boil , reduce heat to low , and simmer for 15 minutes . reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 2 to 3 minutes . remove the lid , and crack in the pot . stir the water into the water . season to taste with salt and pepper . 
 ‣ puree covered with the olive oil in a blender or food processor until smooth and creamy . stir in the parsley and parsley . pour the tomatoes over the shrimp . bring to a boil . reduce heat to medium , and simmer until the liquid has reduced by half , about 5 minutes . remove from the heat . stir the egg yolk and sugar and mix in the cream with a fork . stir in the parsley , and cook until the pasta is boiling and the sauce is still firm , about 1 hour .
