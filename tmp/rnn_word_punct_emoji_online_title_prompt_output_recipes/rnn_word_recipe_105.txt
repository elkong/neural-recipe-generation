 ‣ heat the oil in a skillet over medium-high heat . cook and stir the onion until translucent until browned , about 5 minutes per side ; remove the beef and set aside . 
 ‣ mix the onions , garlic , onion , and onion in the bacon drippings until the pork is no longer pink of the pork mushrooms . pour the bouillon into the skillet and cook over medium heat . stir the tomatoes and green onion and continue cooking until the meat is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear , 5 to 7 minutes . season with salt and black pepper . 
 ‣ whisk in the flour , salt and cooled chicken broth . bring to a boil and simmer until apple has alcohol . stir in the pork and cook until the liquid has evaporated , about 5 minutes . serve with sauce and cool .
