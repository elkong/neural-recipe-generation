🍴 Red Wine Vinegar and Black Pepper Mignonette 

 🥑 
 • 1 tablespoon olive oil 
 • salt and to taste 
 • 1 cucumber , cut into wedges 
 • 1 bunch fresh cilantro , stems removed 
 • 1 ( 29 ounce ) can green tomatoes and cranberries 
 • 1 pinch dried rosemary , or to taste ( optional ) 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat ; cook and stir onion , green onion , bell peppers , green bell pepper , and onion in hot oil until softened , about 5 minutes . remove from pan and set aside . 
 ‣ heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat , and cook and stir roma broth and garlic . mix in onion paste , onion , and red wine , mix until smooth , about 8 minutes . season with cayenne pepper , cayenne pepper , and oregano . bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes , stirring occasionally . 
 ‣ remove from heat and stir in shallot and tomatoes . season with black pepper , cayenne pepper , and cayenne pepper . stir sea salt and pepper in a smooth paste . pour the cooled mixture into a blender and blend until smooth . pour into the white wine and stir to coat . return to the pot , and stir in the parsley and stir until the sauce is dissolved . sprinkle the remaining red pepper into the sterilized pan . stir in the pine nuts and season with chopped fresh parsley . 
 ‣ bring to a boil , and reduce heat to low , and simmer for 5 minutes . serve over the potatoes .
