🍴 Meaty Barbeque Sandwiches

 🥑 
 • 1 pound ground beef 
 • 3 tablespoons dried parsley 
 • 1 tablespoon dried oregano , basil 
 • 1 2 cup dried basil 
 • 1 4 cup dried tomato juice 
 • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish . 
 ‣ in a large skillet over medium heat , cook the ground beef until evenly browned . drain excess fat . stir in the melted butter , oregano , cooked chicken broth , and tarragon . season with salt and pepper . 
 ‣ crumble pasta into the skillet mixture ; bring to a simmer , then reduce heat to medium-low , and cook 5 minutes . mix in melted butter , mushrooms , salami , and tomato paste . cook for 10 minutes . transfer to a bowl . 
 ‣ shape into a 2 quart casserole dish . fold the tomatoes in the cream cheese mixture . 
 ‣ bake for 45 minutes in the preheated oven , until lightly browned .
