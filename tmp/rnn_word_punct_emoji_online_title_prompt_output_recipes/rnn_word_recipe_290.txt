🍴 Deviled Dungeness Crab with Wild Rice and Orzo Pilaf

 🥑 
 • salt to taste 
 • 1 tablespoon olive oil 
 • 1 large eggplant , shredded 
 • 1 pinch dried oregano 
 • 1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat . stir in the onion and garlic ; cook and stir until the onions have softened and turned translucent , about 5 minutes . remove from the heat . stir in the garlic and cook until the onion has softened and turned translucent , about 5 minutes . stir in the eggs , and stir until the kale and mushrooms are very tender . 
 ‣ when the cauliflower heats , stir in the onion and garlic ; cook and stir until the onions begin to soften , about 10 minutes . stir in the turmeric , and cook until the carrot is translucent . stir in the parsley , and cook , stirring constantly , until the rice is tender , about 10 minutes . stir in the cheddar cheese until fully melted . sprinkle with bacon bits and feta cheese .
