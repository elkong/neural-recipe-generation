 ‣ in a white bowl , mix the beef with the butter , and brown . stir in the chipotle peppers , and cook until the meat is rendered and brown . season with italian seasoning . rub meat into meat . 
 ‣ heat oil in a medium skillet over medium heat . saute the beef in the hot skillet until browned and shows translucent , about 5 minutes per side . 
 ‣ stir in the same skillet and cook until brown and golden , about 5 minutes . add the reserved pork to the pan and cook until pink and browned , 5 to 7 minutes . remove from heat . place the skillet in a skillet over medium heat and cook the pork until tender , about 5 minutes . remove from the heat and stir in the bacon as much . 
 ‣ stir the chicken pieces in the remaining box pieces , then return to a simmer , and cook until the meat is tender ; the legs are tender , about 3 minutes . return the and green skillet to the pan . add the rice and chicken to the bread pieces ; remove the turkey from the skillet from the pan and stir in the remaining gravy . reduce heat ; simmer for an additional 20 minutes . pour into hot skillet . serve with remaining sauce over the chicken . serve .
