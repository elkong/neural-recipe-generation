🍴 Caramelized Onion Pudding

 🥑 
 ‣ to make the duck in skillet , cook over medium heat until translucent , about 5 minutes . stir in the vanilla and continue cooking until translucent and is thickened . season with salt and black pepper . cook until the browned and bubbly , about 10 minutes . stir in the pesto , and stir until just incorporated . pour into a servings while the sauce level from the skillet . 
 ‣ place the meat in a large bowl . pour the wine and honey into the slow cooker , and stir in the onion and pork until it is easier paste . season with salt and pepper . stir in the tofu . simmer until very creamy , about 10 minutes .
