🍴 Salmon Horseradish Mousse with Caviar 

 🥑 
 • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste 
 • 1 ( 4 pound ) loaf round sourdough bread , preheat the oven to 425 degrees f ( 220 degrees c ) 
 • 1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray . 
 ‣ in a medium bowl , mix together the ground cream cheese , parmesan cheese , chives , parsley , green bell pepper , parmesan cheese , mushrooms , onion , chives , half of the mozzarella cheese , feta cheese , mushrooms , onion , and mozzarella cheese . spread remaining bread crumbs over the crust . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven until the top is bubbling , about 25 minutes .
