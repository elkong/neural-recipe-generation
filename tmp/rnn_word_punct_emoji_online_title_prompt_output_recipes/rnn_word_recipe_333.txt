🍴 Keith Young's Chicken Cacciatore

 🥑 
 • 1 2 cup heavy seasoned salt 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . 
 ‣ mix mayonnaise , cream cheese , chicken breast , cracker crumbs , brown sugar , salt , and pepper together in a bowl until chicken is no crumbly ; transfer to a bowl . 
 ‣ heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet over medium heat with the hot skillet ; cook and stir onion until softened until translucent and translucent , 5 to 7 minutes . 
 ‣ in a large skillet over medium high heat , saute chicken mixture until tender . 
 ‣ mix in tomatoes , tomatoes , green onions , and egg . cook and stir until the mushrooms are just completely cooked , 15 to 20 minutes . transfer to a bowl . 
 ‣ spread the mixture over the cream cheese layer on both sides and bottom of the skillet . 
 ‣ bake for about 30 minutes in the preheated oven . bake at 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) for 1 hour .
