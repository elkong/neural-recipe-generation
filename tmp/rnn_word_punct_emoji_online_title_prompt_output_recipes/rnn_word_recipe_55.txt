🍴 Heinz 57 Vinaigrette

 🥑 
 • 1 ( 1 pound ) loaf should rise bread , thawed 
 • 12 ounces semisweet chocolate chips 
 
 🥣 
 ‣ preheat oven to 350 degrees f ( 175 degrees c ) . lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan . 
 ‣ in a saucepan over low heat , combine sugar , salt , and butter . cook , stirring , until it is crumbly and crumbly . remove from heat and sprinkle with cheddar cheese . 
 ‣ spoon 1 3 of 2 cups of sour cream into each layer ; pour melted chocolate evenly on top of the crust . 
 ‣ bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes or until the center is set . cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes before removing from the pan . 
 ‣ in a small saucepan over low heat , combine the confectioners ' sugar , rice and water ; stir until smooth . remove from heat . stir in egg , nuts , vanilla extract and pecans . spread over cooled cake . 
 ‣ bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven , or until firm . cool at room temperature for 18 hours before cutting . 
 ‣ combine with confectioners sugar , chives , and white chocolate . top each with ice cream with melted butter . 
 ‣ gently spread frosting over the top of each . drizzle with cream cheese . spread glaze over the top of springform pan .
