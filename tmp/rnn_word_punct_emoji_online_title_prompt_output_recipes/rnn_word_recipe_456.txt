🍴 Parsnip and Apple Soup 

 🥑 
 ‣ in a small saucepan combine bell pepper , red onion , eggs , water , chipotle peppers , raisins , and onion powder . bring to a boil , then reduce heat . simmer for 10 minutes . 
 ‣ remove from heat and stir in chopped nuts . pour into sterilized jars . 
 ‣ continue to 6 hours to release the meat from the edges . 
 ‣ transfer the jars to a slow cooker . stir in the mushrooms with the reserved lid . pour the remaining glaze over the meat in the remaining bowl .
