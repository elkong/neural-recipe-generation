TITLE
Cubano Salad)

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (8 ounce) can sliced pineapple, drained
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1 cup water
• 1 1/2 cups whipping cream
• 1 (15 ounce) can pear syrup, or more if necessary
• 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed and spucked
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1 tablespoon milk or vanilla extract
• 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
• 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (such as Chocolate Gravy)
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together crushed spinach, brown sugar, 1/2 cup water, vegetable oil, eggs, and marshmallows until smooth.
‣ Pour dill mixture into an 8x8 inch baking dish. Sprinkle philled chops with flavored dried cranberries; garnish with remaining sweet cherry pie filling.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Let cool 10 minutes before cutting into cookies.
