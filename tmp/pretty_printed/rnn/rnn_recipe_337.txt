INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pans.
‣ In a small bowl, mix together the half-and-half and 1/2 tablespoon vanilla extract. Spread mixture along with margarine; sprinkle rum over top. Sprinkle with sugar and pepper. Pour the butter mixture over the bread. Place the sheets of wax paper, and sprinkle with brown sugar and pecans. Set aside.
‣ Place a 3-quart casserole dish on top of the bread with the butter. Broil until lightly browned. Remove from the oven and serve on top of tart.
