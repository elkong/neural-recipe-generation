TITLE
James A. Beard's Chilled Irish Coffee Sauce II
• 1 head cabbage where whisked in water for a glass rims of green and candy bubbles, with margarine, and spoon prepared mixture over the top.
• 1 (15.25 ounce) can chicken broth
• 1 1/2 cups white rum
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1 cup water

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium heat. Add chicken and cook until toasted. Stir in green juice and remaining ingredients for several minutes. Stir in corn, green onions and potatoes. Add the chicken and sweet potatoes. Stir until smooth and hot. Bring to a simmer, then cover and simmer 10 minutes more. Serve warm or at room temperature.
