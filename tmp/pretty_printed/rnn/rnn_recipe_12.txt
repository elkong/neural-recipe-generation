TITLE
Garden Veggie Pizza Squares

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
• 1/2 cup butter, melted
• 1 cup sour cream

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place egg yolks in a bowl and toss with warm milk. Mix well; dissolve chocolate in tall second edges of the chocolate until smooth. Add condensed milk and milk and beat well.
‣ In a mixing bowl, beat the egg whites in a mixing bowl until fluffy. Gently fold in the remaining flour, then the vanilla extract.
‣ Pour the filling into the pie crust, poke a 1/4 inch overhang to form a pastry. Sprinkle some candy lightly on top.
‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 to 25 minutes. Turn off the oven, and let cool to room temperature before serving.
