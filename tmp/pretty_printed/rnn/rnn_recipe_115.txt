TITLE
Arugula Salad with Carrot and Yellow Pepper Banana Mama's Spiced Caramel Glaze

INGREDIENTS
• 1 1/2 cups white sugar
• 1/2 cup unsweetened canned black olives, stemmed and chopped
• 1 1/2 cups peeled and chopped walnuts
• 1 (13 ounce) package zettuce.
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 3 tablespoons peanut butter
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 5 tablespoons butter, softened

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8 inch square pan.
‣ In a medium bowl combine the chopped pecans, walnuts, pumpkin puree and the molasses
‣ In a small bowl, sift the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar to a mixing bowl. Add the spinach and white wine. Stir to combine.
‣ In a separate bowl, stir together the crushed, pear, half-and-half, yeast, and pecans. Spread the batter into the prepared baking pan.
‣ Bake for 35 to 45 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbles are tender. Serve hot.
