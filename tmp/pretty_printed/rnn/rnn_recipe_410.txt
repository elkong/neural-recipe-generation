TITLE
Flaming Bananas Foster with Vanilla Ice Cream and Honey Sea Salt Peanuts.
• 1 1/2 cups frozen whipped topping, thawed
• 1/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place cheesecake in large pot or a blender, and process until smooth; place in a small bowl. Whisk together eggs, milk and 1/2 cup of the zest and spoon into mug. Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Arrange 8 large lemon slices over each whipped topping. Place pies on a baking sheet. Place the biscuit pieces in the chocolate pieces and sprinkle with the remaining crumbled cookie spread (optional). Set aside.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until filling is bubbling and crunchy brown, about 30 minutes. Add the almonds and stir until melted.
‣ Pour the cream cheese mixture into the pudding mixture and stir in the chopped walnuts. Pour into prepared serving bowls, and sprinkle with chopped pecans on top. Serve with red wine.
