TITLE
Fusilli Carbonara with Fines Herbes de Provence; Sugar Snap Ice Cola Maria Vers coated Muffins: Whipped cream
• 1 1/2 pounds ground beef

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl, mix together butter, cream of chia seeds, sour cream, cinnamon, vanilla extract, salt and pepper. Pour mixture into pie crust.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 45 minutes, until golden brown. Serve warm.
