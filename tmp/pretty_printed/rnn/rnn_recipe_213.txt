TITLE
Fabulous Fruit Salad III

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Pour 2/3 cup pistachio 1/3 cup butter into the fruit juice. Stir in duck sauce. Cover and refrigerate for 20 minutes.
‣ Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (225 degrees C).
‣ Dissolve cornstarch in the frond topping mixture, repeat with mixer on medium speed to lukewarm the mixture. With a fork, soak the paste until mixture has resemble coarse meal. Place pastry slices in a baking dish.
‣ Pour oil over the springform pan.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes or until heated through and soft.
‣ Allow the strips to cool slightly before serving.
