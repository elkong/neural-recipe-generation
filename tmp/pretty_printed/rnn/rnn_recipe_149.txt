INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl, blend the peanut butter, egg, and sugar until smooth. Stir in the butter until smooth. Fold in the peaches, and pecans. Transfer to the prepared pan.
‣ Turn oven temperature another 10 inch baking pan. Pour into the pie shell.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour, or until well browned. Cool in pan on wire rack. Serve warm.
