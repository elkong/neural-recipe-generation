TITLE
Peppermint Patty Crust pies)

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups white sugar
• 2 cups white sugar
• 2 cups water
• 3 cups whole milk
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 2 1/2 cups peanut butter
• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 3 1/2 cups fresh blueberries
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Combine sugar, apple and vanilla and butter in medium mixing bowl. Combine berries, sugar and vanilla and mix into sugar mixture. Stir in 1 cup confectioners' sugar, 1 cup at a time, until just moistened. Slowly add 1 tablespoon fresh lime zest and 3 tablespoons sugar and continue beating until stiff. Add 1 cup flour and 1/8 teaspoon salt and beat until well blended. Frost the top of each apple with non-stick cooking spray. Drop by rounded pieces for each corn flake top. Using dough sticks, cover surface with foil. Place in preheated oven for 35 minutes. Serve warm.
