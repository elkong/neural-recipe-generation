TITLE
Smoked Macaroni Salad Dressing; Cherry Dill No Macaroni Sauce; Spoon (see Cook's Note)
• 1 (12 ounce) package BBQ Sauce; cooked and drained

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place pineapple in the bottom of a 9x9-inch glass baking dish. Sprinkle with half thyme. Pour over the orange juice. Brush outside of pineapple with egg whites. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup of temperature chopped onion. Place on cookie sheet.
‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes. Remove from oven and let stand in refrigerator until set, about 20 minutes. Cut into bite-size pieces.
