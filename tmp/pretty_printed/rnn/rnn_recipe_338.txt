TITLE
Mediterranean Pasta Salad with Olives and Capers and Rosemary, Arugula

INGREDIENTS
• 1 1/2 pounds ground pork sausage
• 1 cup fresh mushrooms
• 1 cucumber, seeded and diced
• 1 tablespoon minced garlic
• 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
• 1 1/2 teaspoons minced fresh garlic
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 2 1/4 cups dry bread crumbs

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat an oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl, mix together the Gruyere cheese, sour cream, pecans, 1/2 cup water, salt and pepper; mix in the bread chunks until evenly mixed. Drop into the boiling water and stir until well combined and slightly lumpy. Serve.
