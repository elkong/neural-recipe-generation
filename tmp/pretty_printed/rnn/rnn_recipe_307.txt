TITLE
Tomato and Parmesan Tartar Rolls

INGREDIENTS
• 1 1/2 cups white sugar
• 3 eggs, room temperature
• 1 cup milk
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 tablespoon honey
• 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
• 5 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar syrup, for dusting (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
‣ Combine egg and eggs; mix well. Sprinkle coconut all topping over everything on a lightly floured surface; the liquid will be stiff. Add 1/2 cup of milk; stir until combined. Chill 2 hours or overnight.
‣ Mix 1 1/2 cups sugar, 1/2 cup flax seeds and 1 1/2 cups water in a mixing bowl. Beat on low speed until smooth. Stir in the eggs; stir until well blended. Stir in pineapple and walnuts. Drizzle mixture over each pastry lined cookie sheet. Bake 20 minutes in preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted into center of cookie curling rosette or the second layer. Stir every 25 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes.
