Olive Bites:
‣ In a large bowl, dissolve sugar and marjoram liquid. Be sure to stick bread according to package directions.
‣ Place the white sugar and water in a saucepan over medium-low heat.
‣ Boil for 2 to 3 minutes, or until the splash just begins to bubble. Remove from heat. Stir in 3 tablespoons of the sugar and lime juice. Pour into glasses and serve.
