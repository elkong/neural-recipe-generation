INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch pan.
‣ Line two 18-inch pie plate with parchment paper.
‣ Combine eggs, egg whites and cream of tartar in a mixing bowl. Stir in 1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar, 1/2 cup hot water, and 1 1/2 cups flour until well blended. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.
‣ Arrange toasted buns cut side up in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Pour 1/3 of the cream over filling. Pour pie on top, and sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup chopped sugar.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes more. Let cool.
