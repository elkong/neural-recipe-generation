TITLE
Juliana's Surprise Sandwich Cookies

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/2 cup water
• 1 (3 ounce) package sweetened cream cheese food coloring (White Chocolate ice Cream Tea cornstarch)
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 2 1/2 cups white sugar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
• 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line muffin cups with paper spray.
‣ Sift flour, sugar, salt, egg, brown sugar and salt into a separate bowl add the egg. Mix thoroughly, then combine the egg yolks and sugar with your fingers.
‣ Turn on low speed, and stir in the evaporated milk, Parmesan cheese, coconut, and cake mix.
‣ Divide the chocolate mixture into the prepared muffin cups. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes or until golden brown and a meat thermometer on top.
