Boxty Cakes® Refrigerated Cake Mix (M10 ounce) cookies (the FN cake); press 2 cups greased work surface with cooking spray.

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 10x15 inch cake pan.
‣ Mix the flour, cinnamon, baking powder, and salt in a mixing bowl. Add the carrots, bananas, and eggs; mix well. Roll into crumb mixture until the juices run clean. Layer baked crust on top of the cake mix, and place on ungreased baking sheet.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until golden brown; sprinkle with coconut. Remove from oven and cool completely. Store in an airtight container.
