INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan.
‣ Mix together the butter, milk, and eggs together.
‣ In a separate bowl, beat the eggs one at a time, with the egg yolk and whipped topping. Stir in the mashed banana, milk and vanilla. Combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and ginger in a separate bowl until well blended. Mix in the chocolate chips and nuts. Spoon into prepared pans.
