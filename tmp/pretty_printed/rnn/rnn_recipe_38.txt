Donna's Lasagna Chips
‣ Pour 10 ounces evaporated milk and 2 cups flour and 2 tablespoons butter over medium heat for 15 minutes. Boil for 10 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Beat milk, sugar, and vanilla in a mixing bowl; beat until light. Stir in mashed banana, mixing just until smooth. Pour into prepared muffin cups on the prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until the tops are lightly browned, 25 to 30 minutes. Let cool in the pan for 15 minutes before serving.
