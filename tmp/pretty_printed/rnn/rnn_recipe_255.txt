TITLE
Peas and Prosciutto Mix with Cherry BBQ Sauce and Spinach Filling
• 8 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
• 1/2 cup whipping cream
• 1/3 cup chopped pecans
• 3/4 cup pecan halves

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring a pot of water to a boil; pour in the salted water. Bring to a boil over high heat. Mix in brown sugar and peanut butter, cook until almost tender, about 10 minutes. Drain peas; reserve. Pour fruit preserves over the peaches; sprinkle with cooking spray. Stir in the remaining 1/2 cup cheese and 1/2 cup of the eggs. Pour into a 9x13 inch pan. Pour the remaining apricot pieces over the filling. Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of spiced over the peach pieces. Sprinkle crushed pepperoni with Parmesan cheese.
‣ Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean. Allow to cool, and serve.
