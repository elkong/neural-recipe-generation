TITLE
Harry's Uni Shooter, Mozzarella and Spiced Tea Bay Sweet Potatoes with Apple Butter Sauce

INGREDIENTS
• 1/2 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1 tablespoon minced garlic
• 1 1/2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
• 2 cloves garlic, minced
• 2 (10 ounce) packages fresh Mushrooms, peeled and chopped

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place the pumpkin pie spice in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil, and cook over medium heat until the sugar melts, about 5 minutes. Stir in the half-and-half, red pepper flakes, salt, pepper, and bay leaf. Bring to a boil, then stir in the water and salt. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the syrins are tender, about 10 minutes. Stir in the Worcestershire sauce and basil. Simmer uncovered for 3 to 4 minutes, until reduced to a syrup.
‣ Stir in the half-and-half and the cream. Serve hot.
