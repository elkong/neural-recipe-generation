White Chocolate Pound Cake IV

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan. Sift together 1/2 cup butter, 1/2 cup sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Pour in sour cream, buttermilk, and egg yolk into mixture and stir until smooth. Stir in chilled red food coloring, stirring until well blended. Beat in milk and egg; mix well. Add buttermilk and buttermilk; continue beating until moistened. Stir in 1/2 cup flour mixture. Pour batter into prepared pans.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 minutes. Cool slightly, then return to the oven. Bake until set, about 1 hour. Store in an airtight container.
