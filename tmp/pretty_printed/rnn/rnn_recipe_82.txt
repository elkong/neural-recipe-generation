TITLE
Chicken Marbella Strips)

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Stir the cream of mushroom soup mix and 3 tablespoons vegetable stock into a large pot with ear of chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
‣ Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook until tender, about 10 minutes; drain.
‣ Meanwhile, place the chicken breasts into the bottom of a large saucepan and pour in the tomato sauce; bring to a simmer over medium heat. Reduce heat to medium, and simmer until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the potatoes are tender, about 12 minutes. Drain off the excess grease. Transfer the chicken to a large bowl; discard the soaking liquid. Stir in the cooked pasta and milk powder. Pour the pasta and mix into the prepared baking dish and pour into the baking dish and sprinkle with green onions. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes more.
