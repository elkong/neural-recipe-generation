TITLE
White Yeast Loaves

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
• 1 cup water
• 1 1/2 cups white sugar
• 1/3 cup honey
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract; reserve extra-virgin olive oil
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Combine pumpkin and white sugar in a small bowl; stir in milk; stir until a bubble can have about 1/2 cup at a time. Spread the remaining chocolate chip in a small bowl. Combine the rhubarb, water, sugar and salt in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in the vanilla and the Marsala water; stir to blend.
‣ Pour mixture into 2 cans halfway with greased loaf pan.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until top is golden brown, about 20 minutes. Let cool slightly before slicing and serving.
