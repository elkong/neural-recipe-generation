TITLE
Honey Nut Banana Splits with Warm Milk Vanilla Glaze

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
• 3 tablespoons cornstarch
• 1 tablespoon vegetable oil or more if needed or fresh red food coloring or apple juice
• 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
• 2 cups plus 2 tablespoons buttermilk
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
• 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper, or to taste
• ground nutmeg to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Combine the milk, sugar, orange juice, and butter in a small saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir until thick and creamy, 7 to 7 minutes. Set aside to cool.
‣ Meanwhile, whisk the whole egg, the sour cream, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract together in a separate bowl. Stir in the butter, 1/4 cup of the sugar, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla, then pour in the beaten egg. Fold in the confectioners' sugar and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla.
