TITLE
Melt-in-Your-Mouth Braised and Barbecued Chicken Stockpot, or Manu-Hurrout 90X

INGREDIENTS
• 4 bacon strips; patted dry
• 1 bunch green onions, sliced
• 1 bunch green onions, chopped
• 3 cloves garlic, crushed
• 1 teaspoon chili powder
• 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
• 2 teaspoons hot sauce

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat grill for medium-high heat.
‣ Place the beef steak on a plate and set over medium-low heat. Season the bottom with garlic salt, garlic powder, onion pepper, and ground black pepper. Cook until the meat is cooked through, 3 to 5 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 145 degrees F (74 degrees C). Let the chicken rest 10 minutes before serving.
