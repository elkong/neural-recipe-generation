TITLE
Indian-Style Vegetable Rice Pilaft Chocolate Cover
• 1/2 cup milk
• 1 1/2 cups water, or as needed
• 1 tablespoon rice fluke or more if needed
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring the wine to a boil in a saucepan. Cook over medium-low heat until the chicken is lightly browned, about 10 minutes. Remove from the heat, and set aside.
‣ Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken; cook and stir until melted, about 5 minutes. Add the chicken broth, and the chicken; continue cooking until the sauce thickens, 2 to 3 more minutes. Stir in the chopped spinach so loose pan often. Add the chicken and butter, and stir until melted. Stir in the cream. Simmer for another 15 minutes, until the cheese has melted; add the chicken stock and continue to cook until the cheese melts and the sauce is bubbly, about 5 minutes.
