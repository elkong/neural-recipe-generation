TITLE
Yellow Rice Style Spritz Coated Spring Milk
• 1/2 cup water
• 1 (10 ounce) package frozen cooked pineapple chunks (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (135 degrees C).
‣ Place pork in a mug or 9 inch pie pan. Sprinkle with cinnamon, salt and pepper.
‣ Place peaches in the prepared dish. Season with cayenne pepper. Pour mixture into pan and make a paste gelatin mixture over baked pie crust.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and bake an additional 10 minutes. This should reach at least 20 minutes. Strain tortillas through a fine sieve into a bowl.
‣ Pour in all of their juice; toss to coat. Adjust seasoning and serve.
