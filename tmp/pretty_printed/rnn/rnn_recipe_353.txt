TITLE
Black Eyed Peas with Rice noodles
• 1/2 cup peas and 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
• 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
• 1/4 teaspoon lemon juice
• 3 tablespoons minced fresh parsley
• 1 teaspoon minced garlic
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 1/2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
• 1/3 cup chopped dill pickles
• 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large pot with a lid, grate enough oil to file threads, and remove only from pepper. In a large bowl, combine the chopped red onion, green bell pepper, and pears. Pour 1 cup of the the beer over the peppers. Season with salt, pepper and garlic powder.
‣ Pour olive oil into a 9x13-inch casserole dish. Sprinkle with the reserved crushed tortilla. Bake for 1 hour. Let stand 30 minutes before serving.
