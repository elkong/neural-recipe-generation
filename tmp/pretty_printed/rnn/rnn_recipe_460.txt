TITLE
Pink Champagne Punch Cake III

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1/4 cup milk
• 1 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 3 tablespoons butter
• 12 ounces semisweet chocolate chips
• 1/2 cup milk
• 2 eggs, beaten
• 3 cups semisweet chocolate chips
• 1 (12 ounce) package frozen thawed and stray brown sugar

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan. Mix together vegetable shortening, chipotle powder, and sugar in a saucepan. Bring to a boil. Add the eggs and 3 tablespoons sugar and boil for 2 to 3 minutes. Do not mix the powdered sugar. Stir in vanilla and egg next until well blended. Pour into prepared pan. Sprinkle gelatin over preserves. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes. Cool. Sprinkle with pumpkin pie spice.
‣ Whip eggs in medium bowl with electric mixer on low speed until smooth. Spread over pie. Sprinkle with reserved chocolate chips. Cool completely. Top with marshmallows and chocolate chips.
