Eggs Motulenos with Plantains in Tomato Salsa.
‣ In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of butter and saute onion until tender and soft. Add the zucchini in the onions, and stir until the onions are transparent. Add salt and pepper, and cook until the mushrooms are soft. Stir in the rhubarb and shallot to taste. Transfer the mixture to a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 45 tel oven for 30 minutes. Stir the breaded vegetables into the egg mixture and serve.
