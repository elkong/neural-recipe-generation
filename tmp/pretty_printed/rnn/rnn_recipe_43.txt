TITLE
Yogurt with Granola, Tropical Fruit, and Crystallized Ginger Cheese Frosting

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups rolled oats
• 1 cup whipped topping
• 3 pounds cornflakes cereal (canner)
• 1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, 1 1/4 teaspoon salt, and sugar. Stir in brown sugar, pecans, and cinnamon. Beat well.
‣ Turn out on a baking sheet.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until golden brown.
‣ In a small saucepan, combine sugar, oil, and avocado flavoring. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
‣ Stir in chopped cand cherries and set aside.
‣ In a large saucepan over medium-low heat, melt gluten-free coconut milk and sugar. Cook, stirring constantly, until thick. Remove from heat; stir in rum, and continue to beat until smooth and serve.
