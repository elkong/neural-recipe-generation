TITLE
Savory Sausage Toad-in-the-Hole) Pork Back Pumpkin Pudding

INGREDIENTS
• 1 1/3 cups milk
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1 teaspoon almond extract
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 cup water for decoration

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a mixing bowl, sift together 1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar, 1/2 cup butter, and cornstarch. Beat until smooth. Pour lemon juice into pan and cover with water. Stir over low heat until mixture comes to a boil. Remove from heat and stir in orange juice. Cool for 10 minutes, then stir in cream of potato mixture. Bring to a boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Add potatoes and simmer 5 minutes or until melted. Pour in the condensed milk to lightly stir.
‣ Pour over sugar mixture in the pan. Spoon over bread slices and enjoy!
