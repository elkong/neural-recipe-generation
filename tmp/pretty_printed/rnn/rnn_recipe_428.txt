TITLE
Grilled Romaine with Balsamic Dressing: Cooking oats, chopped asparagus, sherry wine, ketchup, 2 tablespoons ketchup, water, and lemon juice
• 2 tablespoons honey
• 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
• 3 tablespoons rice vinegar (not sugar)
• 1/3 cup brown sugar, red wine vinegar
• 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes, or to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Blend shortening, soy sauce, and cilantro in a blender until smooth.
‣ Pour mixed vegetable mixture over squash, and season with salt and black pepper. Toss, and refrigerate overnight.
‣ Preheat grill for medium-high plastic and oven temperature or heat. Brush the glaze with olive oil. Grill about 1 to 2 minutes, until golden brown. Remove from the grill and let stand 5 minutes. Stir the lemon juice and orange juice into the garlic sauce. Garnish with remaining pepper to taste.
