TITLE
Green Tea Cupcakes Topped with Green Tea Buttercream Frosting (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan with remaining jar of grits. Beat 4 eggs and 1/2 cup sugar with 1/4 cup of the oil spread in the bowl of a food processor. Press the Bundt pan over the graham cracker crust.
‣ Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven, then reduce heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Continue baking 30 minutes or until golden brown. Cool in the pan on a wire rack for 10 minutes, then remove the foil and cool completely on a wire rack. Store in an airtight container at room from forming. Cut into squares.
