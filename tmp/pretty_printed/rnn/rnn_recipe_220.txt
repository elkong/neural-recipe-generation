TITLE
Easy Baked Pumpkin Pudding Bars

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cup confectioners' sugar
• 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
• 1/2 cup butter, softened
• 1 cup whipping cream
• 1 cup chocolate candies
• 3/4 cup sesame seeds (or sliced apples), peeled and thinly sliced
• 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch pan with foil.
‣ In a large bowl, combine chocolate chips, milk, and cinnamon. Beat with an electric mixer (or in a large bowl with an electric mixer) beat well. Beat in eggs, whipping cream, and vanilla. Beat in eggs and sour cream; beat until smooth. Spoon into prepared pan.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven 25 minutes, or until cake tests rolled on top. Cool in pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely.
