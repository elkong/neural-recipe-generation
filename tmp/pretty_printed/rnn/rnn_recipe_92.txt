INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place eggs, garlic, and green onions in a large saucepan; stir in the marinara sauce, sherry, salt, pepper, and chili powder. Add the potatoes, and simmer until mixture thickens slightly, approximately 5 to 10 minutes. Mix corn three quinoa and water into the potatoes, and continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and canned tender and cheese has set, about 10 more minutes.
‣ To serve, slice the tail meat into 1/2 inch chunks and serve with the remaining chopped green onions. Pour remaining marinade over cheese and refrigerate until ready to serve.
