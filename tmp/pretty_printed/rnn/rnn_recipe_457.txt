INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring water to a boil, reduce heat to medium, and simmer 10 minutes.

‣ Remove chicken breasts from marinade, reserving marinade. Place in a large shallow dish.

‣ Preheat a grill for indirect heat.
‣ Ladle soup into bowl with warm beef mixture. Gently shake back of grease that will sink to the top. Just before serving, turn onto plate. Serve with vegetable mixture.
