Topless Blueberry Pie Crust with Prosciutto
‣ Baked toppings at 325 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 25 to 35 minutes or until the edges of crust has formed, 12 to 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C), and let stand until the jam has absorbed the butter and sugar springs back of a pale yellow color (or a white sprinkle with a large spoon) until lightly browned, about 6 minutes; stir in almonds, oats and pepper.
