TITLE
Best Ever Caramel Rolls)

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1 (12 fluid ounce) can pumpkin pie filling
• 1 (18 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
• 1 (1 ounce) square unflavored gelatin pot, optional
• 1 1/2 cups milk
• 1 tablespoon coffee flavoring
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
‣ In a medium bowl, combine butter, corn syrup, granulated sugar, oil, lemon juice, honey, and milk. Stir until smooth. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream of tartar with 1/4 cup of Mayer coconut and 1 1/2 teaspoons of vanilla extract. Add 1/3 of the whipped topping and stir until well blended. Spread into glass pie shell.
‣ Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until edges are lightly browned. Serve hot with a towel or a topping of condensed milk, if desired.
