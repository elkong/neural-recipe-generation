TITLE
Sprightly Lemon Vinaigrette Mix; Cover and shot New Mixer Spoon Marshmallow
• 1 (1.5 ounce) package instant French vanilla pudding mix
• 1 (1 ounce) square-stemmed Apple Pie II Moratina BINES(Starting Cookies®)

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Pour 1 cup flour over flour to cover. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup chopped bananas, if desired. Gently stir in crust, and 1/2 cup water. Batter will be used. Pour the melted butter over the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Sprinkle 1/2 cup butter or margarine over the top.
‣ Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let stand 10 minutes before cut into bars.
