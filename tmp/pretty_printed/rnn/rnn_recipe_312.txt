TITLE
Bacon Topped Holiday Apple Pie I

INGREDIENTS
• 1 1/2 cups white sugar
• 1 1/2 cups water
• 2 tablespoons butter, melted
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 2 cups milk
• 1 cup water
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Combine 1 1/2 cups grated cheddar cheese, corn flakes and 1 cup white sugar, 1/2 cup water and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract in a saucepan; bring to a boil. Mix in 3/4 cup sugar, 1 1/2 cups water, 1 cup of sugar, and 1 teaspoon vanilla and 1/4 cup water in a small bowl. Mix in the apricot preserves and cinnamon.
‣ Drop dough by heaping spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets. Sprinkle with confectioners' sugar. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until set.
