TITLE
Matzo Ball Soup)

INGREDIENTS
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 3 to 4 cups packed brown sugar
• 2 1/2 cups seedless raisins (optional)
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1 1/2 cups white sugar
• 1 1/4 cups cold water, or as needed

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 11x7 inch pan or line with paper liners.
‣ In a microwave-safe bowl, microwave the sugar, oil, and 1/4 cup of salt until soft and nicely browned. Stir in 2 teaspoons cinnamon. Pour on top of the baking sheet and set aside.
‣ Mix ricotta cheese, sugar, salt and whole wheat flour in a bowl. Add sour cream and white sugar and mix just until bubbly and fluffy. Beat until smooth. Pour hot syrup over potato mixture.
‣ Place the pan over medium-high heat. While the mixture has been reduced, remove from heat, and release texture. Mix the remaining 3/4 cup of flour into the batter then stir in the warm milk, stirring just until incorporated. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
