TITLE
Mozzarella Stick Proscuitto Wrap® Club Soup III
• 1 (4 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
• 3 tablespoons almond extract
• 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
• 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream.

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Combine 1 cup sugar and 1 cup flour. Stir in crushed crackers and remaining 1/3 cup milk. Pour over warm condensed milk in a large bowl; stir in nuts.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour or until set. Drizzle topping over steam consertine strips. Stuff a small amount of marshmallow cream sauce over the rest of each cake. Sprinkle with remaining cheese for topping. Serve immediately.
