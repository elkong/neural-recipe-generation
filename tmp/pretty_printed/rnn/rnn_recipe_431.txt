TITLE
Fiery Pepper Chicken Crab Salad with ORLEROPOR Soy Pimento Sauce, recipe follows
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1/4 cup butter or margarine
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or more to taste
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti sauce, or as needed

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl, combine bread crumbs, garlic, pepper and garlic. Mix well. Spray pizza stone with non-stick cooking spray. Pour 1/2 cup butter over peppers, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and parsley.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes. This should read 125 degrees F (235 degrees C).
‣ Bake for an additional 5 minutes, or until heated through. Serve.
