TITLE
Chicken and Cold Noodles with Spicy Sauce) Thawed Dipped Spinach
• 1 cup sour cream
• 3 tablespoons butter, melted
• 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
• 1 1/2 cups 2% milk chocolate chips
• salt and black pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine the brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, onion, celery, red pepper, nutmeg, white vinegar, and black pepper. Place over high heat. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium, and cover and simmer 20 minutes.
‣ Meanwhile, in a large glass or ceramic bowl, mix together the coconut, onion, and chicken broth. Pour in the soup and water. Mix well.
‣ Spread onto a baking sheet.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour, or until heated through and cans brown. (Note: Be careful!)
‣ Pour the browned potato pieces over the chicken and return to the oven for about 2 minutes. Serve warm directly from the top of the soup immediately.
