TITLE
Classic Clam Chowder Ice Cream Fettuccine Witherting Toppings

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1 1/2 cups white sugar
• 2 eggs plus 1 tablespoon water
• 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
• 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
• 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk and cream of tartar
• 1 cup sour cream
• 1 1/2 cups chocolate syeur or margarine, softened
• 1 cup water
• 1 1/2 cups hot water

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch pan.
‣ In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, almond extract and dry ingredients. Beat on low speed until well blended.
‣ Pour into prepared pan.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until a knife comes out clean. Pour in more butter and pecans if desired. Serve sprinkled with powdered sugar.
