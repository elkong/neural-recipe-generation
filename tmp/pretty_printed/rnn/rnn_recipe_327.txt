Warm Crab Dip Italian Cannelop Pasta also

• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

• 1/2 teaspoon salt

• 1 cup chopped celery

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x9 inch baking dish.
‣ Mix the melted butter into the beaten eggs and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
‣ In a large bowl, combine the mozzarella, salt, sugar, onion, celery, mayonnaise, Cheddar cheese, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine. Pour mixture over the bread and fold together and spread over the bread pudding. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, then lower heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes. Remove from oven and spoon herb mixture over the top. Cover and cook 20 minutes on High pressure. Serve hot.
