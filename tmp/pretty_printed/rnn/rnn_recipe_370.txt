Spicy Ranch Dressing) with canned pumpkin puree

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat 2 tablespoons water in saucepan over low heat until mixture boils. Set aside in skillet. Set Zingerbon at preheat broiler, and lightly grease a 2 quart casserole dish. Arrange two slices of cream cheese over the ham. Sprinkle with chopped nutmeg. Sprinkle with paprika, and salt and mince on top for the remaining noodles and season with salt and pepper. Stir in the parmesan cheese and 1/3 cup cherries. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Sprinkle ranch drippings on top of each toast and serve.
