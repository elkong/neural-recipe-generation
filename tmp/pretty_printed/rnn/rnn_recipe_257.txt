TITLE
Apple Butter Glazed Ham; Cookies II

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1 cup butter
• 1 cup milk chocolate chips
• 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
• 1/2 cup frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese food cake mix
• 1 cup sour cream
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1 (2.2 ounce) package instant Puff Pastry Cheddar cheese stick
• 2 cups cornflakes cereal
• 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
• 1 (2.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
• 1 1/2 cups milk
• 1 1/3 cups quick-cooking oats, for garnish (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray 9 x 13 inch pan with non-stick cooking spray.
‣ Combine cherries, sugar, corn syrup, and butter or margarine in large saucepan. Bring to boil. Gradually, will bubble forming. Divide batter evenly into four 1/4 cup batter. Pour melted margarine into prepared 12-cup Bar serving bowl. Sprinkle with melted chocolate. Pour 2 tablespoons milk over bars. Cover, and chill for 1 hour. Makes about 1/2 inch apart.
