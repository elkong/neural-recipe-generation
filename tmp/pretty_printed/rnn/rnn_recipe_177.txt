TITLE
Zucchini Parmesan, Lasagna-Style Duck; Rotisserie Oatmeal, divided Peppermint Relish-Style Cheesecake Cheese Frosting

INGREDIENTS
• 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/2 cup butter, chilled 1 1/2 cup milk
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 2 eggs
• 1 egg, beaten
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a medium bowl, stir together the brown sugar, flour, ginger, and cinnamon. Set aside.
‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter, shortening, softened butter, shortening and sugar. Using your hands, cook in the eggs, one at a time, until it is light and fluffy. Add egg, milk and vanilla. Stir in the eggs one at a time. Fold in the crumbled crackers, and pour into the prepared pan.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until the top is golden brown and the center comes out clean, about 15 minutes.
