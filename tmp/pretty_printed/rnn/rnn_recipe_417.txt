INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x9 inch pan.
‣ Melt margarine in a large saucepan over medium heat. Beat cream with milk and vanilla until smooth.
‣ In a large pot over medium heat, combine the eggs and water. Pour the butter mixture into the egg mixture. Stir until the cream do not longer stiff. Fold in the nuts and the butter and lemon zest and pour into serving bowl or 2 wafers. Top with pecan strokes on the side of the pan. Bake in a preheated 350 degree F (175 degrees C) oven for 25 to 30 minutes, until bubbling. Cool on a wire rack. Serve warm.
