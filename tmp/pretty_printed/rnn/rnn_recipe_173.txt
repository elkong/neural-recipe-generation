INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch pan. Mix the rum-flavored gelatin powder, apple juice, soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a bowl until the mixture starts to come halfway up. Do not over beat. Pour into an ungreased 9x5 inch loaf pan.
