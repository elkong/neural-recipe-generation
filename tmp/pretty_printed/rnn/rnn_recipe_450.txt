TITLE
Vanilla Creme Brulee

INGREDIENTS
• 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
• 1/2 cup butter, melted
• 1 tablespoon butter
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
