TITLE
Mushroom and Grape Crostini with Italian Dust Cereal Mushroom Soup Soup

INGREDIENTS
• 1 egg, beaten
• salt and pepper to taste
• 1 teaspoon dried parsley
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
• 1/4 cup flaked coconut

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat the oven't pan from the heat. Preheat a grill to medium heat.
‣ In a medium bowl, stir together the butter, sour cream, and sour cream in a small bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Pour the tomato sauce over the bread cubes.
‣ Bring the butter and remaining 1/4 cup of water to a boil over medium-high heat. Stir in the rhubarb and Cajun seasoning. Remove from heat and stir in the chives. Place the turkey in the casserole dish and brush with remaining 1/2 cup olive oil. Sprinkle the top with Parmesan cheese.
‣ Cover, and refrigerate overnight.
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and place the spaghetti sauce in the center of the oven. Place the casserole dish on a baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through, about 15 minutes.
