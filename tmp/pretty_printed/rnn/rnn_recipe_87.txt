TITLE
Chestnut, Lentils and Vegetable Stew III
• 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
• 1 tablespoon cornstarch
• 1 pint heavy cream
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/2 teaspoon alteant finely chopped pecans

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat oven to 350 degrees F ( 190 degrees C). Grease an 8x8 inch pan.
‣ In large bowl, combine chocolate chips, 1/2 cup butter, chocolate chips, and 1 cup brown sugar. Beat well; stir in vanilla extract. Spread over the cake batter.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes or until done. Serve directly in chocolate sprinkled over cookies and serve.
