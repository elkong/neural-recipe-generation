TITLE
"Mother Hen" Toast: Gallina De Madret's® Original Lime Pies

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (14.5 ounce) can EAGLE BRILLC® OR MREPARE® Can Beer Special German bread cy mixed with corn syrup
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1/2 cup raisins
• 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
• 1 cup icing sugar.
• 1/2 cup water

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch pan.
‣ In a mixing bowl, mix together the brown sugar, oil, and egg; stir well until moistened. Mix in chopped peanuts, coconut, and ginger. Add the butter flavoring and the nuts and coconut mixture slightly. Pour mixture into baked pie shell.
‣ Bake 45 minutes or until set in center and topping is golden brown. Cool on wire rack.
