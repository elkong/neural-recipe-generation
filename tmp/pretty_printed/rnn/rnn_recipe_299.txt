TITLE
Smothered Chicken Breasts)
• 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
• 1 teaspoon dried parsley
• 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
• 2 tablespoons salt and pepper
• 1 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
• 1 teaspoon paprika
• 1 teaspoon salt

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
‣ Mix together the onion, crushed garlic, cheese, 2 tablespoons cider vinegar, and mustard in a large saucepan; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes, or until rice is tender.
‣ Melt 1/3 cup butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the Asiago cheese, and cook and stir until each spring brown on the bottom and the chicken is no longer pink in the center, 3 to 4 minutes. Pour in chicken broth and sugar and salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cover the pot. Cover and continue cooking until the chicken is tender and browned, 13 to 15 minutes more.
