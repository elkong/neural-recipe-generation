TITLE
Indian-Spiced Nuts With Coconut Syrup.
‣ Bring 1 1/2 cups water to a boil in a small saucepan over medium-high heat.
‣ Cool strips 12 minutes, then break into 1/2-inch slices. In a medium bowl, combine raisins, cream, syrup, and oats; stir until well-blended.
‣ Mix crumbled cookies and nuts into one cup of shortening with a spoon; do not over whip. After 5 to 10 minutes in spoon should be firm and sticks to the bottom of the waffle iron; cool completely.
‣ Using an electric mixer, beat shortening and sugar in a mixing bowl until crumbly; stir in 1/2 cup flour mixture; sift together shortening and 1/2 cup white sugar; add coconut milk. Return to the freezer and freeze according to manufacturer's directions.
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper; top with remaining 1 1/2 cups bread cubes and 1 tablespoon graham cracker crumbs.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until topping is lightly browned, 20 minutes.
