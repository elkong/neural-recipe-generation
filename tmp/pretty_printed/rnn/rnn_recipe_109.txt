Favorite Jell-O® Pie Crust with Lemon Crocker Muffins, Pitted with 1/2 cup finely chopped fresh ginger and 2 tablespoons pure maple syrup
• 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese; store-bought white frozen prepared spinach and 1/2 cup peanut butter

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (120 degrees C).
‣ Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add pumpkin puree and sugar and stir until mixture forms a colored, stirring occasionally, about 20 minutes. Drain well.
‣ Whisk together the eggs, adding 1/2 teaspoon salt, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon. Pour into pie shell. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Let cool 30 minutes before serving.
