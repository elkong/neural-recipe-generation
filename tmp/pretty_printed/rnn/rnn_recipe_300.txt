TITLE
Double Berry Custard Pie X
• 1 (12 ounce) package frozen strawberries, thawed
• 1 cup Dijon mustard
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1/2 cup butter, melted
• 1 (16 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 2 1/4 cups breakfast strawberries, thawed
• 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Mix together the raisins, 1/4 cup butter or margarine, pumpkin and 1 cup chopped pecans. In a separate bowl, stir together the crushed spices and white chocolate chips, orange zest and juice. Toss together the brown sugar, cream of tartar and 1/4 cup confectioners' sugar in a mixing bowl. Add the butter, buttermilk, flour, and salt; mix well. Mix until well blended.
‣ Pour mixture into prepared pan. Pour water into prepared pan. Spread peanut butter onto the top. Crumb to generously sprinkle top with remaining 1 cup Pills of brown sugar. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until done. Serve pudding with butterscotch chips.
