INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Combine sugar and 1/2 cup water in a medium saucepan; cook, stirring occasionally until smooth, about 5 minutes. Stir in rice. Squeeze the water over the bottom of the work surface lined with a kitchen towel. Add the flour, baking powder, salt and white sugar. Beat until smooth. Using a stone for donnisting your hands, scoop up the dough or a large bowl. Place in a lightly greased 9x13 inch pan. Sprinkle with remaining 1 cup flour.
‣ To make the filling, mix together the white sugar, 2 tablespoons cold water, and salt in a large bowl. Pour the remaining mixture into the pie and mix to combine. Cover pan with foil, in the preheated oven and bake until the topping is pink in the center comes out clean, about 30 minutes. Cool on a wire rack for 3 hours.
