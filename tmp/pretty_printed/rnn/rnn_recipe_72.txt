Dark Carrot Raisin Muffins with Fresh Pecans, and or an 8-inch pie shell.
‣ Combine 4 cups whipped topping and 1 tablespoon cold butter. Mix well, and stir in milk or pecans, butter, and creamed together. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Pour 1/4 cup granoli onto baking pan; replace the ends together. Roll the puff pastry over the top crust and place on baking sheet.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until the sugar begins to flake and bread is brown at the edges, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely before slicing.
‣ Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil grate. Cut into four 6-inch squares and shape each into 8 squares. Grill until toasted, 3 to 4 minutes. They should be crunchy from sticking to the bottom. Place a piece of skin-side up on a baking sheet.
