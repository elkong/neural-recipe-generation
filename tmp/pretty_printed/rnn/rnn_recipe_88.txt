TITLE
Kiki's Spiced Habanero Peach Jam)

INGREDIENTS
• 1 1/2 cups peanut oil for frying
• 1/3 cup soy sauce
• 1/2 cup water
• 1 1/2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
• 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
• 1 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1 tablespoon white vinegar
• 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
• 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
• 1 (16 ounce) jar marinated carrot juice
• 1/2 cup chicken broth
• 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
• 1/3 cup thawed fruit
• 1/4 cup margarine

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat 1 tablespoon of butter in a large skillet over high heat. Stir in the garlic and ginger, and cook and stir until tender and the edges are translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in the bouillon, curry paste, and lime juice; simmer 2 minutes.
‣ Stir in the corn, peas, spice mix, and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Beat until smooth. Add the sour cream and the cornstarch and cook, stirring constantly, until mixture is thickened, 5 to 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Divide the feta cheese mixture among the lined baking pans, and sprinkle with more salt and pepper to taste.
‣ Bake, uncovered, 15 minutes more.
