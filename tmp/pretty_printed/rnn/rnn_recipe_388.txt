TITLE
Joey's Power-Granola Bars
‣ Dip bottom of pan with non-stick spray or peanut butter; a few drops of water or blue cheese to make 2 large munch balls. Press a small hole into the top of each cookie, then place on plate. Sprinkle top with chopped orange peel.
‣ Place pieces on slow cooker. Top with and sprinkle with red pepper flakes.
‣ Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C), or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean. Allow to cool.
