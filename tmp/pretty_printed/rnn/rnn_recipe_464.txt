TITLE
Bistro Steamed Clams with Roasted Shallots and Saffron Glaze
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and chopped (optional)
• 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
• 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
• 1 tablespoon cornstarch
• 1 tablespoon honey
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 1-quart casserole dish.
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ In a small bowl, mix the green onions, mustard, salt, and pepper. Cover the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan with butter and fill with lighten minute. Repeat layers, reserving about half the cola mixture. Arrange one-third of the filling in the bottom of the pan, and pour the remaining butter over the root. Bake in the preheated oven on top half of the preheated oven until sauce is bubbling and the cheese has melted and bubbling, about 30 minutes.
