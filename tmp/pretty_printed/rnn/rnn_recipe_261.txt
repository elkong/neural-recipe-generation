TITLE
PIE CRUSTRERT
• 2 1/2 cups water
• 1 1/2 cups sweetened dried cranberries (optional)
• 1/4 cup granulated sugar (optional)
• 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, melted
• 1 (14 ounce) package individually wrapped carrots, celery, fresh mushrooms, 8 ounces each about 8 inches (12 ounce) pears, thawed and chopped
• 1 1/2 cups crushed pineapple with jar sauce
• 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degreect spray).
‣ In a large bowl, combine rice, oats, sugar, and pepper; mix well. Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cayenne pepper, and cinnamon. Sprinkle over peach pieces and stir in milk. Press into the bottom of a 9 inch springform pan. Bake in preheated oven for 55 minutes, or until pie is set. Let cool in pan for 15 minutes before slicing.
