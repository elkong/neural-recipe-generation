TITLE
Bacon Wrapped Water Chestnuts II

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (10.5 ounce) cans sweetened condensed cream of margarine
• 2 1/2 cups white sugar
• 1 1/2 cups chopped candied cherries
• 10 cups milk chocolate chips
• 2 tablespoons white sugar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 cup butter flavored chocolate chips

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Melt butter or margarine over low heat. Stir in just until chocolate is melted. Stir in butter and milk until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Beat eggs and beat in flour, then stir 1/2 of the egg mixture into cooking water. Fold in walnuts. Pour over crust.
‣ Bake in preheated oven until sauce is set, about 30 minutes more. Let cool then cut into bite-size pieces.
