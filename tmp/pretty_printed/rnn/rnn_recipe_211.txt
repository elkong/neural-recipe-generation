TITLE
German Pork Chops and Sauerkraut with Steak Sauce)
• 1 cup barbeque sauce mixture, divided
• 1 1/2 cups chopped tomatoes, peeled and chopped

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add the bay leaf and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente. Drain and set aside.
‣ Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic, lemon juice, and hot pepper sauce. Pour in marinade, scraping stew to seal and mash. Stir well, and add the pasta and thyme. Simmer until cheese has melted, about 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cover seam side.
