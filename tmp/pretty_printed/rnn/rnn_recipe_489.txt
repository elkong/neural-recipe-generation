TITLE
Uncle Monkey Smoothie)

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
• 1 cup milk
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 (8 ounce) package semisweet chocolate chips
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1/4 teaspoon almond extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large mixing bowl, combine sugar, 1 tablespoon coconut flavoring, sugar and boiling water. Bring to a boil, then pour over chocolate and set aside.
‣ In separate bowl, add butter or margarine, sugar, and sugar. Mash with a pinch of salt, beat until well incorporated.
‣ Whisk mixture into butter mixture until well blended. Thin with fork at low speed just until combined.
‣ In a medium bowl, stir together the sugar, cookie mix and vanilla. Add zucchini, and stir until smooth. Spread on cooled cake.
