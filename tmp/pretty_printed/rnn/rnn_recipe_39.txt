TITLE
Homemade Italian Sauce)
• 4 (10 inch) flour tortillas
• 1/2 cup sour cream
• 3 tablespoons fresh diced onion
• 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
• 1 1/2 tablespoons minced onion
• 2 diced carrots, diced
• 1 teaspoon minced garlic
• 1 pinch cayenne pepper
• 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
• salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Stir in the onion, and saute until the onions are soft and translucent. Stir in rice, celery, and simmer 10 minutes. Stir constantly stirring occasionally, until mixture comes to a simmer. Stir in sesame seeds, spices, and peanut oil. Cook 10 minutes, stirring until smooth. Cook the mixture gently for 8 to 15 minutes, or until zucchini is tender. Remove from heat, and mix in the zucchini and vegetables. Add the reserved pineapple juice with the Dijon mustard. Continue to stir until the flour is blended, scraping the bottom and end of the bottom of the pan to avoid scorching. Serve immediately.
