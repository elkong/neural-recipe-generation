TITLE
Easy Chickpea Salad III

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cup water
• 1/2 cup vegetable oil
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1 tablespoon cornstarch
• 1 (12 ounce) package semisweet chocolate chips (optional)
• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 cup rolled oats

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
‣ Whisk together the eggs, rum and seeds, oil, and butter in a bowl. Blend the ingredients and the sugar in a bowl until the eggs are combined. Spoon the batter into the prepared pan. Place the chocolate chips in the casserole dish and cover the waxed paper towels with aluminum foil.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until cake tender and golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Allow each roll with 1/3 cup of the butter, and dip them in the the egg mixture.
