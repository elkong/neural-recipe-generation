TITLE
Refreshing Sweet and Spicy Jicama Salad (Vegan)
• 1/4 cup finely chopped pecans

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 minutes or until al dente; drain and rinse with cold water.
‣ Place all the cooked pasta on a rimmed baking sheet and in a small bowl toss to coat with oil. Add the pasta and toss to coat.
‣ Place half of the peas over the pesto, being careful not to over-made the bowl. Season with the salt and pepper. Set aside, and cool down the simmered bitter well.
‣ To make the sauce: In a punch bowl, combine the salt, pepper, and raisins. Mix well and spoon into shallow dishes. Pour remaining oil over the enchiladas.
‣ Whisk together the egg yolks, milk, yogurt, and cayenne pepper in a small bowl with an electric mixer until smooth, scatter and mix at high speed until smooth. Toss with the dressing until evenly coated. Pour pepper mixture over the top. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and serve.
