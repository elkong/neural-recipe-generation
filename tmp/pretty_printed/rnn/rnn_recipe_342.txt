TITLE
Garlic Basil Mashed Potatoes with Blackberry Potato Sauce I

INGREDIENTS
• 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
• 1 cup water
• 1 tablespoon potatoes (optional)
• 1 (12 fluid oun mini melting milk), finely minced
• 1/4 cup butter, softened
• 1 (8 ounce) container prepared milk chocolate topping

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x8 inch baking dish.
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the eggs and oil. Pour in the cream of mushroom soup and the heavy cream. Season with salt, pepper, and salt, then fold in the peanut butter pieces and pecans if using.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 more minutes.
