TITLE
Crescent Bacon Cheese Tartlets Recape Bar

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cup light corn syrup
• 1/4 cup sour cream
• 1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted chocolate chips, 1/2 cup shredded conserve stick, or a few pieces of chopped nuts
• 1 cup milk chocolate chips

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch pan.
‣ Mix butter and cocoa until evenly coated. Pour mixture over crust in layer or pan and bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 55 minutes. Let cool completely.
‣ Melt the chocolate chips in a small bowl before serving.
‣ Spoon 1 cup of the cookie mixture in the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan and pour in the chocolate bar over the top of the chocolate mixture. Place in the refrigerator and chill until set. Store in an airtight container.
