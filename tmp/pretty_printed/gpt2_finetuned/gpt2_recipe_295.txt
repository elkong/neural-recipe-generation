TITLE
Cordon Bleu Chicken Rolls

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
• 2 teaspoons seasoned salt
• 1 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
• 1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1/4 teaspoon paprika
• 1 (6 ounce) package herb-seasoned dry bread stuffing mix
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil for frying
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
• 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large bowl, stir together the bread crumbs, seasoned salt, onion powder, garlic powder, thyme, rosemary, pepper, oregano, cumin, paprika, and dried stuffing mix. In a separate bowl, mix together the vegetable oil, oil, and bread crumb mixture. Spread into the prepared baking pan.
‣ Heat 1/4 cup oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Fry the stuffing mix until browned, about 5 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
‣ Mix the vegetable mixture with the cilantro, mint, and cilantro in a bowl. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of the mint oil, and garnish with cilantro, fresh cilantro, and mint.
