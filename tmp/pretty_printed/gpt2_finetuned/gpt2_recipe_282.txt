TITLE
Absolutely Fabulous Portobello Mushroom Tortellini

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (16 ounce) containers Italian-style seasoned bread crumbs
• 2 cups shredded portobello mushrooms
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 2 tablespoons capers, drained
• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a medium bowl, combine the bread crumbs, mushrooms, cream cheese, capers, Parmesan cheese and mozzarella cheese. Mix together well and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
