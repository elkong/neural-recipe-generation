TITLE
Fajita Quesadillas

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups salsa
• 1 1/2 cups water
• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/2 cup sour cream
• 1/4 cup chopped onion
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 cup chopped green onions
• 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
• 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
• 1/2 cup chopped green onions

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring the salsa, water, cheese, sour cream, onion, garlic, green onions, tomato sauce, tomato sauce, cilantro, and green onions to a boil in a saucepan over medium heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the cheese has melted, about 10 minutes.
