TITLE
Peanut Sauce I

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups water
• 1 1/4 cups sugar
• 1/2 cup peanut butter
• 1/2 cup butter, softened
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine water, sugar and peanut butter. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Stir in vanilla and peanut butter mixture. Reduce heat, and simmer for 5 minutes, or until sauce is thickened.
