TITLE
Easy Marinated Pork Tenderloin

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1/4 cup lemon juice
• 1/4 cup chopped green onion
• 1/4 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
• 1 tablespoon soy sauce
• 2 tablespoons honey
• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 4 (4 ounce) pork tenderloins

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute green onion and mushrooms until tender. Stir in soy sauce, honey, Worcestershire sauce, and pepper. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until tender, about 10 minutes.
‣ Place pork tenderloins in the skillet and simmer 15 minutes. Stir in honey, then continue cooking 10 minutes more.
