TITLE
Claire's Yummy Crepes

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 cup butter
• 2 cups white sugar
• 2 eggs
• 1 cup buttermilk
• 2 tablespoons melted butter
• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/4 cup chopped pecans

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
‣ Sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, ginger, cloves, and nutmeg. Set aside.
‣ Cream together the 2 cups butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the buttermilk, melted butter, vinegar, vanilla, and pecans. Pour batter into prepared pan.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
