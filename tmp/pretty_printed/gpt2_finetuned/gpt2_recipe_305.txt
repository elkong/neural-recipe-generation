TITLE
Spicy Turkey Burgers

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1/4 cup chopped onion
• 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
• 1/2 cup chopped garlic
• 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
• 1 tablespoon ground cumin
• 1 teaspoon chili powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 pounds ground turkey
• 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Stir in the onion, green bell pepper, garlic, ginger, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook and stir until the onion is tender, about 5 minutes. Mix in ground turkey, and cook until well mixed. Stir in the Cheddar cheese, and continue cooking until cheese is melted, about 1 minute.
