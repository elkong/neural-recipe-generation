TITLE
Hobo's Delight

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (12 ounce) packages refrigerated crescent roll dough
• 1 pound lean ground beef
• 1 (8 ounce) package sliced onions
• 1 tablespoon soy sauce
• 2 tablespoons honey
• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1 tablespoon white sugar
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
‣ Mix together the crescent roll dough, ground beef, onion, soy sauce, honey, Worcestershire sauce, black pepper, garlic powder, sugar, cilantro, and 1/4 cup of the chopped cilantro. Roll the dough out to 1/4 inch thickness and place in an ungreased 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bake for 40 minutes in the preheated oven, until lightly browned.
