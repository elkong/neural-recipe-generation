TITLE
Ranch Burgers

INGREDIENTS
• 2 pounds ground beef
• 1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated crescent roll dough
• 1/2 cup ketchup
• 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 cup chopped onion
• 1/2 cup brown sugar
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat grill for high heat.
‣ In a large bowl, combine ground beef, crescent roll dough, ketchup, vinegar, salt, pepper and onion. Shape mixture into 4 patties.
‣ Lightly oil grill grate. Place patties on grill and cook 8 minutes, or until well done.
