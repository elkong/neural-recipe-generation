TITLE
No Fuss Chicken

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 cup chopped onion
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped carrot
• 2 cups diced cooked chicken breast meat
• 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
• 1/2 cup chicken broth
• 1/4 teaspoon paprika
• 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon paprika
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 cups chopped cooked chicken

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, and cook the onion, celery, and carrot until the onion is tender, about 10 minutes. Mix in the chicken and chicken broth, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, and continue cooking 10 minutes, until chicken is cooked through and no longer pink inside. Stir in paprika, cayenne pepper, and paprika. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 20 minutes, until thickened.
‣ Stir in chicken, and continue cooking 10 minutes, until chicken is heated through.
