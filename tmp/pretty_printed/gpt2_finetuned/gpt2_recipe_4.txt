TITLE
Spicy Potato Wedges

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups peeled, shredded potatoes
• 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen hash brown potatoes
• 1/2 cup olive oil
• 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Place potatoes in a medium baking dish. In a medium bowl, mix hash browns, olive oil, and cheese.
‣ Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, until cheese is melted.
