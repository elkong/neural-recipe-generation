TITLE
Chocolate Chip Dream Bars

INGREDIENTS
• 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
• 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
• 1 cup milk
• 2 cups white sugar
• 3 eggs
• 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
• 1 cup chopped walnuts
• 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar for decoration

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease two 9 inch round cake pans.
‣ Sift together the flour, cocoa, and milk. Set aside. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the chocolate chips and walnuts. Stir in the confectioners' sugar, one cup at a time, beating well after each addition. Pour into the prepared pans.
‣ Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until the edges start to turn golden. Allow to cool on wire racks.
