TITLE
Catherine's Spicy Chicken Soup

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 large onion, chopped
• 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 tablespoon ground cumin
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 3 cups water
• 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
• 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
• 1 (15 ounce) can chili beans, drained
• 2 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
• 2 cups chopped fresh cilantro
• 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
• 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
• 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
• 1/8 teaspoon ground white pepper
• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1 cup uncooked white rice
• 1/2 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir onion in hot oil until translucent, 5 to 7 minutes. Add chicken and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes more.
‣ Stir in pepper, 1/4 cup cumin, celery, water, kidney beans, garbanzo beans, chili beans, mushrooms, and cilantro. Stir in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, 1/2 cup cilantro, chili powder, cayenne pepper, cinnamon, cloves, cayenne pepper, white pepper, Worcestershire sauce, and rice. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer 20 minutes.
‣ Stir Cheddar cheese into soup just before serving.
