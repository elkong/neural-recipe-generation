TITLE
Eggnog Cake

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1/4 cup butter
• 1 egg, beaten
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 2 tablespoons butter
• 1 cup buttermilk
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9 inch round cake pan.
‣ Sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon and cloves. Set aside.
‣ Cream 1/4 cup butter and 1 egg together. Add 1/2 cup sugar, 1/4 cup butter and buttermilk and beat until fluffy. Stir in vanilla. Beat in flour mixture alternately with the buttermilk. Pour batter into prepared pan.
‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Remove cake from oven and cool on wire rack.
