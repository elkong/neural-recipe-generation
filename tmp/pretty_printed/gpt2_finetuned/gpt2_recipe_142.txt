TITLE
Aussie Omelet

INGREDIENTS
• 2 eggs
• 1 1/2 cups milk
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
• 1 1/2 cups chopped pecans
• 1/4 cup chopped almonds

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a saucepan, combine the eggs, milk, flour, oil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Cook for 1 minute. Remove from heat, and stir in vanilla extract. Remove from heat, and stir in the pecans and almonds. Serve hot.
