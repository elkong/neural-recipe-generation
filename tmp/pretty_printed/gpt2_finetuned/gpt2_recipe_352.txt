TITLE
Turkey 'n Stuffing Bake

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1/2 cup diced onion
• 1/4 cup chopped celery
• 3 cups turkey or turkey stuffing mix
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
• 2 tablespoons lemon juice
• 1 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine cooked macaroni, spinach, butter, onion, celery, and stuffing mix. Cook and stir until warm. Stir in frozen vegetables, lemon juice, oregano, pepper, and macaroni. Simmer until heated through.
