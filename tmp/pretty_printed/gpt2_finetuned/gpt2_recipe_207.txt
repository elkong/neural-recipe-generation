TITLE
Chewy Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups butter, softened
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
• 1 cup peanut butter
• 1/2 cup water
• 3 eggs
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.
‣ Cream together the butter or margarine, white sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the peanut butter, water, eggs, flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix in vanilla. Drop by heaping teaspoonfuls onto the prepared cookie sheets.
‣ Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, until edges are lightly browned. Cool on wire racks.
