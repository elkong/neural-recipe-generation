TITLE
Easy Pavlova

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup half-and-half
• 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
• 1 teaspoon dried basil
• 1 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
• 1 teaspoon dried thyme
• 1 teaspoon dried parsley
• 1 teaspoon dried marjoram
• 1 teaspoon dried sage
• 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring chicken broth, half-and-half, Worcestershire sauce, Italian seasoning, basil, oregano, rosemary, thyme, parsley, marjoram, sage, oregano, and pepper to a boil in a large saucepan over high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the liquid has reduced by half, about 20 minutes.
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease an 8x4-inch baking dish.
‣ Whisk flour with 1/4 cup reserved chicken broth until smooth; stir in chicken broth mixture. Pour into the prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until the top is golden brown and the bottom is golden, 45 to 50 minutes.
