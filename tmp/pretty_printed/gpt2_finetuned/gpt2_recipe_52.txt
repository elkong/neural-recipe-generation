TITLE
Cape Breton Blueberry Grunt

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups fresh blueberries
• 2 cups fresh blueberries
• 1 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1/4 cup milk
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/4 cup white sugar

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 10 inch Bundt pan.
‣ In a large bowl, combine blueberries, flour, 1 cup sugar, baking powder, salt, cinnamon and butter. Beat well and stir in milk, vanilla and 1/4 cup sugar. Pour into prepared pan.
‣ Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and light brown.
