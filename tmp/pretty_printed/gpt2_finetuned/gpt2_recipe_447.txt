TITLE
French Apple Pie with Cream Cheese Topping

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1 cup packed brown sugar
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
• 1/4 cup chopped pecans

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
‣ In a saucepan over medium heat, combine white sugar, brown sugar, butter, vegetable oil, cinnamon, vanilla and flour. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer until thickened.
‣ Stir in pecans and continue simmering for 5 minutes, or until thickened.
