TITLE
Hobart's Chicken and Red Bean Soup

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 large onion, chopped
• 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
• 1 cup diced cooked ham
• 2 cups chicken stock
• 1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 bay leaves
• 2 cups chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat; cook and stir onion until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add chicken; cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 10 minutes.
‣ Pour ham into pot; stir chicken stock, chicken broth, salt, and pepper into ham mixture; bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer until chicken is no longer pink in the center, about 20 minutes.
‣ Stir cilantro into pot; simmer until cilantro is tender, about 10 minutes.
