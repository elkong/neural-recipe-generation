TITLE
Taiwanese Fried Tofu

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon sesame oil
• 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
• 2 cups tofu, cubed
• 1 pound ground beef
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh garlic
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
• 1 teaspoon minced cilantro
• 2 tablespoons butter
• 2 teaspoons minced fresh cilantro
• 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro
• 1/4 cup sesame oil
• 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Stir in garlic and cook for 1 minute. Stir in sesame oil, sugar, and tofu. Cook, stirring, until mixture has a rich, golden brown consistency.
‣ Stir ground beef, ginger, and garlic into tofu mixture. Cook until meat is thoroughly browned, about 10 minutes. Stir in cilantro, butter, cilantro, cilantro seeds, and black pepper. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat to medium-low, and cook until liquid has evaporated, about 5 minutes.
