TITLE
Barbequed Marinated Flank Steak

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
• 1 (2 to 3 pound) flank steak, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat grill for high heat.
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in garlic and red pepper flakes, and cook until fragrant, about 5 minutes.
‣ Stir in Worcestershire sauce, pepper, salt, and cayenne pepper. Cook and stir 5 minutes, until Worcestershire sauce is dissolved.
‣ Brush flank steak with Worcestershire sauce. Discard any remaining marinade from the skillet. Place steak on the prepared grill, and cook 5 minutes per side, or until well done.
