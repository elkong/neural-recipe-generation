TITLE
Spicy Sweet Potato Salad

INGREDIENTS
• 2 sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch slices
• 1/4 cup mayonnaise
• 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
• 1 teaspoon honey
• 1 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon paprika
• 1/4 teaspoon celery salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
‣ Place the sweet potatoes into a large bowl. Mix together the mayonnaise, vinegar, honey, ginger, salt, paprika, celery salt, and black pepper in a small bowl. Stir in the sweet potato slices, and spread to coat.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until the sweet potatoes are tender and the dressing is hot, 40 to 45 minutes.
