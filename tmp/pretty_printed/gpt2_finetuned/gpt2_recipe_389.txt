TITLE
Stir-Fry Broccoli With Orange Sauce

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1/4 cup chopped green onions
• 1/4 cup orange juice
• 1/4 cup honey
• 2 tablespoons soy sauce
• 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
• 1 teaspoon sesame oil
• 1 teaspoon sesame oil
• 1 teaspoon white sugar
• 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in garlic, green onions, orange juice, honey, soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, sesame oil, sugar, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper; cook and stir until vegetables are tender, about 15 minutes.
