TITLE
Exotic Mushroom and Walnut Pate

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups walnuts
• 1 cup raisins
• 1/2 cup orange juice
• 1/2 cup olive oil
• 2 cups packed brown sugar
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/2 cup butter, cut into pieces

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Place walnuts and raisins in a microwave-safe bowl, and cook in the microwave for 5 minutes, or until lightly toasted.
‣ Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat, and cook and stir brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves until light and fragrant, about 5 minutes. Stir in butter, and cook and stir until melted and smooth, about 1 minute. Pour sauce over walnuts and raisins, and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar mixture.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until puffed and golden brown, stirring every 10 to 15 minutes, about 30 minutes.
