TITLE
Blueberry Stuffed French Toast

INGREDIENTS
• 2 eggs
• 1 1/2 cups milk
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup blueberries
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
• 1/4 cup confectioners' sugar for dusting

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a saucepan, combine the eggs, milk, flour, blueberries, butter and sugar. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Cook for 1 minute. Remove from heat, and stir in walnuts. Spoon into a greased 8x8 inch loaf pan. Sprinkle with confectioner's sugar.
‣ Bake for 1 hour in a preheated 350 degree F (175 degrees C) oven. Let cool, then cut into wedges.
