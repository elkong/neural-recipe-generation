TITLE
Roasted Pumpkin Seeds

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups pumpkin seeds
• 1 cup packed brown sugar
• 1/2 cup butter, softened
• 1 cup packed brown sugar
• 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a baking sheet.
‣ Place pumpkin seeds in a large bowl. Mix together the brown sugar, butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon in a small bowl. Spread the mixture evenly into the prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake pumpkin seeds for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until tender.
