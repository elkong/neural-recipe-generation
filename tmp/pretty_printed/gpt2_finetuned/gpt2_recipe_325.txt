TITLE
Garlic Cheese Biscuits

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1 cup packed brown sugar
• 1 egg
• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a baking sheet.
‣ In a large bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, salt, pepper and cloves. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add 1/2 cup butter and stir until mixture is crumbly.
‣ In a large bowl, beat together egg and cheese. Stir egg mixture into flour mixture. Stir in Parmesan cheese. Drop dough by heaping spoonfuls onto prepared baking sheet.
‣ Bake for 12 to 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until lightly browned.
