TITLE
Smoked Salmon Scramble

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups butter, softened
• 2 cups chopped fresh mushrooms
• 1 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup uncooked long grain white rice
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 1 large onion, chopped
• 4 cloves garlic, chopped
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen smoked salmon, thawed and drained
• 1/4 cup dry sherry
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
• 1/4 cup chopped red pepper flakes
• 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
• 1 cup chopped fresh sage

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together butter, mushrooms, flour, rice, salt, pepper, and olive oil. Stir in onion and garlic; toss until flour is evenly mixed.
‣ Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Add salmon and sherry; cook and stir until liquid is reduced by half, about 3 minutes. Stir in parsley, mint, red pepper flakes, and mushrooms; cook and stir until liquid is reduced by half, about 2 minutes.
‣ Pour sauce into a 3 quart casserole dish. Sprinkle with fresh mushrooms and sage. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
