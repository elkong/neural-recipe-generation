TITLE
Chicken Tetrazzini IV

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked pasta
• 2 cups chopped, cooked chicken
• 1 tablespoon butter
• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 cup milk
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 1/4 cup chopped onion
• 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
• 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
• 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
• 3/4 cup butter, melted

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain, and set aside.
‣ In a large skillet over medium heat, melt 1/2 cup butter. Stir in flour and salt. Cook and stir 5 minutes, until bubbly. Add milk, olive oil, onion, green bell pepper, red bell pepper, and parsley. Cook, stirring frequently, until vegetables are tender.
‣ Melt remaining 1/4 cup butter in saucepan over medium heat. Stir in Monterey Jack cheese, and cook until cheese melts. Pour in melted butter. Add chicken, and stir to coat. Cook until heated through.
