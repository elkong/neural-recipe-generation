TITLE
Cilantro Serrano Cream Sauce

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
• 1 (16 ounce) package angel hair pasta
• 1 (16 ounce) jar roasted red peppers, drained and chopped
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Stir in the garlic and ginger; cook and stir until the garlic begins to turn golden brown, about 5 minutes. Mix in the angel hair pasta, and stir to coat. Place the pasta in the saucepan, and cook for 5 minutes, until tender.
‣ Stir the cilantro into the saucepan, and simmer for 5 more minutes, until the sauce thickens and coats the back of a metal spoon.
