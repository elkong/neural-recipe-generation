TITLE
Microwave Oven Peanut Brittle

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked regular long grain white rice
• 1/2 cup water
• 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
• 1/4 cup butter, softened
• 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
• 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring the rice and water to a boil in a saucepan over high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender and water has been absorbed, about 20 minutes.
‣ Combine the peanut butter, butter, 1/4 cup brown sugar, 1/4 cup brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, allspice, and 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir until the peanut butter has melted and the mixture has thickened, about 2 minutes. Stir in the rice and continue cooking and stirring until the peanut butter mixture has thickened, about 2 more minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the cinnamon sugar, cloves, and allspice until the cinnamon sugar has dissolved.
