TITLE
Easy Bok Choy Pork Chops

INGREDIENTS
• 1 1/2 cups water
• 1/2 cup sesame oil
• 1/2 cup ketchup
• 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
• 2 tablespoons honey
• 1 tablespoon soy sauce
• 1/4 cup chopped green onions
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a medium bowl, mix the water, sesame oil, ketchup, rice vinegar, honey, and soy sauce.
‣ Place the pork chops into the marinade, and marinate for 1 hour, or overnight.
‣ Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
‣ Lightly oil the grill grate. Discard marinade. Grill pork chops for 6 to 8 minutes per side, turning once.
