TITLE
Mexican Bean Burgers

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
• 1/4 cup salsa
• 1/2 cup sour cream
• 1 tablespoon chili powder
• 2 tablespoons ground cumin
• 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
• 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
• 1 pound ground beef

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large skillet, heat the salsa and sour cream over medium heat. Mix in the chili powder, cumin, and cilantro. Mix in the taco seasoning. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes.
‣ Preheat grill for high heat.
‣ Lightly oil grill grate. Grill burgers for 15 minutes on each side, or until well done.
