TITLE
Curried Chicken and Broccoli Casserole

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
• 2 cups low-fat sour cream
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
• 1/4 cup butter
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ Meanwhile, place broccoli in a steamer over 1 inch of boiling water, and cover. Cook until tender but still firm, about 20 minutes. Drain.
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together broccoli, Cheddar cheese, sour cream, celery, cream of chicken soup, butter, onion and green bell pepper. Pour into prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
