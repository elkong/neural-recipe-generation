TITLE
Brown Rice and Black Bean Casserole

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked brown rice
• 2 (14.5 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
• 1/2 cup butter, melted
• 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
• 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
• 1 (15 ounce) can white beans, drained and rinsed
• 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers, drained and chopped
• 1/4 cup butter, melted

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a medium bowl, mix brown rice, black beans, and butter. Transfer to a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until lightly browned.
‣ In a saucepan over medium heat, cook and stir black beans, kidney beans, and white beans until beans are slightly tender. Stir in green chile peppers and melted butter. Serve warm.
