TITLE
Vegan Split Pea Soup II

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup half-and-half
• 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
• 1/2 onion, chopped
• 2 cups diced carrot
• 3 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 1/2 cups chopped celery
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
• 2 (15 ounce) cans garbanzo beans, drained
• 1/4 cup water
• 1/2 cup dry lentils
• 1/2 cup sliced fresh mushrooms

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large stock pot combine chicken broth, half-and-half, oil, onion, carrot, garlic, celery, pepper, salt, cumin, thyme, cayenne pepper, basil, garbanzo beans, water, lentils and mushrooms. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to low. Simmer for 20 minutes, or until lentils are tender.
