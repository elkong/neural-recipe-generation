TITLE
Squashed Washington

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1/4 cup chopped onion
• 1/4 cup chopped celery
• 3 cups milk
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ In a large saucepan over medium heat, combine spinach, butter, onion, celery, milk, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil and cook until mixture is heated through, stirring frequently. Remove from heat and stir in pasta and spinach mixture.
‣ Spread onto prepared baking dish and bake 30 minutes, or until bubbly and lightly browned.
