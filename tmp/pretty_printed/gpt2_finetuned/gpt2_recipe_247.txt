TITLE
Tender Breaded Turkey Cutlets

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/2 teaspoon paprika
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/2 cup butter, softened
• 1 egg
• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
• 1 teaspoon white sugar
• 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
• 1 teaspoon baking powder
• 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
• 2 tablespoons butter, softened
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 2 quart baking dish.
‣ In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, black pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, paprika, nutmeg, cayenne pepper, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and 1/2 cup butter. Cut in the egg until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
‣ Shape dough into 1 inch balls, and place them 2 inches apart on prepared baking sheet. Brush each ball with 1/4 cup butter, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup vinegar.
‣ Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, until golden brown. Remove from heat, and allow to cool.
‣ In a small bowl, beat together 1/4 cup butter and 1 teaspoon vanilla until smooth. Drizzle over the baked turkey, and allow to cool completely.
