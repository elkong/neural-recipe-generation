TITLE
Peanut Crunch Cake

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cup butter, softened
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
• 1 cup vegetable oil
• 1/2 cup water
• 3 eggs
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon baking powder
• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 cup peanut butter
• 1 cup chopped pecans

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan.
‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar and brown sugar. Beat in the vegetable oil, water, eggs and flour. Stir in the baking powder, baking soda and sugar. Stir in vanilla. Pour batter into prepared pan.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
