TITLE
S.O.B. (South of the Border) Casserole

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/2 cup sour cream
• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Monterey Jack cheese
• 1/4 cup sour cream
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/2 cup sour cream
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh chives
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill weed
• 1/4 cup water
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/2 cup sour cream
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together cooked macaroni, spinach, 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese, 1 cup Cheddar cheese, sour cream, 1/4 cup Monterey Jack cheese, 1/4 cup sour cream, 1/4 cup parsley, onion, 1/2 cup sour cream, and 1/4 cup parsley.
‣ In a large bowl, mix together cream cheese, 1 cup Cheddar cheese, 1/2 cup sour cream, and 1/4 cup parsley.
‣ Spread mixture into prepared baking dish, and sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup Cheddar cheese.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until heated through and bubbly.
