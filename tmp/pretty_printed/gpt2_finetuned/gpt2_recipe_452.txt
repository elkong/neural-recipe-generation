TITLE
Dark Chocolate Cake I

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups cake flour
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
• 1 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 3 eggs
• 1 cup light brown sugar
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
• 1/4 cup sour cream
• 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1/2 cup butter
• 2 tablespoons light corn syrup
• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan.
‣ In a large bowl, combine the cake flour, 1 cup white sugar, cocoa, baking soda, salt and vanilla. In a separate bowl, cream together the eggs and brown sugar. Add the flour mixture alternately with the light brown sugar, mixing just until incorporated. Pour into the prepared pan.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 50 to 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter, cream cheese, sour cream, 1/4 cup cocoa and 1/4 cup sugar until smooth. Beat in the remaining 1/2 cup butter until well blended. Pour over the cake.
‣ To make the frosting, in a saucepan over medium heat, combine the sour cream, 1/4 cup cocoa and 1/4 cup sugar. Bring to a simmer over medium heat. Reduce heat to low and cook for 5 minutes. Stir in the corn syrup and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla. Continue to simmer until thickened.
