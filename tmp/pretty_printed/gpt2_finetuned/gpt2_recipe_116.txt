TITLE
Preety's Chickpea Salad

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups baby carrots
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon paprika
• 1/2 teaspoon paprika
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1 tablespoon paprika
• 1 tablespoon ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place carrots, celery, onion, celery, rice vinegar, white sugar, cumin, salt, paprika, paprika, black pepper, parsley, vegetable oil, and distilled white vinegar into a food processor; blend until smooth. Add chopped fresh parsley; blend again until parsley is moistened.
