TITLE
Bacon Asparagus Pizza

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 large onion, chopped
• 1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1/2-inch slices
• 1 (10 ounce) bag fresh asparagus tips
• 1 cup chopped fresh basil
• salt and pepper to taste
• 4 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
• 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a pizza pan or pizza stone.
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion; cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. Add asparagus; cook until asparagus is tender, about 3 minutes. Add basil; cook and stir until basil has softened, about 1 minute.
‣ Place a layer of mozzarella cheese on each pizza crust. Top each pizza with a layer of onion and asparagus. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until cheese has melted and crust is golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
