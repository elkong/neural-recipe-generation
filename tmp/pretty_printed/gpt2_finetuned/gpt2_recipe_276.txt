TITLE
Slow Cooker Creamy Chicken and Dumplings

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup half-and-half
• 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1 tablespoon dried oregano
• 1 tablespoon dried basil
• 2 tablespoons garlic powder
• 1 tablespoon onion powder
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon celery salt
• 1/4 teaspoon celery seed
• 1/4 teaspoon paprika
• 1/4 teaspoon ground sage
• 1/4 teaspoon celery seed
• 1/4 teaspoon dried parsley
• 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
• 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon celery salt
• 2 cups water

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Pour chicken broth into a slow cooker. Sprinkle Worcestershire sauce, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, celery salt, celery seed, paprika, sage, celery seed, parsley, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, nutmeg, oregano, thyme, pepper, and celery salt over the chicken.
‣ Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Stir water and 2 cups of the broth into the slow cooker.
