TITLE
Hazelnut Melting Moments

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
• 1/2 cup flaked coconut
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1/2 cup brown sugar
• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place chocolate chips, coconut, butter, brown sugar, nutmeg, and cinnamon into a large microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on high for 2 minutes, stirring after each minute.
