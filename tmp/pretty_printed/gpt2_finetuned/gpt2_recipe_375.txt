TITLE
Perfect Breakfast Sausage Soup

INGREDIENTS
• 1 pound ground pork sausage
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
• 1 (15 ounce) can great Northern beans, drained
• 1 cup chicken broth
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 tablespoon butter
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 2/3 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Cook and stir sausage in a large skillet over medium-high heat until evenly browned, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard any excess grease.
‣ In a large pot over medium-high heat, combine flour, mixed vegetables, great Northern beans, chicken broth, salt, pepper and butter. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for about 30 minutes.
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat, and add onion, celery and cheese. Cook and stir until cheese melts, about 5 minutes.
