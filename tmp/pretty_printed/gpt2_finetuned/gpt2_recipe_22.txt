TITLE
Chef John's Baked Acorn Squash

INGREDIENTS
• 2 acorn squash, halved and seeded
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 2 cups milk
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 2 tablespoons white sugar
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
‣ Arrange squash halved in a single layer in a single layer on a cookie sheet. Sprinkle with butter, and bake for about 10 minutes.
‣ Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour, and cook for 1 minute. Gradually whisk milk, salt, sugar, and olive oil into flour mixture, stirring constantly. Stir in parsley.
‣ Drop the squash halved into the butter mixture, and bake about 15 minutes. Turn squash, and bake about 5 minutes more.
