TITLE
Broccoli Chicken Casserole I

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
• 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/2 cup butter, melted
• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/2 cup milk
• 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ Combine broccoli, broccoli, and Cheddar cheese in a large bowl. Mix together and set aside.
‣ Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir flour and pepper in butter until blended. Add milk, stirring constantly, and cook and stir until smooth. Pour into a 2 quart casserole dish.
‣ Sprinkle 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese and 1/2 cup butter over the casserole. Spread 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese and 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese over the cheese.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted, about 15 minutes.
