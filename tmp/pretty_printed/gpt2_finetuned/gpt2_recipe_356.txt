TITLE
Fruited Coleslaw

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups mayonnaise
• 2 cups sour cream
• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• 1 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
• 1 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
• 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
• 1/4 teaspoon dried parsley
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Mix mayonnaise, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, Colby-Monterey Jack cheese, celery, green bell pepper, onion, celery, green bell pepper, oregano, garlic powder, basil, thyme, parsley, and black pepper in a large bowl. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes to blend flavors before serving.
