TITLE
Sweet and Sour Meatballs I

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
• 2 teaspoons seasoned salt
• 1 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 pound ground beef
• 1 cup chopped onion
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
• 1/4 cup sour cream
• 1/2 cup vegetable oil
• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1/4 cup water
• 1 teaspoon paprika
• 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together bread crumbs, seasoned salt, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, ground beef, onion, celery, and green pepper. Spread the ground beef mixture into a shallow baking dish.
‣ Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the meat is no longer pink and the juices run clear. Stir in the sour cream, vegetable oil, Worcestershire sauce, water, paprika, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Allow to cool, then refrigerate for at least 3 hours before slicing.
