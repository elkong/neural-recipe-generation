TITLE
Collard-Kielbasa Soup

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 large onion, chopped
• 1 pound collard greens, rinsed and chopped
• 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 3 cups water
• 1/2 cup heavy cream
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped parsley
• 1/4 cup butter
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
• 1 teaspoon white sugar
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 tablespoon butter
• 1/2 cup milk
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onions, and cook until they begin to turn translucent, about 5 minutes. Add collard greens, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper; simmer for 1 minute.
‣ Whisk in 1 cup of celery, water, cream, and celery, parsley, butter, and onion; simmer for another minute.
‣ Stir in flour until well blended. Gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of water, then 1/2 cup of milk. Simmer over low heat until thickened. Remove from heat and stir in cilantro, and serve hot.
