TITLE
Wasabi Encrusted Tuna Steaks

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh turmeric
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 teaspoon ground coriander
• 1 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
• 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
• 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in garlic, ginger, turmeric, black pepper, cinnamon, coriander, cumin, turmeric, cayenne pepper, and cilantro. Cook, stirring occasionally, until fragrant, about 2 minutes. Sprinkle with parsley, and serve.
