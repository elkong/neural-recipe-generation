TITLE
Clone of a Pretzel Dip

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (12 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough
• 1 cup butter
• 1 cup chopped pecans
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
• 1 (8 ounce) jar lemon flavored Jell-O®
• 1 (12 ounce) package miniature marshmallows

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
‣ Cut each pretzel dough into four pieces. Wrap each piece of dough in a plastic bag, and place in the oven until warm, about 10 minutes.
‣ Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in pecans, brown sugar and vanilla. Cook, stirring, until light brown, about 2 minutes.
‣ Pour warm butter mixture over the pretzels.
‣ Bake for 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown. Allow to cool completely before cutting into bars.
