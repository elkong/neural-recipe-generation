TITLE
Grilled Zucchini Pizza

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups grated zucchini
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup water
• 3/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
• 1 egg, beaten
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
• salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat, and lightly oil the grate.
‣ Mix the zucchini, flour, vegetable oil, water, Parmesan cheese, egg, cumin, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; shape the mixture into patties about 1/2 inch thick.
‣ Grill the patties for 5 minutes per side, or until well done.
