TITLE
Warm Brie and Pear Tartlets

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups peeled, cored and thinly sliced Brie
• 1 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup water
• 3/4 cup brown sugar
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 cup brown sugar
• 2 eggs
• 1/2 cup vegetable oil
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Place the Cheddar cheese, brie, flour, and oil in a large bowl. Stir in the water, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and sugar. Cut in the eggs one at a time, and mix with an electric mixer on low speed until mixture is thick and well blended. Pour batter into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the tartlets comes out clean.
