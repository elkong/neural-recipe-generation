TITLE
Apricot-Glazed Pork Chops

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh turmeric
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and ginger; cook and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute.
‣ Stir in the turmeric, cinnamon, cumin, and pepper. Cook and stir for 1 minute, until fragrant. Mix in the cilantro, and reduce heat to low. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
