TITLE
Raspberry Cheesecake Stuffed French Toast

INGREDIENTS
• 2 eggs
• 1 1/2 cups white sugar
• 1/2 cup butter, softened
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1 cup sour cream
• 1 cup chopped pecans
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/2 cup butter, softened
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar for dusting

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ In a medium bowl, beat together eggs, sugar, and butter. Mix in cream cheese, cream cheese, sour cream, pecans, and vanilla. Stir in flour until just blended. Pour into prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 60 minutes, or until center of cake springs back when touched. Allow to cool.
‣ In a small bowl, combine 1/2 cup softened butter and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
‣ To Make Filling: In a large bowl, mix together flour and 1/2 cup softened butter. Beat until light and fluffy. Fold in confectioners' sugar until just incorporated. Spread filling on cooled cake.
