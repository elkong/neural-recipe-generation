TITLE
Chocolate Chip Bundt Cake

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cup butter, softened
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
• 1 cup vegetable oil
• 1/2 cup water
• 3 eggs
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon baking powder
• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
• 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan.
‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, white sugar, oil, water, and eggs. Mix in the flour, baking powder and baking soda. Stir in chocolate chips. Pour batter into prepared pan.
‣ Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool in pan for 10 minutes.
