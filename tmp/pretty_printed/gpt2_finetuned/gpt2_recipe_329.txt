TITLE
Vegan Brownies

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cup butter, softened
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
• 1 egg
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1 cup brown sugar
• 1/2 cup vegetable oil
• 1 cup white chocolate chips

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan.
‣ In a large bowl, cream together butter, brown sugar, white sugar and egg until smooth. Beat in vanilla. Combine flour, baking soda, salt and brown sugar; blend into creamed mixture. Stir in oil, 1 cup at a time, beating well after each addition. Fold in chocolate chips. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto prepared pan.
‣ Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are golden. Cool on wire racks.
