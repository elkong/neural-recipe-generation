TITLE
Josephine's No-Bake Granola Bars

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups quick cooking oats
• 1 cup chopped pecans
• 1/2 cup brown sugar
• 1/2 cup butter, melted
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
• 1 cup chopped walnuts
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
‣ In a large bowl, mix together the oats, pecans, brown sugar, and butter. Set aside.
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the vanilla extract and chocolate chips. Stir in the nuts and walnuts until evenly blended. Press the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until the top is golden brown and the edges are firm, 25 to 30 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes before cutting into squares.
