TITLE
Coconut Custard Pie I

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cup butter, softened
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 3 eggs
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon baking powder
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 cup coconut milk
• 1 cup chopped walnuts

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar. Beat in the cream cheese, eggs, and flour. Combine the baking powder and salt, gradually stir into the creamed mixture. Spread the batter into the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan.
‣ Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
