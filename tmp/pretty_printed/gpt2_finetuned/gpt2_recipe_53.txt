TITLE
Bacon Wrapped Pesto Chicken

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
• 2 teaspoons seasoned salt
• 1 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/8 teaspoon dried thyme
• 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1/8 teaspoon paprika
• 1 (6 ounce) package feta cheese, crumbled

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a medium baking dish.
‣ In a medium bowl, mix bread crumbs, seasoned salt, garlic powder, onion powder, basil, oregano, black pepper, thyme, and cayenne pepper.
‣ Dip chicken pieces in bread crumb mixture, and place on prepared baking dish. Top with feta cheese.
‣ Bake 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until cheese is melted and chicken is no longer pink in the center.
