TITLE
Parmesan Zucchini Patties

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups Italian-seasoned bread crumbs
• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• 1 egg, beaten
• 2 cups bread crumbs
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1 (8 ounce) package sliced zucchini
• 1 cup bread crumbs
• 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, bread crumbs, salt and pepper. Sprinkle over bread crumb mixture.
‣ Arrange zucchini slices on a baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven 10 minutes, or until lightly browned.
‣ Top each zucchini slice with a slice of bread crumb mixture. Arrange sliced zucchini slices on top of the bread crumb mixture. Sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese.
‣ Bake 15 minutes, until bubbly.
