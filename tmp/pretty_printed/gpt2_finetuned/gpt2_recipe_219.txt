TITLE
Mango, Peach and Pineapple Salsa

INGREDIENTS
• 2 mangoes, peeled
• 2 pineapple chunks, sliced
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
• 1/2 lime, juiced
• 1 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Combine mango, pineapple, cilantro, lime juice, cumin, and pepper in a bowl; mix well.
