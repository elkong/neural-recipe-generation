TITLE
Avocado Ranch Salad Dressing

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups mayonnaise
• 2 cups sour cream
• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• 1 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese blend

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, Parmesan cheese and Jack cheese. Pour dressing over salad and toss lightly.
