TITLE
The Cake That Doesn't Last Cake

INGREDIENTS
• 1 1/4 cups white sugar
• 1 cup butter
• 1/2 cup shortening
• 1 egg
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 teaspoon almond extract
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon baking powder
• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
• 1 cup milk
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/4 cup butter
• 1/2 cup shortening
• 2 eggs
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.
‣ In a large bowl, cream 1 1/4 cups sugar and 1/2 cup butter until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg, 1 teaspoon vanilla and almond extracts.
‣ Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and milk. Add flour mixture to butter mixture alternately with milk. Blend well.
‣ Combine 1 cup sugar and 1/4 cup butter with the milk mixture; beat until smooth. Stir in 1 cup flour mixture alternately with milk.
‣ Pour batter into prepared pans. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
‣ To Make Filling: In a medium bowl, cream 1 1/4 cups sugar and 1/4 cup butter until light and fluffy. Beat in 1 egg and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
‣ Beat 1 cup of filling until combined. Spread over cooled cake.
