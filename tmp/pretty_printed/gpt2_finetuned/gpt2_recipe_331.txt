TITLE
White Bean Dip with Pine Nuts

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (15 ounce) cans white beans, rinsed and drained
• 1/4 cup mayonnaise
• 1/4 cup pine nuts
• 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
• 1/4 cup pine nuts
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1 cup white sugar
• 2 eggs
• 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
• 1 teaspoon lemon zest
• 1 pinch cayenne pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking sheet.
‣ In a medium bowl, stir together the white beans, mayonnaise, pine nuts, ranch dressing mix, and pine nuts.
‣ In a separate bowl, mix the cream cheese, sugar, eggs, lemon juice, lemon zest, and cayenne pepper until smooth.
‣ Pour the dressing mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Sprinkle with the cayenne pepper.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until the top is golden brown, 25 to 30 minutes.
