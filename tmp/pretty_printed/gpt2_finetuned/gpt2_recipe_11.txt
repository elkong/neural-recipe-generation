TITLE
Albondigas Soup I

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup half-and-half
• 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1 tablespoon chili powder
• 1 teaspoon dried minced onion
• 1 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1 teaspoon dried parsley
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
• 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 1 1/2 pounds lean ground turkey
• 1 1/2 cups chopped celery
• 1 onion, chopped
• 1/2 cup chopped carrots
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 3 stalks celery, chopped
• 3 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 tablespoon butter
• 2 large tomatoes, diced
• 1/2 cup fresh cranberries
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh lemon juice
• 1 cup sour cream
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
• 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large stock pot over medium heat, combine chicken broth, half-and-half, Worcestershire sauce, chili powder, dried minced onion, cumin, parsley, pepper, oregano, salt, cayenne, ginger, cayenne, allspice and olive oil. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until all the vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes. Remove from heat.
‣ Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook turkey, celery, onion, carrots, celery, onion, carrots, celery, garlic, parsley, black pepper, butter, tomatoes, cranberries, cilantro, cilantro, lemon juice, sour cream and cilantro until thoroughly combined.
