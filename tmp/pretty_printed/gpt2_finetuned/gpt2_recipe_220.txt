TITLE
Char Siu (Chinese BBQ Pork)

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
• 1 cup packed light brown sugar
• 1 cup sesame seeds
• 1/2 cup ketchup
• 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
• 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
• 1 tablespoon ground ginger
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger root
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 pounds boneless pork shoulder

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute onions and green onions until tender. Stir in sesame seeds, ketchup, rice vinegar, oil, ground ginger, cinnamon, cloves, ground ginger, pepper and pork. Reduce heat to low and simmer until pork is tender.
‣ While the pork is simmering, heat remaining 2 tablespoons oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in ground pork and cook until browned.
