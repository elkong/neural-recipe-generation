TITLE
Filet Mignons With Pepper Cream Sauce

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (12 ounce) packages fresh filet mignon steaks
• 1 tablespoon butter
• 1 cup heavy cream
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
‣ In a medium skillet over medium heat, melt butter with the heavy cream. Stir in the cheese, and season with salt and pepper. Cook until melted and smooth. Pour into a shallow baking dish, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is lightly browned and the sauce is bubbling, about 20 minutes.
