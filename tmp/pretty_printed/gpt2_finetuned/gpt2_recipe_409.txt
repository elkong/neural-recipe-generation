TITLE
Jalapeno Garlic Tilapia Pasta

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (16 ounce) tilapia fillets
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 1 tablespoon minced fresh garlic
• 1 tablespoon lime juice
• salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place the tilapia fillets in a medium skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning once, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain the fillets on paper towels and set aside.
‣ In a large bowl, toss the olive oil, garlic, lime juice, salt and pepper. Whisk in the tilapia until coated.
