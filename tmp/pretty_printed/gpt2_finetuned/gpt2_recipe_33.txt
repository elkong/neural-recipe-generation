TITLE
October Apple Pie

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1 cup packed brown sugar
• 1/2 cup butter, softened
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/4 cup chopped pecans
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl, mix together white sugar, brown sugar, butter and cream cheese until smooth. Mix in flour, pecans, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, allspice, and cloves. Stir in apples. Pour mixture into pastry shell.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
