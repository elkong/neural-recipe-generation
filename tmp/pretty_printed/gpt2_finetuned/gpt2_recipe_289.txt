TITLE
Sweet Cornbread Cake

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1/4 cup brown sugar
• 1 egg
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 1/2 cups chopped sweet corn

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.
‣ Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, ground cloves, nutmeg, allspice, ginger, ground cloves, and nutmeg; set aside.
‣ Cream butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add egg and vanilla and beat until combined. Add flour mixture and beat until light and fluffy. Stir in corn.
‣ Pour batter into prepared pans. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 50 to 55 minutes. Allow to cool in pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
