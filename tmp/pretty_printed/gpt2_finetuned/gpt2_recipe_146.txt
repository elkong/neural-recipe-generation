TITLE
Spicy Sausage Quiche

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1 (1 pound) loaf processed cheese, cubed
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
• 1 tablespoon white sugar
• 1 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/4 cup sliced green onions

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
‣ In a medium bowl, mix flour, cream cheese, and processed cheese until well blended. Gradually mix in cilantro, cilantro, cilantro sugar, garlic powder, pepper, and onion. Fold in green onions. Spoon into pie shell.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes.
