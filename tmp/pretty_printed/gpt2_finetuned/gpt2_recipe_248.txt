TITLE
No Bake Sugar Free Strawberry Cheesecake

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 2 cups crushed graham cracker crumbs
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 cup strawberry preserves
• 2 eggs
• 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
• 1 cup sour cream
• 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
• 1 teaspoon lemon juice
• 2 tablespoons light corn syrup

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Mix cream cheese and 1/2 cup sugar in a bowl until well blended. Spread cream cheese mixture into bottom of 9 inch springform pan.
‣ Mix graham cracker crumbs and cream cheese mixture into cream cheese mixture until crumbly. Press crumb mixture into the bottom of the springform pan.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown, about 15 minutes. Cool completely.
‣ Beat together eggs, cream, confectioners' sugar, and lemon juice in a bowl. Pour egg mixture over cream cheese layer.
‣ Beat sour cream, confectioners' sugar, and lemon juice in a bowl until smooth and creamy. Pour over whipped cream layer.
‣ Beat 1 cup sour cream until smooth.
‣ Spoon 1/4 cup cream cheese mixture over top of whipped cream layer.
‣ Bake in the oven until topping is golden brown and cream cheese filling is set, about 30 minutes.
