TITLE
Mom's Squash Casserole

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/2 cup butter, melted
• 1/4 cup mayonnaise
• 1/2 cup milk
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• salt and ground black pepper to taste
• 2 tablespoons chopped onion
• 1/4 cup sliced green onions

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ In a large bowl, mix together the spinach, Cheddar cheese, Cheddar cheese, melted butter, mayonnaise, milk, Parmesan cheese, salt, black pepper, onion, and green onions. Mix well and pour mixture into prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until heated through.
