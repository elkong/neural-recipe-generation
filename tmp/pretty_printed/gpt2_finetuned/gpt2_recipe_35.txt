TITLE
Lemony Steamed Fish

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1/4 cup chopped green onions
• 1/4 cup sliced fresh mint leaves
• 1 (12 pound) cod fillet, peeled and deveined
• 1 cup pitted and chopped fresh cilantro
• 1/4 cup lemon juice
• 1 tablespoon white sugar

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic and green onions until tender. Stir in mint, cod, and cilantro. Season with lemon juice, sugar, and remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until cod is done.
