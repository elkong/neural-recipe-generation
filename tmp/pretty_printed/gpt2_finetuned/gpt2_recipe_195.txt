TITLE
Campbell's Kitchen Easy Chicken Pot Pie

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can milk
• 1 cup uncooked instant mashed potatoes

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Combine the chicken broth, chicken soup, celery soup, milk, and instant mashed potatoes in a slow cooker. Stir until smooth. Cover and cook on Low for 8 hours or on High for 8 hours.
