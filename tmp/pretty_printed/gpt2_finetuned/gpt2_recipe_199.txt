TITLE
Mango Corn Salsa

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
• 1 (15 ounce) can black-eyed peas, rinsed and drained
• 1 (15 ounce) can corn, drained
• 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
• 1 (15 ounce) can red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
• 1 (15 ounce) can white corn, drained
• 1 (4 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Monterey Jack cheese, divided

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Mix black beans, black-eyed peas, corn, kidney beans, red kidney beans, white corn, olives, and Monterey Jack cheese in a large bowl. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
