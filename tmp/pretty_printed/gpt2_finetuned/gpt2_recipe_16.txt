TITLE
Tomato and Mozzarella Bites

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/4 cup olive oil
• 1/4 cup water
• 3/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
• 1 cup chopped fresh basil
• 1 (8 ounce) package baby spinach leaves
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
• salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the Parmesan cheese, flour, olive oil, water, and 1/4 cup of the Parmesan cheese. Season with 1/2 cup of the basil, and 1/4 cup of the spinach. Stir until thoroughly combined. Spread evenly in the prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until bubbly and golden brown. Sprinkle with 1/4 cup of the basil and 1/4 cup of the spinach. Continue baking 15 minutes, until golden. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup of basil and 1/4 cup of spinach. Season with 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
