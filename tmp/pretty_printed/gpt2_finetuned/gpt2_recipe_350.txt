TITLE
Wanda's Chicken Noodle Bake

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
• 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent roll dough
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
• 1/2 cup milk
• 1/4 cup butter
• 1/4 cup butter
• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 cup milk
• 1/2 cup sour cream

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 2 quart casserole dish.
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add casserole and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ Place cooked macaroni in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Stir broccoli, soup, milk, butter, flour, salt, and pepper in a bowl; spread over macaroni and broccoli mixture.
‣ Bake in preheated oven until macaroni is golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
‣ While the pasta is cooking, heat butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute sifted confectioners' sugar, 1/4 cup at a time, until lightly golden brown.
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together sour cream and milk until smooth. Pour over macaroni and broccoli mixture.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until the sauce is bubbling, 35 to 40 minutes.
