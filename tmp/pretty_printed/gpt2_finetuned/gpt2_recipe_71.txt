TITLE
Boiled Mustard Potatoes

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (16 ounce) package frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed
• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
• 1/4 cup milk
• 1/4 teaspoon salt

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large pot over medium heat, combine potatoes, cheese, soup, milk and salt. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.
