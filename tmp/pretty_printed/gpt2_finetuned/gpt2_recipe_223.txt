TITLE
Cranapana Bread

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/2 cup chopped cranberries

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
‣ Sift the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, ginger, cloves, and nutmeg together in a large bowl. Add the cranberries and stir until evenly blended. Pour into prepared loaf pan.
‣ Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour.
