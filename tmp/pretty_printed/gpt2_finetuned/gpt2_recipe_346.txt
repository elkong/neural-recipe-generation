TITLE
Cookie Balls III

INGREDIENTS
• 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 cup butter
• 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
• 3/4 cup rolled oats
• 1/4 cup buttermilk
• 2 eggs
• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 teaspoon baking soda

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a medium bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, oats, buttermilk and eggs. Stir in the dry ingredients.
‣ Sift together the flour, baking soda and cinnamon. Stir into the creamed mixture. Pour into the greased and floured 12x8 inch baking pan.
‣ Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until lightly browned. Cool on wire racks.
