TITLE
Potato Salmon Patties

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
• 1/4 cup milk
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/2 cup sour cream
• 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ Meanwhile, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add flour; cook and stir until browned, about 1 minute. Gradually stir in celery, mushroom soup, milk, salt, oregano, pepper, and Cheddar cheese. Continue stirring until smooth. Stir in sour cream, and cook 1 minute more. Remove from heat.
‣ Place macaroni in a 9x13 inch baking dish. In a small bowl, mix the Cheddar cheese, sour cream, and mushrooms. Spoon over macaroni.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, until heated through and lightly browned.
