TITLE
Eclair Cake III

INGREDIENTS
• 1 1/4 cups white sugar
• 1 cup butter
• 1/2 cup brown sugar
• 1 egg
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 teaspoon lemon extract
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon baking powder
• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 cup confectioners' sugar
• 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
• 1/4 teaspoon lemon extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour one 9 inch round pan.
‣ Cream together the 1 1/4 cups white sugar, butter, brown sugar, egg and vanilla. Mix in the lemon flavoring. Add flour, baking powder and baking soda; stir into the sugar mixture until just blended. Pour into prepared pan.
‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
‣ To Make Filling: In a medium bowl, combine confectioners' sugar, cream of tartar and lemon extract. Blend until smooth. Spread over top of cake.
