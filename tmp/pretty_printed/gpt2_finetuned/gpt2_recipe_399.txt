TITLE
Very Berry Blueberry Smoothie

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups frozen blueberries
• 2 cups blueberries
• 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
• 1/2 cup fresh blueberries

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Blend blueberries, strawberries, and blueberries together until blended. Add blueberries and strawberries to blender. Blend until smooth.
