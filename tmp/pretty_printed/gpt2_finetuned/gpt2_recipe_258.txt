TITLE
Sauteed Grouse with Peach-Balsamic Sauce

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh peaches
• 1 cup sliced fresh strawberries
• 1/4 cup honey
• 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
• 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 tablespoons butter
• 2 teaspoons minced fresh rosemary
• 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh sage

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, and stir in the garlic. Cook and stir until the garlic has softened and turned golden brown, about 5 minutes. Mix in the peaches, strawberries, honey, and balsamic vinegar, and season with salt, pepper, butter, rosemary, ginger, and sage. Bring to a simmer over medium-high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer for 20 minutes.
