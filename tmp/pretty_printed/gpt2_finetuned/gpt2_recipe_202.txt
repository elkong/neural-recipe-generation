TITLE
Slow Cooker Tapioca Pudding

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups milk
• 1 1/4 cups white sugar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup water
• 3/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips
• 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place milk, sugar, and vanilla in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Cover, remove from heat, and let stand for 10 minutes.
‣ In a large bowl, beat together oil, water, and chocolate chips. Stir in cream and cinnamon.
‣ Pour into a slow cooker. Stir in cinnamon and nutmeg.
‣ Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours.
