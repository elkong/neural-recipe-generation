TITLE
Poppy Seed Dressing I

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups mayonnaise
• 2 cups sour cream
• 1/2 cup poppy seeds
• 1/2 cup lemon juice
• 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a medium bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, poppy seeds, lemon juice and vinegar. Cover and chill in the refrigerator at least 30 minutes before serving.
