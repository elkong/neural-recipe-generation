TITLE
Alicia's Italian Nachos

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
• 1 (16 ounce) package angel hair pasta
• 1 (16 ounce) jar roasted red peppers, drained
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic until lightly browned. Stir in crushed red pepper flakes, and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Stir in angel hair pasta, and cook until heated through, about 10 minutes.
‣ Stir in roasted red peppers, celery, tomato sauce, and cilantro. Bring to a simmer, and cook until heated through, about 5 minutes.
