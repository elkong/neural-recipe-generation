TITLE
Poppy Seed Chicken Casserole

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked white rice
• 2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup
• 1 1/2 cups sour cream
• 1 cup chopped fresh parsley
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh dill

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ In a saucepan, combine the rice, cream of chicken soup, cream of celery soup, sour cream, parsley, and dill. Bring to a boil.
‣ Spread the rice mixture evenly in the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake for 45 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and lightly browned.
