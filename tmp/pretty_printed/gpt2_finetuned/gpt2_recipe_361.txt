TITLE
Golompke (Beef and Cabbage Casserole)

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1 pound ground beef
• 1 small onion, chopped
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
• 1/2 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/2 cup milk
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
• 1/4 cup butter
• 1/2 cup water
• 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
• 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/2 cup sour cream
• 1 teaspoon white sugar
• 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large skillet over medium heat, melt 1/4 cup melted butter. Saute ground beef until beef is crumbly. Stir in onion and garlic and cook until onion is soft and translucent. Stir in spinach and cook until wilted.
‣ In a large bowl, combine ground beef mixture, spinach, cheese, soup, Cheddar cheese, milk, salt, pepper, thyme, butter, water and flour. Mix well and pour into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Sprinkle with 1/2 cup cheese.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
