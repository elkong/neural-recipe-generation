TITLE
Watermelon Vodka Slush

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups water
• 1 1/4 cups vodka
• 1/2 cup orange juice
• 1/2 cup lemon juice
• 2 cups sliced watermelon

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring the water, vodka, orange juice, and lemon juice to a boil in a saucepan over medium heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until the watermelon has softened, about 10 minutes.
‣ Pour the vodka mixture into a blender. Blend until smooth, about 3 minutes. Pour the lemon juice mixture over the ice, and stir to combine.
