TITLE
Winter Root Vegetable Soup

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup water
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed
• 1 (12 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
• 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
• 1 (14 ounce) can French-style stewed tomatoes
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 teaspoon dried thyme
• 2 cloves garlic, minced
• 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/8 teaspoon ground sage

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large pot, bring the chicken broth, water, broccoli, mixed vegetables, tomatoes, stewed tomatoes, parsley, olive oil, thyme, garlic, basil, oregano, black pepper, sage and pepper to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
