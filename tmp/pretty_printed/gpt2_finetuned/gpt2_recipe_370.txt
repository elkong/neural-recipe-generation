TITLE
Split Pea Soup

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup half-and-half
• 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1 tablespoon chili powder
• 1 teaspoon dried thyme
• 1 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1 teaspoon dried parsley
• 1 teaspoon onion powder
• 1 teaspoon dried basil
• 1 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 2 large split peas
• 1/4 cup chopped onion
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place chicken broth, half-and-half, Worcestershire sauce, chili powder, thyme, cumin, parsley, onion powder, basil, oregano, salt, and parsley into a large saucepan; bring to a boil and reduce heat to low. Simmer until chicken is tender, about 20 minutes.
‣ Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir split peas and onion until onion is tender, 3 to 5 minutes. Pour peas and onion mixture into soup; cook until heated through, about 5 more minutes.
‣ Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
