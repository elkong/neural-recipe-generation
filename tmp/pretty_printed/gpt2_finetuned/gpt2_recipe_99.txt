TITLE
Absolutely Awesome BBQ Sauce

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 cups red or green beans, rinsed and drained
• 1 (2 pound) pork loin, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen cut green peas
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
• 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook the garlic and onion powder in the hot oil until fragrant and golden, 5 to 7 minutes. Add the red beans, and cook for 5 more minutes. Stir in the pork; cook and stir until the pork is no longer pink in the center, about 5 minutes more.
‣ Stir in the peas, cumin, coriander, cayenne, vinegar, salt, and black pepper; bring to a simmer, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until the peas are tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
