TITLE
Yummy Cube Steaks

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked regular long grain white rice
• 1/2 cup water
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 2 cups milk
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 tablespoon ground ginger
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 cup uncooked instant rice
• 1/2 cup raisins
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring the rice, water, flour, milk, ground black pepper, ginger, salt, rice, raisins, and oil to a boil in a saucepan over high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender, and the liquid has been absorbed, 45 to 60 minutes.
