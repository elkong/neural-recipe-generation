TITLE
Amish Friendship Bread Starter

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 cup margarine, softened
• 1 cup brown sugar
• 1 cup white sugar
• 2 eggs
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 cup chopped pecans
• 1 cup raisins

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch tube pan.
‣ In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, and margarine. Cut in brown sugar, white sugar, eggs, and vanilla until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Mix in the chopped nuts, and mix thoroughly.
‣ Pour into prepared pan. Bake for 1 hour, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
