TITLE
Berry Almond Bars

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1/4 cup butter
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
• 1/4 cup sour cream
• 1/2 cup fresh blueberries

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
‣ Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg. Set aside.
‣ Cream together white sugar, butter, and brown sugar. Stir in sour cream and blueberries. Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan.
‣ Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Cool completely before frosting.
