TITLE
No Bake Oatmeal Cookies

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups quick cooking oats
• 1 cup chopped walnuts
• 1 cup raisins
• 1/2 cup butter, softened
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
• 1 egg
• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 cup butter
• 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl, combine oats, walnuts, raisins, butter, brown sugar, brown sugar, egg, vanilla and flour. Stir in baking soda, salt and butter. Mix until smooth.
‣ Roll dough into walnut sized balls and place 2 inches apart on cookie sheet. Flatten slightly and bake for 12 to 15 minutes. Remove to wire rack and cool on wire rack.
