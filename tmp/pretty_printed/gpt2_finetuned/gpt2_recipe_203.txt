TITLE
Nonnie's Ham Chowder

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 large onion, chopped
• 1 pound frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of potato soup
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup
• 1 1/2 cups milk
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1/2 cup chopped cooked ham

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large pot over medium heat, heat oil over medium-high heat. Saute onion until tender, then stir in hash browns and potatoes. Cook until potatoes are tender. Stir in the soup, celery soup, milk, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, nutmeg, chicken broth and ham. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
