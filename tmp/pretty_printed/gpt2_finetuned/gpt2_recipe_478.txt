TITLE
Indian Spiced Rice Treats

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked white rice
• 2 teaspoons garam masala
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
• 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander seed
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
• 1 cup water

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a saucepan, combine the rice, garam masala, cinnamon, ground ginger, turmeric, cumin, coriander, coriander, coriander seed, cinnamon, cloves, ginger, allspice, allspice, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
‣ Mix frozen vegetables, water, and 1 cup of the rice mixture into the saucepan. Bring to a boil, and simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
