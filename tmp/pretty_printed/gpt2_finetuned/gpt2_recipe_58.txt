TITLE
Breaded Brussels Sprouts

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups Italian bread flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup water
• 3 cloves garlic, crushed
• 1 1/2 cups chopped Brussels sprouts
• 1/2 cup chopped red onion
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/4 cup chopped dried apricots
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl; set aside.
‣ Heat oil and water in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir garlic into hot oil; cook and stir until fragrant, about 3 minutes.
‣ Mix Brussels sprouts, red onion, celery, apricots, and Parmesan cheese into the skillet.
‣ Transfer Brussels sprouts mixture to a baking dish; pour olive oil mixture over the Brussels sprouts mixture.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until Brussels sprouts are tender, about 25 minutes.
