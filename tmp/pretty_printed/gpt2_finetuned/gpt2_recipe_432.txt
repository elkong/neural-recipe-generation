TITLE
Bow Ties, Zucchini, Carrots, and Chicken

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup chopped onion
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon paprika
• 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes; drain.
‣ Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and stir in spinach, butter, flour, onion, cumin, salt, pepper, coriander, cayenne pepper, and paprika. Cook and stir until thoroughly combined, about 5 minutes.
‣ Mix Cheddar cheese into the spinach mixture; stir until evenly distributed.
‣ Divide pasta in half and roll each half into a 10-inch round. Arrange 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese over each round. Spread 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese on top of pasta.
‣ Cover baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 15 minutes.
