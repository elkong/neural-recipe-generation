TITLE
Scalloped Corn III

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked white rice
• 2 1/2 cups water
• 3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
• 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
• 1/2 teaspoon pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large saucepan over medium heat, mix the rice, water, and Cheddar cheese. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in the mushroom soup, cream of mushroom soup, salt, and pepper; simmer for another 5 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
