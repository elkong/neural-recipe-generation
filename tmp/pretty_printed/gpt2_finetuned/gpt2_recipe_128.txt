TITLE
Minestrone Soup I

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup half-and-half
• 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1 tablespoon dried oregano
• 1 tablespoon dried basil
• 2 tablespoons garlic powder
• 1 tablespoon dried parsley
• 1 teaspoon onion powder
• 1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
• 1 teaspoon white sugar
• 1/4 cup water

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place the chicken broth, half-and-half, Worcestershire sauce, oregano, basil, garlic powder, parsley, onion powder, basil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, nutmeg, vegetable oil, vinegar, sugar, and water into a blender; blend until smooth. Pour into bowls and serve.
