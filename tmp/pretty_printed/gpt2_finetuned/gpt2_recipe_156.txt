TITLE
Chicken Noodle Soup II

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup half-and-half
• 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
• 1/2 onion, chopped
• 2 cups diced cooked chicken breast meat
• 2 cups frozen peas, thawed
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 3 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup
• 1 1/2 cups milk
• 2 cubes chicken bouillon
• salt and pepper to taste
• 2 cups dry bread cubes

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large pot over medium heat, combine chicken broth, half-and-half, oil, onion, chicken, peas, celery, and cream of chicken soup. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
‣ Add milk, bouillon cubes, salt, and pepper to taste; simmer for another 15 minutes. Pour soup mixture into a small saucepan and bring to a boil over medium heat. Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer until soup is thickened, about 15 minutes. Stir in the bread cubes.
