TITLE
Seared Sea Scallops

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
• 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
• 1 (8 ounce) can crabmeat, drained and flaked
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
• 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
• 1 tablespoon white sugar

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic until lightly browned. Stir in crushed red pepper flakes, and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Stir in mushrooms, and cook until lightly browned, about 1 minute. Mix in crabmeat, and continue cooking until crabmeat is no longer pink. Stir in parsley, lemon juice, and sugar. Reduce heat to low, cover skillet, and simmer until sauce is reduced by half, and the crabmeat is heated through, about 30 minutes.
