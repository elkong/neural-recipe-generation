TITLE
Spicy Coconut and Lime Grilled Shrimp

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh cilantro
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh cilantro
• 2 tablespoons soy sauce
• 1 tablespoon sweet chili sauce
• 1 tablespoon brown sugar
• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon paprika
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 2 teaspoons chili powder
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 4 medium shrimp, peeled and deveined

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in garlic, ginger, cilantro, cilantro, soy sauce, chili sauce, brown sugar, cayenne pepper, paprika, black pepper, salt, chili powder, and cumin. Cook until fragrant, about 10 minutes.
‣ Add shrimp to skillet and cook until shrimp turn pink and turn opaque, about 5 minutes.
