TITLE
Buffalo Chicken Salad

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups mayonnaise
• 2 cups sour cream
• 1/2 cup mayonnaise
• 1 cup prepared mustard
• 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, mayonnaise and mustard.
‣ In a medium bowl, combine the mayonnaise mixture, the mayonnaise dressing, the cheese and the dressing. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes before serving.
