TITLE
Creamy Garlic Sauce

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh basil
• 2 cups fresh or frozen mixed vegetables
• 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
• 1/2 cup chicken broth
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 tablespoons butter
• 2 teaspoons minced fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir garlic and ginger until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add mixed vegetables and chicken broth. Season with salt, pepper, and butter. Reduce heat to low; simmer until vegetables are tender, about 45 minutes. Stir in parsley.
