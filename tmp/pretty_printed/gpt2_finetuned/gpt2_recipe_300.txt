TITLE
Favorite Banana Blueberry Quick Bread

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 cup butter, softened
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1 egg
• 1/4 cup milk
• 1/4 cup honey
• 1/2 cup raisins
• 1/4 cup sour cherries
• 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
• 1/2 cup chopped almonds

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease two 9x5-inch loaf pans.
‣ In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth. Beat in the egg and milk. Stir in the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Beat in the flour mixture until just combined. Stir in the butter mixture and honey.
‣ Stir in the raisins, cherries and nuts. Pour the batter into prepared pans.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
