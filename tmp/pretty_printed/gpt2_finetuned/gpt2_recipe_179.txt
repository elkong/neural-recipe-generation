TITLE
Chef John's Crab Cakes

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1/4 cup milk
• 1 egg, beaten
• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1/4 cup butter
• 1/4 cup water
• 1/4 cup chopped pecans

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, ginger, cloves, and nutmeg. Set aside.
‣ In a medium saucepan, combine 1/4 cup butter, milk, egg, vanilla extract, sugar, and 1/4 cup butter. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, and simmer for 1 1/2 hours, or until thickened.
‣ Remove from heat, and stir in pecans. Stir in cooked crab cakes. Spread onto a baking sheet, and sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup butter.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
