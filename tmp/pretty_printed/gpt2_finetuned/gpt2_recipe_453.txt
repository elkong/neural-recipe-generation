TITLE
Chanterelle Risotto

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked white rice
• 2 1/2 cups water
• 3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1 cup milk
• 2 cups fresh mushrooms, sliced
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 3 tablespoons butter
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 2 cups chopped celery
• 2 cups sliced zucchini
• 3/4 cup butter, melted

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large pot, bring the rice and water to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
‣ In a large bowl, stir together the cheese, milk, mushrooms, and celery. Season with salt, pepper, cinnamon, and celery.
‣ Stir in the rice, and cover. Cook 30 minutes, or until bubbly and lightly browned. Sprinkle in the zucchini, and continue cooking 15 minutes, until heated through.
