TITLE
German Spaghettini

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups Italian-style dry bread crumbs
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup water
• 3 cloves garlic, crushed
• 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
• 1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 cup white wine
• 2 tablespoons butter
• 2 teaspoons minced fresh parsley
• 2 teaspoons minced fresh parsley
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Mix dry bread crumbs, flour, oil, water, garlic, basil, oregano, and salt in a bowl; set aside.
‣ Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in parsley, 1/4 cup parsley, and Parmesan cheese; cook and stir until the cheese has melted and the parsley is lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
‣ Transfer bread mixture to a bowl and stir in 1/4 cup parsley mixture, butter, and parsley until evenly coated. Spread bread mixture evenly in a shallow dish.
‣ Cover dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Brush 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese over bread mixture and sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup parsley mixture.
‣ Bake in preheated oven until golden, about 30 minutes.
