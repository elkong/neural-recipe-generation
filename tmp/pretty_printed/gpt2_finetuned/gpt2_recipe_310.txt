TITLE
Smoked Salmon Pasta with Scotch Whisky

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (8 ounce) package uncooked rigatoni pasta
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1 tablespoon Scotch Whisky
• 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
• 1 cup cubed smoked salmon
• 1 teaspoon ground cumin
• salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add rigatoni and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add butter and cook until butter begins to melt, about 5 minutes. Mix in Scotch Whisky, flour, and smoked salmon. Season with cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook, stirring, until smooth.
