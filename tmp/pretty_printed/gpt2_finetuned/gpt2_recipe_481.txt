TITLE
Rhubarb Custard Pie V

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 3/4 cup shortening
• 1 cup rhubarb, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
• 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
• 1/4 cup white sugar

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9 inch pie pan.
‣ Mix together the flour, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Add the shortening and rhubarb and stir until smooth. Pour the mixture into the pie shell.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 45 minutes, or until set. Cool for 10 minutes before cutting into bars.
