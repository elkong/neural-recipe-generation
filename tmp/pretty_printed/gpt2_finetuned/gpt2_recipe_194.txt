TITLE
Chinese Almond Chicken Stir-Fry

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup soy sauce
• 1/2 cup sesame oil
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
• 2 teaspoons garlic powder
• 1 tablespoon ground ginger
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger root
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
• 1 teaspoon sesame oil
• 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
• 1/2 cup red, white, and yellow bell peppers, cut into bite-size pieces
• 1 cup broccoli florets
• 1 cup chopped broccoli
• 1/4 cup sesame seeds
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint leaves

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large pot over medium heat, combine chicken broth, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, rice vinegar, garlic powder, ground ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and ground ginger. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 20 minutes, or until thickened.
‣ Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Cook and stir chicken until no longer pink in the center, about 10 minutes. Remove chicken from wok, and set aside.
‣ In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil, sesame oil, and chicken until chicken is no longer pink in the center.
‣ Stir in broccoli, broccoli, peppers, and broccoli florets. Cook and stir until vegetables are tender, about 15 minutes.
‣ Stir in mint leaves and cook until heated through.
