TITLE
Dulcia Domestica

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups Italian-style salad dressing
• 1/2 cup prepared Dijon-style mustard
• 1/4 cup prepared Italian-style salad dressing
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
• 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
• 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
• 1/4 cup chopped red onion
• 3/4 cup diced carrots
• 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
• 1/4 cup diced celery
• 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
• 1 cup diced green bell pepper
• 1 cup diced red potatoes
• 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
• 1 cup diced yellow squash
• 1/4 cup diced green onions
• 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
• 1/4 cup diced yellow potatoes
• 1/4 cup diced carrots
• 1/4 cup diced green onions
• 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
• 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
• 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Combine Italian-style salad dressing, Dijon-style mustard, Italian-style dressing, salt, black pepper, celery, mushrooms, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, red onion, carrots, red bell pepper, celery, red bell pepper, and red potatoes in a large bowl.
‣ Pour tomato mixture, vegetables, carrots, red bell pepper, red onion, and carrots into a slow cooker. Add green onions and red bell pepper. Stir to coat vegetables. Cover, and cook on Low 6 to 8 hours.
‣ Mix green bell pepper, red bell pepper, and red onion into vegetable mixture.
‣ Stir in tomatoes and parsley. Season with lemon juice.
