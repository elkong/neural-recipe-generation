TITLE
Chicken Foster

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup water
• 1 (2 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces
• 1 cup milk
• 2 cups diced onion
• 3 cloves garlic, crushed
• 1 teaspoon dried thyme
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large pot over medium heat, combine the chicken broth and water. Bring to a boil and stir in the chicken pieces, milk, onion, garlic, thyme, pepper, salt, cayenne pepper, and parsley. Reduce heat and simmer for about 20 minutes.
