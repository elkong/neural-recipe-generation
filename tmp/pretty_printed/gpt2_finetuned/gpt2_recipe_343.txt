TITLE
Sweet and Sour Turkey Patties

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
• 2 teaspoons seasoned salt
• 1 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1 (8 ounce) package baby carrots
• 1 cup sliced celery
• 1 cup sliced onion
• 1 cup chopped fresh mushrooms
• 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
• 2 tablespoons chili powder
• 1/4 cup brown sugar
• 1 tablespoon water
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large bowl, combine the bread crumbs, seasoned salt, onion powder, garlic powder, black pepper, oregano, celery, carrots, celery, onion, and mushrooms.
‣ In a separate bowl, combine the chili powder, brown sugar, water, cinnamon, and salt.
‣ Pour into a shallow 8x8 inch baking dish. Cover, and refrigerate overnight.
‣ Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until golden brown.
