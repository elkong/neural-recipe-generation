TITLE
Frosted Corn Flake Cereal Clusters

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups whole wheat flour
• 2 cups whole wheat flour
• 1 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup shortening
• 1/4 cup water

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large bowl, mix together whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, and shortening. Stir in water. Gradually stir in flour mixture until dough comes together. Roll out dough to 1/4 inch thickness. Cut into desired shapes using cookie cutters. Place onto waxed paper. Store in airtight container.
