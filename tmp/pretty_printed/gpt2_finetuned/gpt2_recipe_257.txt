TITLE
French Watermelon Bars

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
• 2 cups water
• 1 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1 1/2 cups milk
• 3/4 cup white sugar
• 2 eggs
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 cup water
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
‣ Combine flour, 1/2 cup sugar, cocoa powder, and water. Stir in baking soda, salt, milk, and sugar. Beat until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla. Pour batter into prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes. Cool in pan 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
‣ Combine remaining 1/2 cup sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a small bowl. Beat until light and fluffy. Pour into cooled graham cracker crust.
‣ Bake in preheated oven until set and golden, about 15 minutes.
