TITLE
Risotto alla Milanese

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked white rice
• 2 1/2 cups water
• 3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1 cup milk
• 2 cups sliced mushrooms
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
• 1 cup uncooked instant rice
• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a saucepan bring water to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
‣ In a medium bowl combine the cheese, milk, mushrooms, condensed soup, rice and cheese mixture. Stir and stir until well blended.
‣ Pour into a 2 quart casserole dish and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese. Bake at 400 degrees F (205 degrees C) for 30 minutes or until heated through.
