TITLE
Lemon and Thyme Lamb Chops

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon lemon juice
• 1/4 cup uncooked white rice
• 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
• 3 tablespoons soy sauce
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh lemon grass
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
• 2 tablespoons lemon juice
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 teaspoon sesame oil
• 2 tablespoons honey
• 1 cup sesame seeds

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic; cook and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute. Mix in lemon juice, rice, lemon zest, and soy sauce; stir. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender, about 15 minutes. Stir in lemon grass, mint, cilantro, and 2 tablespoons lemon juice. Simmer until heated through, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in sesame oil, honey, and sesame seeds.
