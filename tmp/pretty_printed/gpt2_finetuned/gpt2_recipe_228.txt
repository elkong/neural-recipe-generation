TITLE
Tangy Honey Glazed Ham

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 cup honey
• 1 cup soy sauce
• 1 cup packed brown sugar
• 1/2 cup white vinegar
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 tablespoon ground ginger
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground gingerbread
• 1 tablespoon white sugar

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Stir in honey, soy sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, pepper, ground ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and gingerbread. Cook and stir until sugar has dissolved, then pour into prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove from heat, and sprinkle with sugar.
