TITLE
Summer Anytime Crisp Corn Salad

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chopped, cooked corn
• 2 (14 ounce) cans sweet corn
• 1 (15 ounce) can creamed corn
• 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
• 1 cup chopped celery rings
• 1 cup sour cream
• 1/4 cup chopped pecans

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Combine corn, corn, creamed corn, black beans, and celery in a large bowl. Mix in sour cream, pecans, and enough dressing to moisten. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
