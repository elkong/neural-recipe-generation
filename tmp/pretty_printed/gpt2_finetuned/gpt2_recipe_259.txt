TITLE
Best Green Beans

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 large onion, chopped
• 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
• 1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
• 1/2 cup brown sugar
• 2 cloves garlic, minced
• 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
• 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir onion in hot oil until transparent, about 5 minutes. Add green beans, corn, salt, black pepper, celery, tomato sauce, brown sugar, garlic, oregano, cumin, basil, parsley, and pepper; bring to a boil and cook until flavors blend, about 5 minutes.
