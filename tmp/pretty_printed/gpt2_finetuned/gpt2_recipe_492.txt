TITLE
White Pizza Dip

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (16 ounce) package tri-colored dough (such as White Pizza Rollers®)
• 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
• 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream cheese
• 1 (4 ounce) can sliced mushrooms
• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
‣ Place the tri-colored dough into a mixing bowl. Whisk together the sour cream, sour cream cheese, mushrooms, and mozzarella cheese in a bowl until well blended. Spread the mixture into the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of the mixture.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until bubbling and lightly browned, 30 to 35 minutes.
