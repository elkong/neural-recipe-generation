TITLE
Refreshing Cucumber Lemonade

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1 cup lemon juice
• 1/2 cup orange juice
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup water

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a saucepan, combine sugar and lemon juice. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until sugar dissolves.
‣ In a large bowl, mix together orange juice, vegetable oil, water and sugar. Stir in lemon and vinegar. Serve over ice.
