TITLE
Melt-In-Your-Mouth Meat Loaf

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
• 2 teaspoons seasoned salt
• 1 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
• 1/4 teaspoon dried rosemary
• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 pound ground beef
• 1 cup ketchup
• 1 cup brown sugar
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 2 eggs, beaten
• 1/4 cup milk
• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Mix bread crumbs, seasoned salt, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, rosemary, allspice, black pepper, and ground beef together in a large bowl.
‣ Mix ketchup, brown sugar, vegetable oil, eggs, milk, Worcestershire sauce, and cayenne pepper together in a separate bowl; pour into ground beef mixture and stir to coat. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
‣ Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Spread beef mixture into a 9x5-inch loaf pan and bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center and a meat thermometer inserted near the bone registers 145 degrees F (63 degrees C), about 1 hour. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
