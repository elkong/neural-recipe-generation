TITLE
Rhubarb Upside Down Cake III

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1 cup packed brown sugar
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup water
• 3 eggs
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon baking powder
• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 cup chopped rhubarb

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9 inch round cake pans.
‣ In a large bowl, cream together the white sugar, brown sugar and butter until smooth. Beat in the oil and water. Combine the eggs, flour, baking powder and baking soda; stir into the creamed mixture. Stir in the chopped rhubarb. Pour batter into prepared pans.
‣ Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Cool cake in pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
