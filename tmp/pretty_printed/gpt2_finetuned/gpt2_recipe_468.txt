TITLE
Quick Yeast Rolls

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 cup shortening
• 2 cups warm water
• 1 egg
• 1/4 cup milk
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar for dusting

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Mix flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, allspice, ginger, and cloves together in a bowl. Add shortening, water, egg, and milk together in a separate bowl until smooth. Pour egg mixture into flour mixture and stir until batter is smooth. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
‣ Roll out the dough to 1/2 inch thickness and cut into desired shapes using cookie cutters. Roll each shape into a 2-inch diameter log about 3/4 inch thick. Place logs 2 inches apart onto cookie sheets.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until edges are slightly browned, 7 to 9 minutes. Remove cookies from oven and let cool on wire racks.
‣ To Make Filling: In a medium bowl, combine sugar and confectioners' sugar. Beat until smooth, then beat in 1/2 cup confectioner's sugar.
