TITLE
London Broil

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and pepper, and cook for a few seconds until the garlic has softened and turned translucent. Stir in the spinach and Cheddar cheese. Season with parsley. Cover, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 5 minutes, or until spinach is tender.
