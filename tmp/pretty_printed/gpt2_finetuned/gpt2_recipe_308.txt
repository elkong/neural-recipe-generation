TITLE
Indian Hot Curried Mangos with Tofu

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
• 1 (16 ounce) package bean sprouts, rinsed and drained
• 1 tablespoon ground turmeric
• 1 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1 teaspoon ground coriander
• 1 teaspoon garam masala
• 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 tablespoons honey
• 1 cup water
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
• 1 teaspoon lime juice
• 1/4 cup plain yogurt
• 1 tablespoon soy sauce
• 1 teaspoon honey
• 1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for about 2 minutes. Stir in the bean sprouts, turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala, red pepper flakes, salt, black pepper, and honey. Cook and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
‣ Stir in the water and vegetable oil, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until the bean sprouts begin to soften, about 10 minutes.
‣ Whisk together the yogurt, soy sauce, and honey in a small bowl until smooth. Pour in the vinegar and lime juice and bring to a simmer. Pour in the honey-water mixture, and simmer for about 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat, and stir in the cilantro.
