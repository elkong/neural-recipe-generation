TITLE
Chocolate Peanut Butter Fudge

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
• 1/2 cup peanut butter
• 1/4 cup milk
• 1 cup milk chocolate chips
• 1/4 cup peanut butter
• 1/4 cup butter
• 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Melt the chocolate and peanut butter together in a saucepan over low heat.
‣ Stir together the milk, milk chocolate chips, peanut butter, brown sugar, vanilla extract, salt, and cinnamon. Pour into the chocolate mixture and stir until well blended. Refrigerate until firm, at least 4 hours.
