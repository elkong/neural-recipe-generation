TITLE
Coconut Avocado Smoothie

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups frozen mango - peeled, seeded, and cubed
• 1/2 cup plain yogurt
• 1/4 cup lime juice
• 1/4 cup honey
• 2 tablespoons soy sauce
• 1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a blender, combine mango, yogurt, lime juice, honey, soy sauce, and pickle relish. Blend until smooth.
