TITLE
Easy Tortellini Soup

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 large onion, chopped
• 1 pound frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent roll dough
• 1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-style diced tomatoes
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 (16 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1 (10 ounce) can cream of mushroom soup
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add spinach and cook for 5 more minutes.
‣ Stir in diced tomatoes, salt, pepper, and tomatoes. Add chicken broth, oregano, basil, and black pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 minutes, or until thickened.
‣ Toss soup with spinach and cream of mushroom soup. Garnish with Parmesan cheese.
