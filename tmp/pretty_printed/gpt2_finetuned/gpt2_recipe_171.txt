TITLE
Stromboli Cake III

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen whipped topping, thawed
• 1 (12 ounce) package frozen bite-size strawberries, thawed
• 1 (14 ounce) package can cherry pie filling
• 1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans
• 1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
• 1/4 cup butter

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.
‣ In a large bowl, stir together cake mix and whipped topping. In a small bowl, stir together pineapple, strawberry filling and pinto beans. Pour batter into prepared pan.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
‣ To make the filling: In a medium bowl, combine pineapple, strawberry filling, and butter. Spread over the top of the cake.
