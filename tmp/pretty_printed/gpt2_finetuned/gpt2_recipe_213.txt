TITLE
Sour Cream and Chive Rolls

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1 egg
• 1/4 cup sour cream
• 1 cup shredded coconut
• 1/4 cup chopped walnuts

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, salt, pepper, and cloves. Cut in butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Mix in sugar, egg, sour cream, and coconut. Press mixture into the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle the walnuts evenly over the top.
‣ Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until lightly browned.
