TITLE
Creamy Cucumber and Tomato Salad

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups mayonnaise
• 2 cups sour cream
• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• 1 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
• 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 2 teaspoons dried oregano
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 cup pitted and chopped cucumber
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
• 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh tomatoes
• 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, Colby-Monterey Jack cheese, celery, green bell pepper, red bell pepper, celery, onion, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Chill until ready to serve.
‣ Toss together the pitted and chopped cucumber, basil, and red bell pepper in a bowl. Sprinkle with feta cheese to serve.
