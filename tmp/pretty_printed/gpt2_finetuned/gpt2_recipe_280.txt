TITLE
Easy Never Forgotten Guacamole

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups guacamole
• 2 teaspoons lemon juice
• 1 teaspoon lemon zest
• 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
• 2 cups crushed tortilla chips

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a blender, combine guacamole, lemon juice, lemon zest, and lemon zest. Blend until smooth. Place in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover with tortilla chips.
‣ Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven, or until chips are lightly browned.
