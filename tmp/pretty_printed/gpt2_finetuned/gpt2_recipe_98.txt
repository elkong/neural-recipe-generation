TITLE
Grilled Marinated Shrimp

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
• 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
• 1 (8 ounce) can shrimp, drained
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
• 1/4 cup mayonnaise
• 1/2 cup sour cream
• 2 tablespoons lemon juice

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in the garlic, and cook until the garlic has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes. Mix in red pepper flakes, and cook for 5 more minutes. Set aside.
‣ Mix the mushrooms and shrimp into the skillet. Season with parsley, mayonnaise, sour cream, and lemon juice. Cook and stir until shrimp is pink and tender, about 5 minutes.
