TITLE
Savory Crab Stuffed Mushrooms

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 large yellow onion, chopped
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
• 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• 2 tablespoons butter
• 2 teaspoons minced fresh parsley
• salt and pepper to taste
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• 2 tablespoons butter, melted

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute onion and garlic until soft, about 5 minutes. Stir in mushrooms and soup; cook, stirring, until mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes more.
‣ Stir in mozzarella, 1/4 cup mozzarella, 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, and 1 tablespoon butter. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
‣ Reduce heat to low and simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
‣ Stir in parsley and parsley; continue cooking until cheese is melted and bubbly. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
