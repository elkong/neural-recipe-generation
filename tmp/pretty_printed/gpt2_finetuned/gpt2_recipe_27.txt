TITLE
BBQ Sauce for the Slow Cooker

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cup barbecue sauce
• 1/2 cup ketchup
• 1/4 cup brown sugar
• 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
• 1/4 cup vinegar
• 1/4 cup water

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a medium bowl, stir together the barbecue sauce, ketchup, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, vinegar and water. Mix well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
