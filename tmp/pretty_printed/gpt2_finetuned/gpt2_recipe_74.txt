TITLE
Italian Spaghetti with Ham and Tomato

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (8 ounce) package spaghetti
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 2 cups milk
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
• 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ In a medium bowl, combine flour, milk, condensed soup and parsley. Stir into pasta. Pour into prepared baking dish.
‣ Sprinkle top with Cheddar cheese and bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
