TITLE
Waldorf Salad I

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups mayonnaise
• 2 cups mayonnaise
• 1 cup sesame seeds
• 1/2 cup lemon juice
• 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
• 1 teaspoon white sugar
• 1 teaspoon cornstarch
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint leaves
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, mayonnaise, sesame seeds, lemon juice, vinegar, sugar, cornstarch, and mint until well blended. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
