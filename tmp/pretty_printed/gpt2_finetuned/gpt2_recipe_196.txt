TITLE
A Fantastic Margarita

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (12 fluid ounce) cans cola-flavored carbonated beverage
• 1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen limeade concentrate, thawed
• 1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
• 1 (12 fluid ounce) can frozen limeade concentrate, thawed
• 1 (4 ounce) can sliced black olives
• 1 (6 ounce) can sliced black olives
• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Fill a pitcher with cola, limeade, lemonade, and lemonade. Stir in the cola, limeade, and limeade. Garnish with crumbled cheese.
