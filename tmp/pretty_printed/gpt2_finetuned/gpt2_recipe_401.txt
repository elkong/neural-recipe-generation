TITLE
Alligator Pears and Apples

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups apricot halves
• 2 cups apple juice
• 1 cup orange juice
• 1/4 cup cider vinegar
• 1/4 cup water
• 1/4 cup brown sugar
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place apricots in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes. Peel and dice.
‣ In a large glass or metal mixing bowl combine the apples, apricots, apple juice, orange juice, cider vinegar, water, brown sugar and cinnamon. Chill and serve.
