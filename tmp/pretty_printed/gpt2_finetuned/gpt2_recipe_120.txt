TITLE
Better Help for Hamburger

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups milk
• 1 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
• 1 tablespoon warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 3/4 cup vegetable oil
• 3 eggs, beaten
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water. Let stand for 5 minutes.
‣ In a large bowl, combine flour, oil, and yeast mixture. Stir in eggs and sugar. Stir in vanilla. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
‣ Pour dough into a greased 9 x 13 inch baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 45 minutes, or until golden brown.
