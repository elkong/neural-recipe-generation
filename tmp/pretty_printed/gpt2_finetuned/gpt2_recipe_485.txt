TITLE
Rice Pudding With Dates

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups white rice
• 1 cup water
• 1 egg
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup water
• 3/4 cup dates, pitted and chopped
• 1/4 cup chopped pecans
• 1/4 cup chopped almonds

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a medium saucepan, bring 1 cup of water to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer 30 minutes, or until rice is tender.
‣ In a large bowl, beat egg and vanilla until smooth. Gradually whisk in oil, 1/4 cup water and dates. Stir in pecans, almonds and dates.
‣ Pour 1/4 cup of the mixture into a resealable plastic bag. Add the rice, seal, and refrigerate overnight.
