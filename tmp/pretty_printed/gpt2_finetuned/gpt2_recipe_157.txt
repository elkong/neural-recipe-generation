TITLE
Chicken Curry I

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 large onion, chopped
• 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-sized pieces
• 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
• 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
• 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
• salt and pepper to taste
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, and cook and stir the onion until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the chicken, and cook until browned, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes. Stir in the garbanzo beans, coconut milk, tomatoes, cumin, salt, and pepper. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 15 minutes. Stir in the cilantro before serving.
