TITLE
Moon Cake III

INGREDIENTS
• 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 cup butter
• 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
• 3/4 cup white sugar
• 1 egg
• 1/3 cup water
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/2 cup chopped pecans

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour three 9 inch round pans.
‣ In a large bowl, cream the butter and white sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg, then stir in the flour, salt, baking soda, cinnamon and 1 cup of the brown sugar. Mix in the butter mixture. Combine the egg, water, and vanilla. Blend until smooth. Pour batter into the prepared pans.
‣ Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
