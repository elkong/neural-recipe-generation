TITLE
Sweet Green Bean Bundles

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups frozen green beans
• 2 cups chopped sweet onions
• 1 cup raisins
• 1/2 cup brown sugar
• 2 cups rolled oats
• 1/2 cup cornstarch
• 1 tablespoon water

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place frozen green beans, sweet onions, raisins, and brown sugar in a large bowl. In a separate bowl, stir together the oats, cornstarch, and water. Pour into the bean mixture, and stir just until blended. Cover, and refrigerate overnight.
