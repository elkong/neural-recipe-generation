TITLE
Sour Milk Spice Cake

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups cake flour
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 cup sour milk
• 1 cup chopped walnuts
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
• 1 cup white sugar
• 2 eggs
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9 inch round cake pans.
‣ Sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa, salt, baking soda and cinnamon. Set aside.
‣ In a large bowl, beat together sour milk, chopped nuts and cream cheese. Stir in 1 cup sugar and eggs. Mix until smooth. Pour batter into prepared pans.
‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
