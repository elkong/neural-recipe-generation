TITLE
Cranberry Brisket

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups cranberries
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup water

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Combine cranberries, sugar, flour and oil in a bowl. Use a rubber spatula to stir cranberries and water together in a clean dish or bowl. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
