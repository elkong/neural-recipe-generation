TITLE
Moroccan Chicken and Whole Grain Couscous

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup uncooked long grain white rice
• 1 cup uncooked couscous
• 1 (8 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and chopped
• 1 1/2 cups couscous
• 1/2 cup honey
• 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
• 1/4 cup red wine seed
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1/4 cup chopped onion
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring chicken broth, rice, couscous, water chestnuts, couscous, honey, vinegar, red wine, red wine seed, salt, and pepper to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender and the liquid has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in onion and cilantro; cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
