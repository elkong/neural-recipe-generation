TITLE
Brioche Cake III

INGREDIENTS
• 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 cup butter, softened
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1 egg
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 cup buttermilk
• 1 1/2 cups raisins
• 1/4 cup chopped pecans

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 8-inch round cake pans.
‣ Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
‣ Cream together butter and sugar. Add egg and vanilla. Beat well.
‣ Sift together the flour mixture, sifted flour, and baking soda. Add alternately with the buttermilk to the creamed mixture, stirring until just incorporated. Stir in the raisins and pecans. Pour batter into prepared pans.
‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes.
