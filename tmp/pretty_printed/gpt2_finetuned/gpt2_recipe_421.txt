TITLE
Alaska Salmon Bake with Pecan Crunch Coating

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked white rice
• 2 1/2 cups water
• 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of salmon soup
• 1 cup milk
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1 cup chopped pecans

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ In a large bowl, combine rice, water, Cheddar cheese, cream of salmon soup, milk, and cream cheese. Stir until smooth. Pour into prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until golden brown. Cool 10 minutes, then cut into squares to serve.
