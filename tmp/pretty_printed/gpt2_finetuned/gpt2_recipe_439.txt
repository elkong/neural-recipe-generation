TITLE
Hot Spinach and Artichoke Salad

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/4 cup mayonnaise
• 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1 (8 ounce) package baby spinach leaves
• 1/4 cup chopped red onion

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
‣ Place spinach in a medium bowl, and pour in mayonnaise, vinegar, salt, pepper and celery. Mix well, and spread into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Sprinkle spinach with a little red onion, and drizzle with dressing.
‣ Bake for 35 to 40 minutes in the preheated oven, until wilted and lightly browned.
