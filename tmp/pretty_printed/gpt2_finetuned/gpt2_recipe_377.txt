TITLE
Refreshing Summertime Salad

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups mayonnaise
• 2 cups mayonnaise
• 1 cup sesame seeds
• 1/2 cup lemon juice
• 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
• 1 teaspoon white sugar
• 1 teaspoon curry powder
• 1 teaspoon lemon zest
• 1 teaspoon honey
• 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
• 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Mix together the mayonnaise, mayonnaise, sesame seeds, lemon juice, vinegar, sugar, curry powder, lemon zest, honey, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Chill for at least 4 hours before serving.
