TITLE
American Lasagna

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1 pound ground beef
• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Monterey Jack cheese
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
• 1 cup milk
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
• 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1 (14.5 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
• 1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
• 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
• 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
• 2 tablespoons butter, melted

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes; drain.
‣ In a large skillet over medium heat, cook ground beef until evenly brown; drain fat. Stir in spinach, 1/4 cup melted butter, ground beef, Cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, cream of mushroom soup, milk, onion, green bell pepper, salt, pepper, thyme, oregano, basil, and garlic powder. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
‣ Stir corn into the simmering mixture and cook 1 minute. Stir in sour cream and cheese until heated through, about 2 minutes. Pour mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup melted butter.
