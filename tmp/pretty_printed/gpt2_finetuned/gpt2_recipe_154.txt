TITLE
Easy Lentil Feta Wraps

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked lentils
• 2 teaspoons olive oil
• 1 teaspoon minced garlic
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
• 2 cups fresh spinach, washed and dried
• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat olive oil. Cook and stir garlic in oil until fragrant. Add basil and spinach. Cook and stir until spinach is wilted.
‣ In a large bowl, mix together lentils, olive oil, and garlic. Sprinkle with basil and spinach mixture.
‣ Spread each tortilla with spinach mixture. Top each with Cheddar cheese, and a sprinkle of parsley.
