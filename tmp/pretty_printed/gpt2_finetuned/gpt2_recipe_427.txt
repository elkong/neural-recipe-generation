TITLE
Seafood Sandwich

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh cilantro
• 1 teaspoon minced fresh cilantro
• 2 tablespoons soy sauce
• 1 tablespoon fish sauce
• 1 teaspoon lemon juice
• 1 teaspoon brown sugar
• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon sesame oil
• 2 pounds cod fillets
• 1/4 cup sliced green onions

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Add garlic, ginger, and cilantro; cook and stir until fragrant, about 5 minutes.
‣ Stir soy sauce, fish sauce, lemon juice, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, red pepper flakes, and salt into garlic mixture; cook and stir until Worcestershire sauce is dissolved, 5 to 10 minutes.
‣ Remove fish from heat. Discard garlic mixture.
‣ Heat sesame oil in a separate skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir green onions in hot oil until browned, about 5 minutes.
