TITLE
Broiled Parmesan-Lemon Tilapia  

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1/4 cup lemon juice
• 1 teaspoon minced garlic
• 1/4 cup olive oil
• 1/4 cup butter, softened
• 1 pound tilapia fillets
• salt and ground black pepper to taste
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil and lemon juice in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute garlic until lightly browned. Stir in olive oil, butter, and tilapia. Reduce heat to medium-low, and continue cooking until the fish flakes easily with a fork, 5 to 10 minutes. Season with salt and black pepper.
‣ Stir in Parmesan cheese, and cook until the cheese melts, about 5 minutes.
