TITLE
Fabulous French Loaves

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups bread flour
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 3/4 cup shortening
• 1 egg
• 1 1/4 cups milk
• 3 eggs, beaten
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, cinnamon, cloves and shortening.
‣ In a small bowl, combine egg, milk and eggs. Stir in flour mixture, then add vanilla.
‣ Divide dough in half and shape into loaves. Place loaves in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover and refrigerate for 3 hours.
‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 60 to 90 minutes.
