TITLE
Dennie's Fresh Lobster Salad

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (6 ounce) lobster tails
• 1/2 cup mayonnaise
• 1/2 cup creamy salad dressing (e.g. Miracle Whip)
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup mayonnaise
• 1/4 cup mayonnaise
• 1/4 cup chopped green onions
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/4 cup minced onion
• 2 tablespoons lemon juice
• 1/4 cup mayonnaise
• 2 tablespoons mustard powder
• 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Place lobster tails in a bowl, and cover with 1/2 cup mayonnaise. Set aside.
‣ In a small bowl, mix together salad dressing, celery, mayonnaise, mayonnaise, green onions, celery, onion, lemon juice, mayonnaise, mustard, and Worcestershire sauce. Stir in dill. Cover, and refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
‣ Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, and lightly oil grate.
‣ Grill lobster tails for 5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness, basting frequently with mayonnaise mixture.
