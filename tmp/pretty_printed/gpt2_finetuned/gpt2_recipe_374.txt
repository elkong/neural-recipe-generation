TITLE
Best in Show Blackberry Cobbler

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups fresh blackberries
• 2 cups fresh blackberries
• 1 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1 cup white sugar
• 1/4 cup butter
• 2 eggs
• 1/2 cup fresh blackberries

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
‣ Mix together the blackberries, blackberries, flour, sugar, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and butter in a large bowl; pour into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Bake in the preheated oven until the top is crisp, about 30 minutes.
‣ Remove from oven and let cool completely before serving.
