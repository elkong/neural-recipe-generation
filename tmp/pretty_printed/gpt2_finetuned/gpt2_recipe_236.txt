TITLE
Hamburger Quiche

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 cup milk
• 1/4 cup salt
• 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 tablespoon ground cumin
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup
• 1/4 cup water
• 1/2 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
• 2 tablespoons butter, melted

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together flour, milk, salt, pepper, and cumin. Press mixture into the bottom of a 9 inch pie pan.
‣ In a large skillet over medium heat, cook and stir celery until tender. Pour in mushroom soup and cream of celery soup; stir to combine.
‣ Pour water into pan and pour mixture over crust. Sprinkle with shredded Cheddar cheese.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until bubbly.
