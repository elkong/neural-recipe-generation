TITLE
Curried Cashew, Pear, and Grape Salad

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
• 1/2 cup cashews
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
• 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
• 1 tablespoon lemon juice
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
• 1/4 cup honey
• 1/2 cup soy sauce
• 1 tablespoon sesame oil
• 1 1/2 tablespoons water
• 2 tablespoons grated fresh ginger root
• 1 tablespoon white sugar
• 1 tablespoon honey
• 1 tablespoon lemon juice
• 2 tablespoons fish sauce
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Rinse with cold water and drain.
‣ In a medium bowl combine macaroni, spinach, feta cheese, cashews, celery, walnuts, cilantro, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, vinegar, honey, soy sauce, sesame oil, water, ginger, sugar, and honey. Stir well and refrigerate for 2 hours or overnight.
‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
‣ In a small bowl combine fish sauce, cilantro, and fresh ginger. Mix well and serve.
