TITLE
Gluten Free Chocolate Cake

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1 cup shortening
• 1 cup milk
• 3 eggs
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/4 cup buttermilk
• 2 tablespoons butter
• 1 cup chopped pecans

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch Bundt pan. Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
‣ In a large bowl, cream together the sugar, shortening and milk. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Gradually blend in the flour mixture alternately with the buttermilk. Pour batter into prepared pan.
‣ Bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
