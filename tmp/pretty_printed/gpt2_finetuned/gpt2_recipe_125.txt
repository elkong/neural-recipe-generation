TITLE
Ibby's Grasshopper Pie

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (9 inch) pie shell
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/4 cup shortening
• 1/4 cup water
• 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
• 3/4 cup butter, softened
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1 cup heavy whipping cream
• 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
‣ Mix flour, shortening, water, butter, sugar, salt, nutmeg, ginger, cloves, and cream together in a bowl until thoroughly combined. Press dough into pie shell; pinch edges to seal. Place dough in a lightly greased 9-inch pie plate.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes. Remove from oven; sprinkle with parsley and serve immediately.
