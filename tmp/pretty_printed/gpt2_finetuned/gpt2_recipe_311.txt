TITLE
Baked Zucchini

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups grated zucchini
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup water
• 3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/4 cup chopped onion
• 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ In a large bowl, mix together flour, vegetable oil, water, Cheddar cheese, onion, nutmeg, salt and pepper. Blend in zucchini. Spread into prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven, until zucchini is tender.
