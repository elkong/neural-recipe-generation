TITLE
Spinach Pie III

INGREDIENTS
• 1 1/4 cups fresh spinach leaves
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/2 cup milk
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl, stir together the spinach, flour, milk and sugar. Transfer to the prepared pie crust.
‣ Bake for 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown. Cool, and cut into squares.
