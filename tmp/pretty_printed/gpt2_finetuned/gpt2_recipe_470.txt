TITLE
Grilled Turkey Reubens

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 2 cups red or green beans, rinsed and drained
• 1 tablespoon olive oil
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat, and lightly oil grate.
‣ Whisk together the olive oil, garlic, onion powder, and black pepper in a bowl; set aside.
‣ Place the red or green beans in a bowl, and drizzle with 1 tablespoon olive oil. Sprinkle with parsley to coat.
‣ Grill the turkey until the seeds start to pop and the meat is beginning to brown, 5 to 10 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
