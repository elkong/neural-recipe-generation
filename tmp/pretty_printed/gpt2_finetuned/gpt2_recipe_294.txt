TITLE
Authentic Bahamian Peas and Rice

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked basmati rice
• 1 (16 ounce) can coconut milk
• 1/2 cup lemon juice
• 2 cups water
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 2 teaspoons garam masala
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Mix the basmati rice, coconut milk, lemon juice, water, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and cloves together in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer until the rice is tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
‣ Pour the rice mixture into a slow cooker, and stir in the cilantro, cinnamon, and cloves. Cover, and cook on Low for 8 to 10 hours, or until the rice is tender and the cilantro has wilted. Stir in the cinnamon mixture during the last 5 minutes of cooking.
