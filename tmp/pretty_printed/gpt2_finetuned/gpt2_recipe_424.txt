TITLE
Spinach Stuffed Chicken Breasts

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups Italian-style dried bread crumbs
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/2 cup milk
• 2 cups fresh spinach, washed and dried
• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
‣ In a medium bowl, mix the bread crumbs, flour, and milk. Cut in the spinach with a fork until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Dip each chicken breast in the bread crumb mixture, then roll in the milk mixture. Place coated chicken breasts in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 10 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and juices run clear.
