TITLE
Easy Baked Indian Samosas

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups peeled, seeded, and chopped tomatoes
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup water
• 3 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon curry powder
• 1 teaspoon cumin
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
• 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon ground coriander
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
• 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon ground coriander
• 1/2 cup shredded coconut
• 1/2 cup chopped peanuts

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Place tomatoes in a large bowl, and add onion, oil, water, garlic, curry powder, cumin, salt, turmeric, cayenne, cinnamon, ground ginger, cayenne, coriander, cumin, red pepper flakes, turmeric, cayenne, cinnamon, ginger, and cayenne pepper in a blender. Blend until smooth. Transfer to a large mixing bowl, and sprinkle with nuts.
‣ Arrange mashed tomatoes, coconut, peanuts, and chicken mixture on a baking sheet.
‣ Bake in preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear, about 20 minutes.
