TITLE
Chicken Enchiladas IV

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/2 cup sour cream
• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Monterey Jack cheese
• 1/4 cup sour cream
• 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
• salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together spinach, 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese, 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese, sour cream, Monterey Jack cheese, sour cream and cilantro. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Transfer to the prepared baking dish, and sprinkle with remaining Cheddar cheese.
‣ Bake 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly.
