TITLE
Shirred Eggs

INGREDIENTS
• 2 eggs
• 1 1/2 cups milk
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1/4 cup butter
• 2 tablespoons butter
• 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
• 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
• 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 cup chopped walnuts

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together eggs, milk, flour, salt, baking soda, ground cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and sugar until smooth.
‣ Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
‣ In a saucepan, heat brown sugar, butter, and vanilla until melted. Remove from heat, and stir in brown sugar mixture and 1/4 cup flour. Mix in nuts. Spread batter evenly into the prepared pan.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 50 to 60 minutes, or until a knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
