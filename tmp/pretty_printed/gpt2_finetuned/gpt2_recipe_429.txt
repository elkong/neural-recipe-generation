TITLE
Sausage Casserole III

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of celery soup
• 1 cup milk
• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/4 cup sour cream

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Mix together cream of chicken soup, mushroom soup, cream of celery soup, milk, Cheddar cheese, sour cream and 1/4 cup sour cream in a large bowl until well blended. Form mixture into 1 inch balls and place on prepared baking dish.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes.
