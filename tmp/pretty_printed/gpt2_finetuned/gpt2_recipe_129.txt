TITLE
Cheesy Bacon and Egg Brunch Casserole

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/2 cup butter, melted
• 1/4 cup milk
• salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ In a medium bowl, combine spinach, 2 cups of the Cheddar cheese, 1/2 cup of the melted butter and milk. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir into cooked pasta.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until heated through and bubbly.
