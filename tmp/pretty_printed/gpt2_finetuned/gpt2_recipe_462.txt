TITLE
Amazing Crab Shells

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1 pound crabmeat
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon dried basil
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/2 teaspoon paprika
• 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1 (4 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking dish.
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ In a large skillet over medium heat, cook and stir cooked macaroni until al dente; drain.
‣ In a small bowl combine the spinach, butter, crabmeat, salt, basil, black pepper, paprika, onion powder and cayenne pepper; mix well.
‣ Transfer mixture to prepared baking dish. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
