TITLE
Mexican Cornbread II

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/2 cup butter, softened
• 1 cup packed brown sugar
• 1/4 cup chopped pecans
• 2 eggs, beaten
• 1/4 cup milk
• 1 cup cornmeal
• 1/2 cup butter, softened
• 1 cup chopped nuts

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
‣ In a large bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, allspice, nutmeg, allspice and butter.
‣ In a separate bowl, beat together eggs, milk and cornmeal. Stir into flour mixture just until combined. Stir in nuts and mix until evenly blended. Spread batter into prepared pan.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
