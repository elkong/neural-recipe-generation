TITLE
Chicken In Basil Cream Sauce

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 2 cups milk
• 1 teaspoon dried basil
• 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon celery salt
• 1/4 teaspoon onion salt
• 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1/4 cup basil pesto
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 4 tablespoons butter, melted

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place chicken breasts in the skillet and brown on both sides. Remove chicken to a platter, and set aside.
‣ In a large bowl, whisk together flour, milk, basil, oregano, salt, pepper, celery salt, onion salt, garlic salt, and 1 tablespoon olive oil.
‣ In a separate bowl, mix together pesto, 2 tablespoons olive oil, and butter. Stir flour mixture into pesto mixture until just blended. Stir in chicken breasts, and cook until no longer pink in the center, about 15 minutes.
