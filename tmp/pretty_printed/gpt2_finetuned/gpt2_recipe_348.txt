TITLE
Potatoes and Peppers

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• salt and ground black pepper to taste
• 1 pound lean ground beef
• 1 large onion, chopped
• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/4 cup sour cream

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large skillet over medium heat, cook and stir potatoes and spinach until spinach is wilted. Stir in butter, salt and pepper. Cook until heated through.
‣ Remove from heat and drain on paper towels.
‣ Divide the spinach mixture into 8 portions and roll each portion into a 9 inch pie dish.
‣ Place the rolled potatoes into a large pot and fill halfway with water. Bring to a boil and cook until tender. Drain well.
‣ Combine sour cream and reserved spinach mixture in a large bowl. Sprinkle with crumbled cheese and bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes or until bubbly.
