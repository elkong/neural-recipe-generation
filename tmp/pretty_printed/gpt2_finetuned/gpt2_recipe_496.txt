TITLE
Fruit Dip II

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (12 ounce) package fresh strawberries
• 1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
• 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese frosting
• 1 cup confectioners' sugar

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Combine strawberries, whipped topping, cream cheese, and cream cheese frosting in a bowl.
