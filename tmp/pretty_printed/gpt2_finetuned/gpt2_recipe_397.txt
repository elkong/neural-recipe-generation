TITLE
Mandarin Orange, Gorgonzola and Almond Delight

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups white sugar
• 1 cup packed light brown sugar
• 1 cup sifted all-purpose flour
• 1 cup milk
• 2 cups fresh mandarin oranges, halved
• 1 tablespoon ground ginger
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/4 teaspoon salt
• 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Combine the white sugar, brown sugar, flour, and milk in a saucepan; bring to a boil over medium heat. Mix in the mandarin oranges, ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Cover, and simmer for 1 hour. Stir occasionally.
‣ Puree the mixture in a blender or food processor with the milk, mandarin oranges, ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Process until smooth, and serve warm.
