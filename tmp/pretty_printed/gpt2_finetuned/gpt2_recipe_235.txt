TITLE
Peanut Butter Frosting

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups butter, softened
• 2 cups confectioners' sugar
• 2 cups peanut butter
• 1 (18.25 ounce) package chocolate frosting

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a medium bowl, cream together the butter and confectioners' sugar until smooth. Beat in the peanut butter. Spread evenly into an 8x8 inch pan. Chill until firm.
