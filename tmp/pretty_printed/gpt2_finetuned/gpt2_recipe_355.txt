TITLE
Snackasnicker Pie

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (9 inch) pie shell
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/4 cup shortening
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 egg
• 1 tablespoon milk
• 1/2 cup chopped pecans
• 1/2 cup raisins
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
• 1/2 cup chopped almonds

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large bowl, mix flour, shortening, sugar, salt and egg. Beat egg and milk until smooth. Mix in pecans, raisins and butter. Stir in nuts and almonds. Pour into crust.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes.
