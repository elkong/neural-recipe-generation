TITLE
Chocolate Beer Cupcakes With Whiskey Filling And Irish Cream Icing

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 3/4 cup shortening
• 1 cup Irish stout beer (such as Guinness®)
• 1 1/2 cups white sugar
• 1/4 cup whiskey
• 2 eggs
• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 teaspoon Irish cream cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10-inch Bundt pan.
‣ In a large bowl, stir together the flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon and cloves. In a separate bowl, cream shortening and stout beer. Add sugar, whiskey, eggs and vanilla. Beat until smooth.
‣ In another bowl, mix together Irish cream cheese and cream cheese mixture. Pour batter into prepared pan.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
