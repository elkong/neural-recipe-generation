TITLE
Grandaddy's Sweet Potato Meringue Pie

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 1 tablespoon cornstarch
• 1/2 cup milk
• 2 tablespoons butter, melted

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Place sweet potatoes into a saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a boil and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and mash.
‣ Place sweet potatoes in a large saucepan with enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, and drain.
‣ Stir in sugar, cornstarch, and milk. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer, stirring constantly, until thickened and thickened, about 10 minutes. Stir in melted butter, and heat through. Serve hot.
