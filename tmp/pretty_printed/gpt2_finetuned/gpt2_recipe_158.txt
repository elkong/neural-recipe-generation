TITLE
Jalapeno Rosemary Alfredo

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 clove garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
• 1 (16 ounce) package angel hair pasta
• 1 (16 ounce) jar marinated artichoke hearts, drained and quartered
• 1 (8 ounce) jar chopped fresh rosemary
• 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in the garlic, and cook until the garlic has softened and turned golden brown, about 5 minutes. Stir in the red pepper flakes, and cook for 5 more minutes.
‣ Pour the artichoke hearts and rosemary into the skillet, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until the artichoke hearts have softened and are starting to show their tender spots, about 20 minutes.
‣ Pour the sauce over the pasta to serve. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese to serve.
