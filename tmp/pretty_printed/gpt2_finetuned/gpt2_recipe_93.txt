TITLE
Portabella Nirvana

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (16 ounce) packages tri-colored cake mix
• 1 cup butter
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 2 cups milk
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 3/4 cup white sugar
• 1 cup chopped pecans
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9x5 inch loaf pans.
‣ Mix the cake mix, butter, flour, milk, vanilla, sugar, pecans, cinnamon, nutmeg, ground cloves, ginger, and cloves together in a large bowl.
‣ Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pans.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 50 to 60 minutes. Allow to cool in the pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
