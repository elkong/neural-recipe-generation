TITLE
Carrots in Dill Butter

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups butter, softened
• 4 cups chopped carrots
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 cup milk
• 1 cup shredded carrots
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup shredded carrots
• 2 tablespoons margarine
• 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar
• 1 teaspoon white sugar
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1 tablespoon butter

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the softened butter, carrots, celery, and onion. In a separate bowl, mix together 1/2 cup flour, 1 cup celery, 3 tablespoons flour, salt, milk, and 1 cup carrots. In a third bowl, mix together 1/2 cup carrots and 1/2 cup celery. Add the remaining flour, 1/2 cup carrots, 1/2 cup celery, 1/2 cup carrots, and 1 tablespoon margarine; mix until smooth.
‣ Pour the batter into an 8x8-inch baking dish. Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
