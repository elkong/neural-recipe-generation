TITLE
Hot Asiago and Spinach Dip

INGREDIENTS
• 2 (10 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough
• 1 cup creamy salad dressing
• 1/2 cup olive oil
• 2 cups fresh spinach, washed and dried
• 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• 1 tablespoon butter
• 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
• salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ Place a biscuit dough onto a work surface, and press firmly to seal. Place in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ In a small bowl, combine salad dressing, olive oil, spinach, Cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, butter, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
