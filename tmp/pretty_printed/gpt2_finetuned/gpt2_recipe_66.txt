TITLE
Celery Stuffing

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
• 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1/4 cup chopped onion
• 1/4 cup chopped celery
• 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1 cup bread crumbs
• 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
• 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni, and cook until tender, about 8 minutes; drain.
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a large skillet over medium heat, combine the spinach, butter, onion, celery, and cheese. Cook and stir until the mixture is heated through, then sprinkle with bread crumbs and Parmesan.
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the pasta, bread crumbs, and Parmesan cheese. Spoon into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Top with macaroni mixture, and top with remaining bread crumb mixture.
‣ Bake 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.
