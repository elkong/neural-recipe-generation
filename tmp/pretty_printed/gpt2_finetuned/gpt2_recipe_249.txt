TITLE
Quinoa Cookies III

INGREDIENTS
• 2 1/4 cups quinoa
• 1 cup boiling water
• 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
• 1 cup shredded carrots
• 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
• 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
• 1/2 cup butter, melted
• 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Bring quinoa and boiling water to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until quinoa is tender, about 20 minutes. Drain, cool, and chop.
‣ In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter and stir in the vanilla. Stir in the melted quinoa and quinoa mixture. Stir until well blended.
‣ Stir in the melted chocolate chips, melted butter, vanilla, salt, and cinnamon. Drop by heaping teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper. Let sit until chocolate has melted, about 5 minutes.
