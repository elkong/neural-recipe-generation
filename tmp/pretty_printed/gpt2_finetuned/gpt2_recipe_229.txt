TITLE
Sweet As Kisses Pie

INGREDIENTS
• 1 (9 inch) pie shell
• 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
• 1/4 cup shortening
• 1/4 cup white sugar
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
• 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
• 1/4 cup butter, melted
• 1/2 cup white sugar
• 2 eggs
• 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
• 1 teaspoon milk
• 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a medium bowl, combine flour, shortening, white sugar, salt, cinnamon and brown sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in melted butter. Mix well, and add eggs, vanilla and milk. Beat until smooth.
‣ Pour filling into pie shell. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 40 minutes. Let cool for 10 minutes before cutting into bars.
