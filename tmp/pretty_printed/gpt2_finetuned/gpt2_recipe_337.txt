TITLE
Spicy Peach Coleslaw

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups mayonnaise
• 2 cups sour cream
• 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
• 1 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese
• 1/2 cup chopped fresh pecans

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Mix mayonnaise, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, and Colby-Monterey Jack cheese in a bowl until smooth. Add pecans and mix thoroughly.
