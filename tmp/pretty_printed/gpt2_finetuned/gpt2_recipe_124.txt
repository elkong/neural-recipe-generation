TITLE
Kai Kang Dang (Chicken Curry with Coconut Milk)

INGREDIENTS
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 1 large onion, chopped
• 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
• 1 green bell pepper, chopped
• 1 red bell pepper, chopped
• 3 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup chicken broth
• 1 1/2 teaspoons soy sauce
• 1 teaspoon ground cumin
• 1 teaspoon lemon juice
• 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
• 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
• 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and green bell pepper and saute until onion is tender.
‣ In a blender, combine the onion mixture with the chicken broth, chicken broth, soy sauce, cumin, lemon juice, ginger, cilantro, and sesame seeds; blend until smooth. Cover and refrigerate for 4 hours before serving.
