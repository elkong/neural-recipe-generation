TITLE
Easy Cheesy Chicken and Rice

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups uncooked regular long-grain white rice
• 1/2 cup water
• 1/4 cup vegetable oil
• 1/4 cup butter
• 3 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
• 1 cup uncooked instant rice
• 1/2 cup heavy cream
• 1 cup chopped cooked chicken
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• salt and pepper to taste
• 1 (4.5 ounce) can mushrooms, drained
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ In a saucepan, bring water to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 20 minutes, or until rice is tender.
‣ In a small skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add garlic and saute for 1 minute, or until garlic is lightly browned. Stir in chicken broth, rice and cream. Heat through, but do not boil.
‣ Stir in cooked rice, chicken, onion, salt and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes, or until rice is heated through. Stir in mushrooms and parsley before serving.
