TITLE
Beef and Biscuit Cake III

INGREDIENTS
• 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/2 cup butter
• 1 cup packed brown sugar
• 1 egg
• 1/2 cup buttermilk
• 2 1/4 cups buttermilk
• 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
• 1 teaspoon baking powder
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
• 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 8 inch round pans.
‣ In a large bowl, combine 1 1/2 cup flour, salt, ground black pepper, and baking soda. Make a well in the center, and pour in butter, brown sugar, egg, buttermilk, buttermilk, and 2 1/4 cups flour. Beat until smooth. Stir in onion, green pepper, and parsley. Pour batter into prepared pans.
‣ Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
