TITLE
Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

INGREDIENTS
• 2 portobello mushroom caps
• 2 teaspoons olive oil
• 1 teaspoon minced garlic
• 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
• 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
• salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
‣ Arrange the mushrooms on a baking sheet. Drizzle olive oil over the mushrooms and sprinkle with garlic. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the mushrooms. Roast the mushrooms for 10 minutes in the preheated oven.
‣ Sprinkle lemon juice over the mushrooms and continue cooking 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
