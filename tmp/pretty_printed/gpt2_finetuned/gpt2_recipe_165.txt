TITLE
Zucchini Bars

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups all-purpose flour
• 2 teaspoons baking powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/2 cup butter
• 2 cups white sugar
• 2 eggs
• 1 cup buttermilk
• 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
• 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
• 1 1/2 cups chopped zucchini

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
‣ Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, ground cloves, nutmeg, allspice, ginger, and cloves. Set aside.
‣ In a large bowl, cream together butter, sugar, eggs, buttermilk, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and zucchini. Stir flour mixture into the zucchini mixture. Pour batter into prepared pan.
‣ Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.
