TITLE
Bok Choy Salad

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups baby carrots
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped onion
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 1/2 cup chopped celery
• 3 tablespoons soy sauce
• 3 tablespoons light brown sugar
• 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
• 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
• 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
• 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Wash and dry the baby carrots and celery. Cut carrots into small chunks, and add to vegetables.
‣ In a large skillet over medium heat, combine the carrots, celery, onion, celery, soy sauce, brown sugar, ground ginger, cinnamon, cloves, allspice, nutmeg, cayenne pepper, olive oil, parsley and salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, and simmer for 20 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
