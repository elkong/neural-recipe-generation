TITLE
The Best Ever Chicken Nuggets

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
• 2 teaspoons seasoned salt
• 1 teaspoon garlic powder
• 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
• 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
• 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
• 1 cup chopped celery
• 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
• 1/4 cup butter
• 1/2 cup milk
• 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
• 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

INSTRUCTIONS
‣ Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
‣ In a medium bowl, mix together the seasoned bread crumbs, seasoned salt, garlic powder, onion powder, pepper, oregano, celery, condensed soup and butter or margarine. Mix in the milk, Worcestershire sauce and Cheddar cheese. Divide evenly between 9x13 inch baking dish.
‣ Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 20 minutes, or until bubbly and lightly browned.
